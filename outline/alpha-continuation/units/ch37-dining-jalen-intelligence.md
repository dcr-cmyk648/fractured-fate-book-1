# Detailed Alpha Unit Outline: Chapter 37

## Status

- Unit: proposed Chapter 37
- Working title: Dining Hall, Jalen's Return, and First Intelligence
- Current status: author-review packet
- Source fragment: `revision/current-draft-map/d1-ch-37-preposed.md`
- Prose drafting: not authorized

## Purpose

Chapter 37 should complete the current Maya POV fragment by turning training/intimacy pressure into a social warmth scene, then interrupting that warmth with Jalen's near-death arrival and the first actionable intelligence about Archeon, the Harbingers, the Heart, and Kelyra.

The chapter's job is not to solve the entire Archeon combat model. Its job is to move Maya from overwork and controlled training into a brief experience of being treated as a person, then snap her back into crisis leadership.

## Current Handoff

The existing fragment already establishes:

- several days have passed after Davian's return/custody;
- Maya is training Davian and Selwin while overworking herself on anti-Archeon schemas and Registry repair;
- Davian is increasingly restless about Archeon and Kelyra;
- Maya worries about Selwin's stalled Shroud reflex;
- Davian is learning to integrate Ink, Shroud, magic, and body instead of treating them as separate tools;
- Maya and Davian have renewed attraction through training;
- Davian invites Maya to eat together;
- Maya enters the crowded worker dining hall and freezes under Registry/Skein anxiety;
- Davian notices and asks if she is okay.

## Working Chapter Shape

### Scene 1 - Dining Hall Recovery

- POV: Maya.
- Start from Davian noticing Maya's distress.
- Davian gives her a simple way back into the moment rather than making a public rescue.
- Maya tries to present the meal as training/logistics, not wanting companionship.
- Erynn can be nearby enough to silently judge Maya's "this is training" performance.
- Tanelle should be present if useful; she would not willingly miss this.
- Kolvar/Jessa/other zänwrights can add worker-hall texture, but the scene should not become ensemble sprawl.

Purpose:

- Pay off Davian's invitation.
- Show Maya wanting ordinary warmth while feeling like an event in a room.
- Let Davian be socially natural somewhere Maya is not.

### Scene 2 - Table Dynamics and Missteps

- Maya asks for whatever Davian is eating, or otherwise tries to participate without knowing the rules.
- Davian understands crowd dynamics, how to deflect questions, how not to make Maya's presence too ceremonial.
- Tanelle or another zänwright tries to figure out whether this is training, politics, romance, or all of it.
- A tray/clatter/noise beat can trigger Davian's protective reflex.
- Maya should read the reflex as both endearing and concerning: if he reacts to a harmless noise this way, what will he do when Archeon or Caius appears?
- Maya notices Davian's Ink/tattoos looking faded, rough, or altered after recent strain.
- If she teases him about it, it should be privileged, curious, and a little intimate.
- Maya may accidentally mention Registry awareness of his movement/trips, landing as surveillance to Davian.

Purpose:

- Make the romance tactile and behavioral, not just internal.
- Put the class/status gulf in the same scene as attraction.
- Preserve the small wound: Maya means "I notice you"; Davian hears "the system watches you."

### Scene 3 - Selwin as Pressure and Mirror

- Selwin may arrive restless and trying to seem grown.
- Maya stiffens into protective-sister mode.
- Selwin resents being managed.
- Davian treats him as someone with a practical problem rather than a fragile object.
- Selwin can mention Shroud/body-awareness language in a simple way, but this should not become a full lesson unless the chapter has room.
- Davian's seriousness with Selwin lets Maya see that Davian can reach people she over-manages.

Purpose:

- Keep Selwin's Shroud thread active.
- Show Davian's empathy under the defensive mask.
- Reinforce Maya's difficulty distinguishing protection from control.

### Scene 4 - First Archeon Pivot, But Not the Full Workshop

- The conversation can briefly touch Archeon because everyone is carrying that pressure.
- Maya may observe that Archeon fighters behave like drilled answers, not adaptive magi.
- Davian can contribute from fighter instinct: drilled forms, one answer repeated until it becomes automatic.
- Harvick can connect this to the idea that getting hit carefully is often more educational than talking.
- Do not run the full captured-gauntlet/focus-myth/Elric-experiment workshop here unless the chapter unexpectedly has room.

Purpose:

- Preserve the source-note function that Archeon is predictable and brittle.
- Seed later detailed research/training without stealing the Jalen interruption's weight.

### Scene 5 - Jalen's Arrival Interrupts

- Jalen arrives or collapses at/near Rezin in essentially dying condition.
- The interruption should cut off the fragile warmth before it resolves.
- He should be delirious, badly injured, and clearly not combat-capable.
- His survival can be attributed later to criminal-underworld route knowledge, stubbornness, and skills, but he should not look fine.
- Davian's reaction should be intense and hard to control.
- Maya has to shift immediately from person-at-table to Rezin decision-maker.

Purpose:

- Move from emotional/social pressure to plot pressure.
- Make Jalen's return feel like the story intruding on the moment Maya wanted.
- Give Davian a new family-pressure spike before Chapter 38.

### Scene 6 - Niall Resists, Maya Argues

- Niall is not enthusiastic about spending expensive/limited healing effort on Jalen.
- Maya argues that they need Jalen alive for information.
- This should not become a long ethical debate; it should show Niall's cold utility logic and Maya's growing leadership.
- Maya can feel the unease of needing to justify saving a person by usefulness.
- Niall may permit healing because the intelligence is necessary, not because he values Jalen.

Purpose:

- Show Maya pushing back in practical command terms.
- Keep Niall's fear/rigidity visible.
- Begin the Jalen/Aldira path without making Niall suddenly generous.

### Scene 7 - Gold Strike-Line Healing

- Use the newer healing model: painful gold strike-lines etched/seared onto the skin and activated.
- The healer is expensive, likely Low Clade/guild-linked, and the reagents/maintenance matter.
- Jalen's condition should justify why he cannot join the final assault.
- Davian can struggle with watching this without being able to act.
- Maya can compare this to all the systems she is trying to hold together: bodies, zän, Registry, politics, trust.

Purpose:

- Reintroduce the current healing model in a plot-relevant way.
- Establish Jalen's physical removal from combat without removing him from story importance.

### Scene 8 - First Intelligence Extraction

Jalen should provide enough to change the strategic state, but not everything:

- Archeon left him for dead during Kelyra's capture.
- Kelyra is in Archeon's hands or effectively under Archeon control.
- The Harbingers/leader betrayed the expected bargain.
- The Heart went to a masked or hidden receiving figure, or at least was passed onward through the Harbinger-Archeon-Drakhal chain.
- Jalen has knowledge of routes, signs, contacts, and Masked Clade/criminal networks Rezin would not normally access.
- Jalen does not need to know Kelyra's exact holding location.
- Davian's knowledge from Karra's message remains important for locating Kelyra later.

Purpose:

- Give Rezin actionable direction without solving the raid.
- Move Jalen into logistics/intelligence support.
- Set up Aldira's excitement over access to Masked Clade networks next chapter/day.

### Scene 9 - Aldira Takes Over

- Aldira arrives or asserts control once Jalen has given enough.
- She recognizes the intelligence value and prevents Maya/Davian/Niall from draining Jalen immediately.
- She can force rest, triage, and structured questioning.
- Davian may hate being pushed out, which feeds Chapter 38.
- Maya may recognize that Aldira is doing exactly what a competent spymaster/support figure should do: protecting the source and the information flow.

Purpose:

- Prevent the scene from becoming an unlimited exposition dump.
- Give Aldira active competence.
- End the chapter with pressure, not resolution.

## Ending Options

### Preferred Ending

End with Aldira taking command of Jalen's room/intelligence handling and Davian being forced away from his father before he has answers. Maya sees that the dining-hall warmth is gone, replaced by the next decision she is not ready to make.

This hands cleanly to Chapter 38 Davian POV: he cannot sleep because Maya, Jalen, Kelyra, Archeon, and his own wanting are all pressing on him.

### If Chapter 37 Gets Too Long

If the dining-hall material plus Jalen's arrival is already too large, end Chapter 37 on Jalen's collapse/arrival and move the healing/intelligence material into the start of Chapter 38 from Davian's POV.

This is approved as a flexible boundary. Chapter 38 has room, and Davian's perspective can carry the emotional experience of watching Jalen healed and being forced to wait.

## What to Move or Defer

- Elric focus experiment recap.
- Detailed Shroud-versus-hereditary-magic theory.
- Extended cognition/splitting-focus mythbust.
- Full Naeroth breathing/forms research.

These can be redistributed into Chapter 38, Chapter 39 training material, or later detailed outline packets if still useful.

## Chapter Goals

### Plot Goals

- Confirm that Jalen survived Kelyra's capture but is gravely injured.
- Establish that Archeon/Harbinger collaboration is now actionable.
- Move Jalen into intelligence/logistics support.
- Give Rezin a reason to use Jalen/Aldira networks before the Drakhal proposal and assault.

### Character Goals

- Maya: briefly chooses warmth, then is forced back into leadership; argues for Jalen's life from necessity while feeling the moral discomfort.
- Davian: experiences warmth with Maya, then gets family trauma and Archeon urgency sharpened.
- Jalen: returns as a damaged but useful support figure, not a combat participant.
- Niall: shows cold utility and fear-driven rigidity without becoming a simple villain.
- Aldira: acts as practical intelligence manager and protects the information source.
- Selwin: remains a mirror for protection/control if used.

### Relationship Goals

- Maya/Davian: warmer, more intimate, still wounded by surveillance/status/class reality.
- Maya/Niall: Maya pushes against his coldness in a practical way.
- Davian/Jalen: Jalen's condition intensifies Davian's urgency and guilt.
- Aldira/Jalen: begins the intelligence partnership.

### Magic / Worldbuilding Goals

- Reaffirm full-body Shroud/body integration through the existing opening fragment without overexplaining.
- Use current gold strike-line healing model.
- Keep Registry surveillance/power limits present through Maya's anxiety and the dining-hall misstep.
- Seed Archeon predictability as future combat doctrine.

### Information-Flow Goals

Reader learns:

- Jalen is alive but badly injured.
- Archeon is a confirmed actionable target.
- Harbinger/Archeon/Heart information is now reaching Rezin.
- Jalen can open networks Rezin lacks.

Reader should not yet learn:

- the full Archeon trap;
- the exact Kelyra rescue route;
- full Elric endgame;
- exact resurrection mechanics;
- full gauntlet/Shroud theory.

### Setup / Payoff Goals

- Pay off Davian's dinner invitation.
- Pay off Jalen being left for dead and routed into support.
- Set up Aldira/Jalen intelligence work.
- Set up Davian's sleepless Chapter 38 urgency.
- Set up Maya later resurrecting Davian from unresolved longing and fear, not settled romance.

## Consistency Checks

- Jalen must not become a combat participant in the final assault.
- Jalen does not need exact knowledge of where Kelyra is being held.
- Davian's Kelyra-location pressure can later come from Karra's message and/or leaked Archeon route.
- Shrouds should remain full-body; do not introduce local-Shroud mechanics.
- Use `zän`, not unaccented `zan`, in new planning/prose references.
- Comments/feedback and older outline notes remain source material, not automatic canon.
