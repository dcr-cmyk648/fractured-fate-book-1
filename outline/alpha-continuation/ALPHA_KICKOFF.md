## Purpose

The immediate goal is to finish the current alpha draft from the present written-prose cutoff through the accepted Book 1 ending. This is not the beta-draft re-outline and not a full front-to-back rewrite pass.

This packet defines the current cutoff, the accepted constraints that govern the remaining alpha, a high-level remaining sequence list, and the decisions needed before outlining the next alpha unit. It does not draft prose, assign final chapter numbers, or create full Chapter Goal Cards.

## What is already accepted

- Davian's resurrection by Maya is a canon end-book event. The exact resurrection mechanics, cost, Heart/Skein interaction, and Maya's later interpretation remain deferred.
- Elric is the hidden primary antagonist shaping the endgame through Harbinger/Syndicate control, Ink Pacts, fate-pressure, the Heart, Davian, Niall, Maya, Serathis, Kelyra, and the "Cord of Six Strands" structure.
- Maya's Fateweaving/Skein capacity is developing under Elric's manipulation. The endgame resurrection damages or interacts with the Skein in a way that matters, but the low-level metaphysics are not locked.
- The Heart has been removed from Rezin containment. Its theft destabilizes Niall's power base and emboldens Drakhal/Archeon pressure, even though the public political layer may obscure the exact facts.
- Niall's power remains terrifying but diminished. His arc requires moving from solitary deterrent/killer toward accepting help from Maya and Davian.
- Maya and Davian's romance/trust arc is central. Their current unresolved emotional state should keep attraction, anger, betrayal, confusion, and care in tension rather than resolving too neatly before the endgame.
- Davian's final alpha role is not to become the lone decisive battlefield solution. He matters because he chooses protection, reduces pressure on Maya/Niall, blocks Vorren at the crucial moment, and ruins Antithesis by using it defensively.
- Antithesis remains a dagger until it is ruined in the final conflict. Paradox is later made from ruined Antithesis by Zirene, designed/named by Maya, and given to Davian by Niall near the end.
- Kelyra is rescued from Archeon pressure in the endgame. Her Shroud manifestation is accepted as a major turn, and she helps Davian fight Serathis or the remaining Archeon heir/magus threat.
- Jalen is left for dead during Kelyra's capture, reaches Rezin basically dead, receives painful gold strike-line healing, and becomes a support/logistics/intelligence bridge rather than a physical assault participant.
- Serathis holds back valuable Archeon force for the interior trap, uses Archeon heirs/magi as pressure, fights Davian/Kelyra, and reveals her broader desire to burn the Clade system down as she dies.
- Vorren is the Heart-empowered final physical antagonist below the Archeon throne-room trap. He serves Drakhal deniability and his own appetite/ambition, and he is defeated only by combined pressure from Maya, Niall, and Davian.
- Drakhal likely keeps Evana and Caius out of the final assault to preserve plausible deniability. Caius remains available for later-book antagonism.
- The final Archeon approach is aboveground through neglected/slum boundary conditions, not a Harbinger tunnel approach.
