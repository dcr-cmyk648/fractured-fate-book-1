## Integrity Checks

### Convergence Ownership

`magic-convergence-heat-transfer` now owns the accepted heat-transfer model:

- rare Naerothi-linked ability associated with Alara and Maya
- manipulates heat flow rather than creating heat from nothing
- cold effects come from heat removal
- strengthened by post-original-sin reality distortion
- described primarily through schemas, mental circuits, carvings, surfaces, and conduits
- limited by distance, complexity, containment, and high-energy concentration

Direct dependent files now link to this owner record.

- Shroud and some high-level mental abilities from Niall

No conflict found.

- Convergence gives Maya a clearer combat and character signature: she gathers pressure, tries to hold it perfectly, and releases it.

- Keeping Convergence visually adjacent to, but distinct from, Fateweaving preserves later reveal space around Maya's end-book resurrection and Alara's higher-plane state.
