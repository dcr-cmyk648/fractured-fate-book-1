## Confirmed Consistency

- The Heart is the accepted in-world object name. `Tzorrax Heart` remains old manuscript terminology, `Seraph Heart` is not accepted in-world terminology, and `Tzorrax Sphere` is not a separate durable object.
- The Treasury sphere contains the Heart and containment armature. Treasury weightlessness is produced by the Heart interacting with the armature and ends when Davian shatters the sphere and removes the Heart.
- The Registry, Treasury, and major Rezin zä infrastructure remain consistent with the accepted model that they were overbuilt around Heart-level input and shrink or fail after the Heart is stolen.
- Antithesis is Davian's dagger throughout Book 1, not an independent magic system and not a transforming sword. It is an Archeon attempted copy of older Zirene designs and functions as a schema sink/anchor for kinesis and backlash.
- Paradox is accepted future-plan material: Maya names/designs it, Zirene makes it from ruined Antithesis, Niall gives it to Davian, it is a normal sword, and it is not aura-bound.
- Davian's mask is a matte black Concord/Masked Clade mask with zan markings: equality/equilibrium at the start, later Nizan zero/negation/void from Kelyra. The older five-scratch design is superseded unless later revision reuses it.
- Alara's journal is Alara's specific auratic codex, not Niall's codex. Elric developed auratic codex technology from fateweaving work and provided journals to Alara, Niall, Harvick, and himself under a benign memory-preservation pretext.
- Alara does not guide Maya through the journal. Any perception of guidance is part of Elric's trap.
