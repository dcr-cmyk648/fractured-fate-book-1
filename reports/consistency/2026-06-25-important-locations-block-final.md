## Confirmed Consistency

- The shared Spire / Central Spire is the central open-air amphitheater at the center of the Six Cities, not an intact tower and not a Rezin or Archeon citadel tower.
- All six High Clade citadels have their own central towers. From the central Spire/amphitheater, the six citadel towers define a hexagon-like geometry around the Six Cities.
- The Rezin citadel's central tower/spire remains an architectural feature distinct from the shared Spire / central amphitheater.
- The Archeon citadel is one of the six High Clade citadels and has its own central tower, throne room, lower complex, internal arena, training halls, child compound, and breeding-pit concept.
- Many citadels may have sprawling forgotten or recently reclaimed subterranean areas; this fits the Rezin old tunnels, the Archeon lower complex, and Harbinger tunnel material without merging those locations.
- The final Archeon attack currently approaches aboveground through slums / neglected city-wedge-boundary areas, then moves into the citadel and central tower.
- The Sorevin manor is the prologue Low Clade manor where Davian fights the Sorevin Head Magus.
- Sorevin is a Rezin proxy-administrator Low Clade and contractor-vetting house; Redbridge remains candidate-only map material, not accepted Sorevin territory.
- The Concord manor remains a separate formerly abandoned Low Clade manor and is not Sorevin manor.
- The Registry remains below ground, the Treasury remains high up, and no elevators or similar magical vertical transport are accepted.
