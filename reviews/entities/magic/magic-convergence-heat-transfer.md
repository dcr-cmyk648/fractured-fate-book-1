# Convergence Heat Transfer Review Packet

## Current Author Decisions

Approved on 2026-06-27:

- Convergence is a rare Naerothi-linked heat-transfer ability associated with Alara and Maya.
- It manipulates heat flow rather than creating heat from nothing.
- It is affected by the same reality distortion that affects Clade hereditary magic, allowing it to be much more powerful than it would have been pre-original sin.
- Maya receives her Shroud from Niall, along with some mental abilities that let her be so powerful and engage reality directly.
- Maya receives her Naerothi Convergence inheritance through Alara.
- Maya's working model is accepted at high level: heat/cold overlays, schemas, mental circuits, carvings, surfaces, conduits, and controlled gathering/compression/containment/redirection/release of heat.
- On-page description should favor carving-based language and surfaces.
- Thread/ribbon/channel imagery can remain visually useful without implying Convergence is identical to Fateweaving.
- Detailed physics language, body-as-focus techniques, heat/charge plasma synergy, and Convergence/Fateweaving/Selwin relationships remain candidate/deferred.

## Review Depth

Standard.

Convergence matters for Maya's combat, Alara's Naerothi ability package, Presentation imagery, close-range training, and the endgame Fateweaving/Skein overlap. It does not need full thermodynamics beyond what keeps scenes coherent.

## Why This Entity Matters

Convergence gives Maya a distinctive magic profile separate from ordinary human hereditary magic, while still linking her to Alara and Naerothi lineage. It also gives future chapter work constraints for what Maya can do with fire, cold, heat gathering, movement, Shroud backlash, and close-quarters counters.

## Source-Backed Information

### Accepted Context

- Alara's accepted high-level ability package includes Shroud, fateweaving, and Convergence heat transfer.
- Naeroth heritage can rarely grant fateweaving and Convergence heat transfer despite the wider High Clade belief that only humans have hereditary magic and Shrouds.
- Maya has heat and flame manipulation in her character file, plus emerging Fateweaving/Skein perception.
- Exact Fateweaving / Convergence relationship remains unresolved.
- Exact relationship among Alara's abilities, Maya's abilities, and Selwin's Dissolution remains unresolved.

### Manuscript and Planning Evidence

Current and planning material repeatedly frames Maya's magic as heat movement rather than simple fire generation:

- Maya can sense heat/cold differences as bright or dark overlays.
- She explains that cold is the absence of heat.
- She reverses heat's natural tendency to spread, pulling heat toward heat.
- She must gather, compress, constrain, and release heat in controlled structures.
- Distance and layered visualization make the work harder.
- Presentation imagery uses heat from below/around her, ice spreading from her feet, and a fire/ice display pushed beyond ordinary Presentation limits.
- Later movement and combat notes use heat transfer, downward heat discharge, ice/friction reduction, and Shroud reinforcement.

### Technical Candidate Notes

Planning notes propose a more formal model:

- Convergence does not generate heat.
- It locally inverts heat transfer / thermal diffusion by carving channels where heat flows "uphill" toward a chosen locus.
- Containment requires a boundary that prevents ordinary heat dispersal.
- Cognitive load scales with distance and/or surface area.
- High temperatures become unstable due to nonlinear losses, radiative emission, ionization, or containment limits.
- Extreme heat is better as short-lived hold-and-discharge behavior than indefinite storage.

## Current Narrative Functions

- Combat function: Maya gathers and redirects heat, creates fire/cold effects, and can use heat transfer for movement/counters.
- Identity function: Convergence marks Maya as Naerothi-linked and Alara-linked rather than simply another human High Clade magus.
- Training function: Convergence can cross-pollinate with Naeroth body-focus / stance work and Davian's martial training.
- Limitation function: Maya does not create unlimited heat; she needs nearby heat, distance matters, containment is cognitively demanding, and extreme concentrations destabilize.
- Thematic function: Convergence mirrors Maya's tendency to draw pressure into herself and try to control it perfectly before discharge.

## Proposed Canon Core

If approved, the accepted record would store:

- Convergence heat transfer is a rare Naerothi-linked ability associated with Alara and Maya.
- Convergence manipulates heat flow rather than creating heat from nothing.
- Maya experiences heat/cold as perceptible overlays and can carve/visualize channels that gather, compress, constrain, redirect, and release heat.
- Cold effects usually result from heat being pulled out of an area or object.
- Distance, complexity, containment surface, and high-energy concentration increase cognitive/technical load.
- Extreme heat concentrations are unstable and tend toward brief hold-and-discharge use rather than indefinite storage.
- Maya's use of Convergence can look visually like ribbons/threads/strands of heat, which can echo but not automatically equal Fateweaving thread perception.
- Alara had Convergence heat transfer at high level, but exact Alara usage remains mostly deferred.
