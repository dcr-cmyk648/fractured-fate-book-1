# Tanelle Review Packet

## Review Depth

Standard.

Tanelle is not a major plot driver, but she is a recurring zánwright contractor with meaningful scene function: she anchors Davian in the worker group, delivers technical exposition through character, provides class contrast around High Clade worship/protection, and helps make Davian's temporary belonging in the Rezin citadel emotionally credible.

## Why This Entity Matters

Tanelle makes the Rezin citadel feel inhabited by ordinary workers rather than only High Clades, guards, and enemies.

For Davian, she is irritating, useful, disarming, and unexpectedly humanizing. Her friendliness and competence make it harder for him to maintain a clean enemy-category for everyone under Rezin protection. Her zánwright skill also gives him plausible access to technical understanding he could not have gained alone.

## Current Narrative Functions

- Worker-space anchor: helps make the contractor group feel socially real.
- Technical exposition through character: explains zán work because she loves it.
- Davian cover pressure: pulls Davian into conversations and makes him maintain his Arrek story.
- Davian humanization: helps create a place where Davian briefly feels warmth, teasing, and belonging.
- Class contrast: admires High Clades in a way that frustrates Davian and shows how Rezin protection can look from inside the protected sphere.
- Treasury clue source: notices the sphere lattice's inward/containment behavior without understanding the Heart.
- Social observer: can witness or misread Maya/Davian tension in low-stakes scenes.

## Relationships and Conflicts That Create Story

### Davian / Arrek

Tanelle treats Davian as a coworker and friend. She repeatedly disrupts his isolation, forces him into social contact, helps him learn zánwright work, and contributes to his sense that some people inside the Rezin system are ordinary, warm, and worth caring about.

### Maya

Tanelle is fascinated by Maya as a High Clade magus and Rezin heir. Her questions can be socially awkward but useful because they expose Maya to ordinary worker curiosity rather than formal court deference.

### Kolvar, Jessa, and Marek

Tanelle is part of the contractor group with Kolvar and Jessa. Their teasing dynamic gives Davian an ordinary work-social context. Marek recognizes Tanelle's productivity, while also noting the cost of her constant talking.

## Proposed Canon Core

- Tanelle is a young zánwright contractor assigned to the Rezin citadel ward/Treasury restoration project.
- She is enthusiastic, talkative, socially forward, and openly admiring of High Clade power, especially Maya.
- She is not merely comic relief: she is unusually skilled at practical zánwright work and pattern recognition.
- She helps Davian maintain and complicate his Arrek cover by pulling him into the worker group with Kolvar and Jessa.
- Her chatter and competence help Davian learn enough zánwright practice to function on the job.
- She notices the Treasury sphere lattice behaves more like containment than outward weightlessness generation, but she does not understand the Heart or the full system.
- She loves doing the work itself and does not want supervisor status because supervision would remove her from hands-on etching.

## Proposed Developmental Profile

Tanelle should be bright, exhausting, and genuinely good at her craft.

Her social function works best when she is both funny and competent. She talks too much, asks awkward questions, and starstruckly admires High Clades, but she also notices patterns other workers ignore. Davian can dismiss her politically while still needing her technically and emotionally.

She is useful because her warmth is not strategic. She makes Davian feel included almost by accident.
