# Erynn Review Packet

## Review Depth

Standard.

Erynn is not a principal plot engine, but she is recurring, close to Maya, present in several key scenes, and useful for emotional grounding, class perspective, social observation, and later Maya/Davian relationship texture.

## Why This Entity Matters

Erynn is Maya's most personal, practical support figure inside the Rezin household.

Where Aldira supports Maya through analysis and dry strategic redirection, Erynn supports Maya through warmth, teasing, ordinary-person perspective, daily care, and social perception.

She helps keep Maya human on the page. She also lets the book show the gap between High Clade power and ordinary people without turning every class observation into a speech.

## Current Narrative Functions

- Personal support: helps Maya dress, prepare, recover, sleep, and keep moving through ordinary tasks.
- Emotional grounding: notices when Maya is dissociated, exhausted, guilty, or overworking.
- Social reader: sees interpersonal tension, romantic cues, guard dynamics, and when Maya is pretending not to care.
- Class perspective: gives Maya a grounded view from outside High Clade assumptions.
- Humanizing function: lets Maya be "just Maya" in private, not only Rezin heir, weapon, or political symbol.
- Light comic function: teasing, dry lines, and practical irreverence.
- Future relationship-observer function: can notice Maya/Davian attraction and awkwardness before they can name it.

## Relationships That Create Story

### Maya

Erynn is one of Maya's closest personal supports. She knows Maya's daily habits, presentation mask, workaholism, anxiety, and need for warmth.

Erynn can challenge Maya in ways that are intimate rather than strategic. She is allowed to tease Maya, call out avoidance, and make simple practical observations that cut through Maya's High Clade abstractions.

### Niall

Erynn is much lower in the hierarchy than Niall, but she will still defend Maya when Maya is being pushed too hard. Her interruption after the assassination attempt matters because she risks speaking up across a massive power gap.

### Aldira

Erynn and Aldira serve complementary Maya-support functions. Erynn is personal/practical; Aldira is strategic/dry.

### Davian

Erynn is useful as an observer of Maya's attraction to Davian, Maya's attempts to rationalize that attraction, and the social awkwardness around Davian entering more intimate Rezin spaces.

## Proposed Developmental Profile

Erynn's useful story shape is "warm practical wisdom from below the High Clade line."

She is not a strategist like Aldira, a trainer like Harvick, or a ruler like Niall. Her value is that she sees the human room Maya is standing in: whether Maya needs sleep, whether she is pretending not to be upset, whether Davian's attention matters, whether High Clade logic is missing what ordinary people would notice.

She should be able to be funny without becoming only comic relief. Her teasing often protects intimacy: it lets Maya hear truths that would be too vulnerable if stated directly.
