# Entity Review: Selwin Rezin

## Proposed identity

- Selwin Rezin is Niall Rezin's young son, Maya Rezin's younger brother, Alara's child, and an unPresented High Clade child at a dangerous pre-Shroud/manifestation stage.
- He functions as a living pressure point for the Rezin family's grief, perfectionism, inheritance, and fear of uncontrolled magic.
- He is also an innocence/mirror figure for Davian: Davian initially wants to see him as a future High Clade monster, but Selwin's openness and desire to protect people cut through that abstraction.

## Manuscript-established information

- Selwin enters after Maya and Niall's tense discussion, excited about Maya's Presentation and confident that she is "the best." Niall's tone and expression soften around him. Provenance: `imports/normalized/melissa-copy.md:2228-2310`, Chapter 8/early training, paragraphs 971-980.
- Maya works with Selwin on basic magical principles, breathing, centering, and focus. Selwin finds centering boring and wants to make a Shroud like Maya's. Provenance: `imports/normalized/melissa-copy.md:2228-2310`, paragraphs 981-990.
- Selwin worries that his magic may not be strong enough or that he may not be able to make a good Shroud. Maya tells him his magic will come when it is ready and that they will figure it out together. Provenance: `imports/normalized/melissa-copy.md:2228-2310`, paragraphs 991-993.
- Maya identifies Selwin as being at the most dangerous age for a High Clade child: his magic type and manifestation pattern are unknown, and uncontrolled manifestation can cause backlash, injury, collateral damage, or death. Provenance: `imports/normalized/melissa-copy.md:2228-2310`, paragraphs 994-995.
- Davian first meets Selwin while Selwin is guarded by elite Rezin guards. Selwin introduces himself cheerfully and asks if Davian is one of his father's friends. Provenance: `imports/normalized/melissa-copy.md:4590-4675`, first Davian/Selwin meeting, paragraphs 1791-1800.
- Selwin casually overrules guards by saying he wants to talk to Davian, causing Davian to recognize the social power of even an unPresented High Clade child. Provenance: `imports/normalized/melissa-copy.md:4590-4675`, paragraphs 1800-1804.
- Selwin does not appear arrogant in that first meeting; Davian sees bright curiosity and finds it hard to view him as a monster. Selwin reminds Davian of Kael. Provenance: `imports/normalized/melissa-copy.md:4590-4675`, paragraphs 1804-1810.
- Selwin says Niall is the strongest and smartest man in the world. He repeats Niall's earlier lesson that importance comes from whether someone can protect the people they love, then admits Niall no longer says this since "he got sad." Provenance: `imports/normalized/melissa-copy.md:4590-4675`, paragraphs 1808-1818.
- Selwin later sits nearby while Maya trains and Harvick trains Davian. He is seated cross-legged, staring at a rock with intense concentration. Provenance: `imports/normalized/melissa-copy.md:5488-5728`, training courtyard, paragraph 2122.
- During a magical outburst, Selwin's sigil flares with raw uncontrolled light, he reacts angrily to being told to calm and focus, and he says Maya and Niall only care about magic. The stone beneath him begins to decay, spreading in jagged rings and threatening a courtyard wall. Provenance: `imports/normalized/melissa-copy.md:5800-6245`, Selwin outburst/rescue, paragraphs 2197-2208.
- Maya tells Selwin he is not ready and screams that he will kill himself. Selwin collapses after one last flare of his sigil. Provenance: `imports/normalized/melissa-copy.md:5800-6245`, paragraphs 2209-2218.
- Davian/Arrek reaches Selwin, grabs him, and carries him away while Maya redirects the collapse and heat backlash. Maya later says Davian saved Selwin's life. Provenance: `imports/normalized/melissa-copy.md:5800-6245`, paragraphs 2222-2263.
- In the infirmary, Selwin is pale and still, with a healer's familiar pulse-syncing against his chest. Niall sees Selwin's unfamiliar sigil and says it is not his or Alara's. Provenance: `imports/normalized/melissa-copy.md:5800-6245`, paragraphs 2273-2286.
- Maya tells Niall that Selwin thought Niall only cared about magic and war. Niall says he thought Maya stepping up meant Selwin would not need as much from him, and Maya says Selwin thought Niall did not want to be with him anymore, as if Selwin did something wrong. Provenance: `imports/normalized/melissa-copy.md:5800-6245`, paragraphs 2286-2308.
- In Chapter 37, Maya splits training attention between Davian and Selwin. Selwin is still trying to find his Shroud reflex, and Maya worries that refusing to put him through the harsh fear-based training Niall used on her may simply delay the inevitable. Provenance: `imports/normalized/melissa-copy.md:1-65`, Chapter 37 opening, paragraphs 9-12.
- In later training, Maya brings Davian to work on Shrouds with Selwin. Selwin greets him as Arrek; Davian corrects his name, apologizes for lying, says he thought the Rezins were enemies but was wrong, and says he is trying to help. Selwin accepts this and says Davian is not as sad today because he cares about what he said. Provenance: `imports/normalized/melissa-copy.md:21580-21645`, Chapter 36/37 training, paragraphs 7526-7538.
- Author clarification: Selwin's ability name `Dissolution` is acceptable, though characters do not frequently name magic types in dialogue because individual abilities are rare. Provenance: author feedback on 2026-06-24.
- Author clarification: Selwin currently cannot form a Shroud at all. Due to his heritage and materially different abilities, it may never be possible for him, but that question does not become very relevant in Book 1. Provenance: author feedback on 2026-06-24.
- Author clarification: Niall subconsciously blames Selwin for Alara's postpartum psychosis. Niall does not know he is doing this, but he may make excuses to avoid or not train Selwin because Selwin makes him uncomfortable. Provenance: author feedback on 2026-06-24.
- Author clarification: Selwin's exact abilities do not need to be dialed in for Book 1. Ultimately, Selwin will be important to the final series resolution because his abilities are what Elric originally hoped to breed as a way to kill the extraplanar deity. This is referenced by Elric's Ink specter beneath the citadel when he says something about the arrow of time. Provenance: author feedback on 2026-06-24.
- Author clarification: Selwin's future martial-training thread with Davian can be stored as future-book planning material because it likely will not fit in the current book. Provenance: author feedback on 2026-06-24.

## Consistent information

- Selwin is young, pre-Presentation, and not yet safely trained into a stable Shroud/magus role.
- Selwin admires Maya and Niall and wants to be seen as capable.
- Selwin's relationship with Niall is shaped by Niall's withdrawal and grief. Selwin interprets the distance as personal failure or loss of worth.
- Selwin's magic manifests dangerously as Dissolution and appears tied to decay/erosion, but exact mechanics remain unresolved.
- Selwin is thematically important as an innocent who complicates Davian's hatred of the High Clades.
- Selwin's interactions with Davian help reveal Davian's underlying protective values and his ability to care about someone he initially wants to categorize as enemy.

## Contradictions and version conflicts

### Shroud status

- Version A: Current manuscript has Selwin trying and failing to find the Shroud reflex in Chapter 37.
- Version B: Planning asks whether Selwin ever develops a Shroud and suggests he may not make a Shroud in the normal way.
- Resolution: Accepted file should say Selwin currently cannot form a Shroud at all. It may never be possible because of his heritage or materially different abilities, but that question does not become very relevant in Book 1.

### Dissolution mechanics

- Version A: Manuscript shows raw light, an unfamiliar sigil, and stone decay/erosion.
- Version B: Planning names this as Dissolution and connects it to possible crossbreed/combo ability and maybe endgame/deity implications.
- Resolution: Accept `Dissolution` as Selwin's ability name, while keeping exact mechanics for `magic-dissolution` or later magic review.

### Niall's relationship to Selwin

- Version A: Manuscript says Niall tried not to push Selwin like he pushed Maya and recognizes Selwin felt abandoned.
- Version B: Planning speculates that Alara had postpartum psychosis and Niall subconsciously blames Selwin.
- Resolution: Accept Niall's subconscious blame of Selwin for Alara's postpartum psychosis. Emphasize that Niall does not consciously know he is doing this; he makes excuses to avoid or not train Selwin because Selwin makes him uncomfortable.

### Selwin as crossbreed / demi-human worldbuilding

- Version A: Bullet notes mention a weird combo ability from being a crossbreed.
- Version B: Current accepted family records establish Maya's Naeroth heritage through Alara, but Selwin's exact heritage implications and power origin are not reviewed.
- Resolution: Keep crossbreed/demi-human mechanics candidate until `char-alara`, `culture-naeroth`, and magic reviews.

## Proposed accepted record

- Selwin Rezin is Niall Rezin and Alara's young son, Maya Rezin's younger brother, and an unPresented High Clade child.
- He is cheerful, open, eager, and deeply admiring of Maya and Niall, but he is also anxious about whether he is strong enough and whether Niall still wants to be with him.
- Selwin is at a dangerous pre-Shroud manifestation stage and currently cannot form a Shroud at all. It may never be possible because of his heritage or materially different abilities, but that question does not become very relevant in Book 1.
- Selwin's relationship with Niall is shaped by Niall's grief, withdrawal, mistaken belief that Maya stepping up meant Selwin needed less from him, and subconscious blame related to Alara's postpartum psychosis.
- Selwin's magical outburst manifests an unfamiliar sigil and a destructive decay/erosion effect called Dissolution that threatens the courtyard and nearly kills him. Exact mechanics remain for magic review.
- Davian saves Selwin during the outburst. This matters for Davian's arc because Selwin is an innocent High Clade child who reminds Davian of Kael and exposes Davian's underlying desire to protect rather than only destroy.
- After the Heart theft, Davian apologizes to Selwin, gives his real name, and says he wants to help make things right. Selwin accepts this and notices that Davian seems less sad because he cares about what he is saying.
