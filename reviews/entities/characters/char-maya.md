# Entity Review: Maya Rezin

## Proposed identity

- Maya Rezin is a principal POV character, Rezin heir, High Clade magus, daughter of Niall Rezin and Alara, and older sister to Selwin. This is a proposed identity summary, not yet an approved bible record.
- She is closely tied to the Rezin citadel, Presentation politics, Shroud magic, fate/Skein perception, Registry and zan-lattice work, and the Maya/Davian romance and trust arc.

## Manuscript-established information

- Maya is presented in the prologue as a young noble/magus preparing for the High Clade Council and Presentation. She is attended by Erynn, reassured by Niall, and concerned about not letting him down. Provenance: `imports/normalized/melissa-copy.md:233-258`, Prologue, paragraphs 89-105.
- Physical details currently in the manuscript include midnight-dark hair, golden eyes, formal midnight-blue/silver clothing, and protective enchantments that preserve mobility for her magic. Provenance: `imports/normalized/melissa-copy.md:233-234`, Prologue, paragraph 89.
- Maya can act with humor and warmth around Erynn before formal pressure overtakes the scene. Provenance: `imports/normalized/melissa-copy.md:237-243`, Prologue, paragraphs 91-95.
- Niall's approval matters intensely to Maya; she responds to his reassurance with gratitude, longing, and a promise not to disappoint him. Provenance: `imports/normalized/melissa-copy.md:246-258`, Prologue, paragraphs 97-105.
- After the attack on the citadel, Maya is dissociated, guilty, and shaken by the scale of force she used. She tells Erynn she held the line because no one else could, then shifts into work and investigation rather than processing the experience. Provenance: `imports/normalized/melissa-copy.md:670-760`, Chapter 1, paragraphs 360-410.
- Maya is recognized by soldiers as "Lady Maya" after the wall defense. Provenance: `imports/normalized/melissa-copy.md:360-362`, Chapter 1, paragraphs 360-362.
- Maya is Niall's daughter; Niall explicitly frames the battle failure partly around "my daughter" during his confrontation with commanders. Provenance: `imports/normalized/melissa-copy.md:392-410`, Chapter 1, paragraphs 392-410.
- Maya studies and evaluates High Clade politics at the Presentation. She sits in the Rezin box, observes other Clades, and notices Archeon, Nyctaris, Drakhal, and other political/magical signals. Provenance: `imports/normalized/melissa-copy.md:690-699`, Prologue/Presentation section, paragraphs 691-699.
- Maya has strong technical magical ability. In Chapter 37, she uses Shroud, schema construction, heat movement, containment surfaces, and a shaped flame discharge while training Davian. Provenance: `imports/normalized/melissa-copy.md:80-120`, Chapter 37, paragraphs 24-37.
- Maya understands some technique as outside standard High Clade norms and connects her research to understanding Archeon methods. Provenance: `imports/normalized/melissa-copy.md:116-117`, Chapter 37, paragraph 36.
- Maya is training Davian and Selwin in Chapter 37 while also working nights on schema and Registry zan lattices. She is sleep-deprived and under pressure. Provenance: `imports/normalized/melissa-copy.md:15-40`, Chapter 37, paragraphs 5-12.
- Maya remembers Niall's harsh childhood Shroud training and does not want to impose the same fear on Selwin, though she worries that the pressure may have been necessary. Provenance: `imports/normalized/melissa-copy.md:40-43`, Chapter 37, paragraph 12.
- Maya experiences fate/Skein perception as bright threads that tug through imagined outcomes and past/future possibilities. In Chapter 30, this compounds guilt over "Arrek"/Davian and the Heart theft aftermath. Provenance: `imports/normalized/melissa-copy.md:16935-16985`, Chapter 30, paragraphs 5949-5965.
- Maya blames herself for failing to anticipate Davian/Arrek's betrayal and is corrected by Aldira, who compares her self-blame to Niall's. Provenance: `imports/normalized/melissa-copy.md:16950-16975`, Chapter 30, paragraphs 5954-5962.
- Maya and Davian have an emotionally and physically charged relationship in the current manuscript: she remembers a kiss and is relieved he survived despite his betrayal. Provenance: `imports/normalized/melissa-copy.md:16947-16984`, Chapter 30, paragraphs 5953-5965.

## Planning-established information

- Ch37 outline positions Maya as trying to hold authority despite realizing the adults around her do not have answers. She projects reassurance to Selwin and the Rezins while wishing she had someone to look to. Provenance: `imports/normalized/book-1-outline.md:127-131`, Ch37, paragraphs 43-44.
- Planning states Maya is working on Registry identification, Archeon-countering schema, and training Davian/Selwin while Erynn says she is a teenager and needs sleep. Provenance: `imports/normalized/book-1-outline.md:109-119`, Ch37 outline, paragraphs 36-39.
- Planning links Maya and Davian's development to flexibility, accepting family histories, and Maya letting go of perfectionism/rigidity. Provenance: `imports/normalized/book-1-outline.md:164-168`, Ch37 outline, paragraphs 56-57.
- Planning states Maya may be the only one who cannot put her Shroud down, even asleep, because Niall trained her never to let her magus visage drop; this is not yet treated here as manuscript-established. Provenance: `imports/normalized/book-1-outline.md:207-211`, Ch37 outline, paragraphs 71-72.
- Planning frames Maya's end-arc as accepting Niall's flaws, choosing her own standards, grounding herself, and using help rather than winning alone. Provenance: `imports/normalized/book-1-outline.md:416-429`, raid/climax planning, paragraphs 162-167.
- Planning says Maya's fear of imperfection is tied to not wanting to be a powerless child again, and that efficient use of mind/attention becomes part of her power development. Provenance: `imports/normalized/book-1-outline.md:431-445`, raid/climax planning, paragraphs 168-172.
- Planning has Maya realizing she cannot beat Vorren alone and needs help; the final magical/combat beat integrates lessons rather than pure solitary force. Provenance: `imports/normalized/book-1-outline.md:447-470`, raid/climax planning, paragraphs 174-182.
- Planning includes potential political pressure around Caius/Drakhal marriage, Niall's weakness, and high-Clade politics accelerating around Maya. Provenance: `imports/normalized/book-1-outline.md:276-302`, broader plot planning.

## Existing decisions

- Maya's accepted physical direction is dark eyes with subtle purple rings around the pupil and outer edge of the iris; Alara is from Naeroth and would have had fully purple eyes. Maya's recurring Presented-magus visual direction includes a high ponytail with a rose-gold ring and sleeveless expensive magus battle outfit with rose-gold accents and boots. Provenance: `decisions/0001-maya-naeroth-heritage-and-appearance.md`.
- Naeroth demi-humans are an exception to the broader cultural assumption that only humans have access to hereditary magic and Shrouds. Naeroth heritage can rarely grant fateweaving and Convergence heat transfer; Alara had both these and a Shroud. High Clade culture includes racist anti-demi-human sentiment, and Maya should receive racist sentiment due to dual heritage from people outside the Rezin sphere of influence. Provenance: `decisions/0002-naeroth-demi-human-worldbuilding.md`.
- Maya's Shroud is effectively permanent while she is awake, but not continuously permanent while asleep. She can mute it enough to eat or interact with objects, but this requires conscious effort and can get out of control. Her Shroud can activate during sleep if she has a bad dream or similar trigger. Provenance: `decisions/0003-maya-shroud-and-prologue-revision-direction.md`.
- The prologue should eventually be revised so Maya is attacked by a distant, not-directly-visible Drakhal-affiliated magus; her frantic reaction causes widespread destruction that affects Davian's combat. Post-combat dissociation/flashbacks/numbness should be barely shown in the prologue and more likely emerge later. Provenance: `decisions/0003-maya-shroud-and-prologue-revision-direction.md`.

## Consistent information

- Maya is a central Rezin heir/magus figure under extreme expectations from Niall, the High Clades, and herself.
- Maya's relationship to Niall is emotionally central: she wants his approval, carries his standards, and must eventually confront his flaws.
- Maya is unusually powerful and technically capable, especially with Shroud/schema/fate-adjacent perception, but her power is tied to pressure, vigilance, and perfectionism.
- Maya and Davian's relationship is a major emotional/narrative thread involving attraction, misunderstanding, betrayal, trust, and eventual cooperation.
- Erynn and Aldira function as important grounding/corrective figures for Maya in different registers: Erynn personal/practical, Aldira strategic/dry.

## Contradictions and version conflicts

### Eye color

- Version A: The current manuscript describes Maya's eyes as golden. Source: `imports/normalized/melissa-copy.md:233-234`.
- Version B: A comment anchor around Davian's observation describes "piercing dark eyes." Source: `imports/normalized/melissa-copy.comments.md:484-491`.
- Version C: Bullet notes say "Purple eyes for Maya." Source: `imports/normalized/bullet-notes.md:195`.
- Consequence: This affects physical-description canon and any future prose.
- Resolution: Superseded by author decision. Accepted direction is dark eyes with subtle purple rings around the pupil and outer edge of the iris. Source: `decisions/0001-maya-naeroth-heritage-and-appearance.md`.

### Maya's Shroud permanence

- Version A: The current manuscript establishes strong Shroud skill and childhood Shroud pressure, but does not in the cited passages explicitly state that Maya cannot ever put her Shroud down.
- Version B: The outline states it is important that Maya is the only one who cannot put her Shroud down, even asleep. Source: `imports/normalized/book-1-outline.md:207-211`.
- Consequence: Major magic-system and character-psychology implication.
- Resolution: Superseded by author decision. Accepted direction is that Maya's Shroud is effectively permanent while awake, can be consciously muted, can get out of control, and may activate while asleep during bad dreams or similar triggers; it is not continuously permanent while asleep. Source: `decisions/0003-maya-shroud-and-prologue-revision-direction.md`.

### First-combat/prologue framing

- Version A: Current prose already shows Maya after the wall defense as dissociated and guilty. Source: `imports/normalized/melissa-copy.md:670-760`.
- Version B: Bullet notes propose revising the prologue to show her barely anchoring her Shroud and losing control in her first real combat. Source: `imports/normalized/bullet-notes.md:119-150`.
- Consequence: Affects reader interpretation of Maya's violence, Davian's judgment, and her early competence/control.
- Resolution: Accepted revision direction. Prologue should include a distant unseen Drakhal-affiliated magus attack that Maya reacts to frantically, causing widespread destruction that affects Davian's combat; dissociation/flashbacks/numbness mostly surface later. Source: `decisions/0003-maya-shroud-and-prologue-revision-direction.md`.

### Naeroth heritage and High Clade racism

- Version A: Current manuscript does not yet foreground Maya's Naeroth dual heritage or anti-demi-human racism.
- Version B: Author decision establishes Alara as Naeroth, Maya as dual-heritage, High Clade culture as racist toward demi-humans, and Maya as receiving racist sentiment from outside the Rezin sphere. Source: `decisions/0002-naeroth-demi-human-worldbuilding.md`.
- Consequence: Requires later revision planning and world-system/entity reviews, especially `culture-naeroth-lineages`, `culture-clade-hierarchy`, `char-alara`, `magic-fateweaving-skein`, and `thread-maya-davian-romance`.
- Resolution: Accepted direction, pending implementation and detailed worldbuilding.

### How much romance is current vs planned

- Version A: Current manuscript shows attraction, kiss memory, relief at Davian's survival, betrayal pain, and charged training scenes. Source: `imports/normalized/melissa-copy.md:80-120`, `imports/normalized/melissa-copy.md:16947-16984`.
- Version B: Outline/bullet notes include additional future romance beats, night scenes, vulnerability, confession/kiss alternatives, and late-book death/resurrection beats. Sources: `imports/normalized/book-1-outline.md:122-131`, `imports/normalized/book-1-outline.md:174-211`, `imports/normalized/bullet-notes.md:39-60`, `imports/normalized/bullet-notes.md:198-204`.
- Consequence: Accepted file should distinguish current manuscript state from intended arc.
- Newer/likely status: Current prose is manuscript state; outline/notes are planning/candidates unless approved.

## Proposed accepted record

Proposal only:

- Maya Rezin is a principal POV character and Rezin heir, daughter of Niall Rezin and Alara and sister to Selwin.
- In the current manuscript, she is a young High Clade magus under intense political, familial, and magical expectations.
- She is powerful, technically minded, and trained to project command, but she is also anxious, self-blaming, and vulnerable to perfectionism.
- Her magic includes advanced Shroud use, schema construction, heat/flame manipulation, Registry/zan-lattice work, and emerging fate/Skein perception.
- Her relationship with Niall is defined by love, approval-seeking, inherited standards, and the need to confront his flaws.
- Her relationship with Davian is a major current and intended arc involving attraction, misunderstanding, betrayal, guilt, and possible trust/partnership.
- Physical direction: dark eyes with subtle purple rings around the pupil and outer edge of the iris; this reflects Maya's dual heritage through Alara's Naeroth origin.
- Maya's Shroud is effectively permanent while she is awake. She can mute it enough to eat and interact with objects, but muting requires conscious effort and can get out of control. It can activate while she sleeps if triggered by a bad dream or similar stressor.
- Maya's recurring Presented-magus outfit direction includes a high ponytail with a rose-gold ring and expensive sleeveless magus battle clothing with rose-gold accents and boots.
