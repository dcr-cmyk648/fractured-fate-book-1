# Entity Review: Harvick

## Proposed identity

- Harvick is an unofficial Rezin core-group member, guard, trainer, and trusted practical operator with enough standing to speak directly with Niall, Maya, Aldira, guard commanders, forerunners, and the broader guard structure.
- He is a blunt, funny, emotionally perceptive mentor figure for Davian and a former devoted follower or close associate of Elric Rezin.
- He has a hidden prototypical Ink Pact from Elric's first experiments with the concept. It gives him bursts of unnatural speed and precision connected to movement and sword forms, but he is not presented as a Shroud-bearing magus.
- His main story function is to pull people away from trauma-driven scripts through grounded action, training, humor, and stubborn practical care.

## Consistent information

- Harvick is a skilled fighter and teacher whose training centers the body, attention, presence, and values rather than brute force alone.
- Harvick is unusually good at reading Davian's fear, self-sabotage, and desire to matter.
- Harvick is trusted by Niall and able to vouch for Davian, but he can also have blind spots around people who remind him of himself.
- Harvick's relationship to Elric is a core wound: he loved or deeply admired Elric, believed in him, and blames himself for not helping him.
- Harvick's response to that wound is not withdrawal. He keeps trying to help others move away from destructive trauma scripts, especially Davian.
- Harvick has hidden Ink history tied to Elric. His prototypical Ink Pact gives bursts of unnatural speed and precision, but exact Ink taxonomy belongs to later magic reviews.
- Harvick has Naeroth or Naeroth-adjacent martial/body-training knowledge that may connect to Archeon foundations and Davian's eventual integration of Shroud, Subversion, Ink exposure, and physical technique.
- Harvick has canonical Zirene-developed returning-weapon technology in the form of a special sword and bracers, but the mechanics belong to later object/technology reviews.

## Contradictions and version conflicts

### Guard title and status

- Version A: Manuscript often presents Harvick as a guard or trainer with unusual access and authority.
- Version B: Planning uses terms such as elite guards, forerunners, buddies, and special guard functions.
- Resolution: Harvick's lack of formal title is accepted. He is an unofficial core-group member who resists definition, almost uncle-like because of his closeness to Elric and his wartime/postwar history with Niall.

### Ink Pact vs Ink Boon terminology

- Version A: Current manuscript dialogue calls Harvick's hidden mark an Ink Pact and distinguishes it from Davian's voice-like Ink pressure.
- Version B: Bullet/outline shorthand sometimes says Ink Boon.
- Consequence: Proposed accepted record should use `Ink Pact` for Harvick and leave exact Ink taxonomy for `magic-ink-boons`.

### Harvick's movement sense

- Version A: Manuscript establishes a feeling for faster or correct ways to move after Elric's Ink experiment.
- Version B: Planning floats broader special martial arts, reality-damage power, bracers, special sword, returning weapon, and Codex/aura material.
- Resolution: Accept the movement-sense seed as part of Harvick's prototypical Ink Pact. Keep broader Ink mechanics and Codex/aura material for later reviews.

### Treasury injury/resurrection

- Version A: Chapter summary explicitly says Davian fatally injures Harvick and Niall revives him.
- Version B: Detailed prose for the sequence may still be planned/revisable, and some details of weapons, staging, and dialogue remain candidates.
- Resolution: Accept the broad beat. Davian stops Harvick's heart with blunt force trauma, and Niall revives Harvick with lightning. The exact prose execution remains candidate material, especially because Davian may not understand what happened.

## Proposed accepted record

- Harvick has an unofficial but central role in the Rezin sphere. He does not have a clean formal title and resists attempts to define one, but everyone knows him and treats him as part of the core group around Niall, Aldira, guard commanders, forerunners, and the old war network.
- He is an expert fighter and teacher. His method emphasizes body awareness, stance, breath, present-moment attention, and acting from values rather than spiraling into fear or control.
- Harvick becomes one of Davian's most important mentors and friends. He sees Davian's pain and talent early, vouches for him more than once, trains him, challenges his self-sabotage, and keeps pushing him toward choice and responsibility rather than emptiness or self-definition through past violence.
- Harvick is perceptive and emotionally intelligent but not polished. He uses humor, bluntness, and awkward practical care more than formal therapy language.
- Harvick is trusted by Niall and has enough standing to speak frankly to Niall and Maya, but his empathy for wounded fighters can be a blind spot.
- Harvick is almost uncle-like in the Rezin core because of his closeness to Elric and his history beside Niall during the war and after.
- Harvick's core wound is Elric. He loved and admired Elric, believed in him as more than a mentor, saw him descend into depression and madness, and blames himself for being unable to help.
- Harvick's response to Elric is central to his current purpose: he keeps trying to help others move off trauma-driven paths. This makes him a key disruption to Elric's plan.
- Harvick worries that Davian may follow a similar path to Elric, and that Niall may already be partly doing so. He voluntarily exposes himself to painful reminders because he knows helping is what he needs to do long-term.
- Harvick has a hidden prototypical Ink Pact from Elric's first experiments with the concept. It did not give him a voice or command pressure like Davian's Ink; instead it imbues him with bursts of unnatural speed and precision and a feeling for faster or more correct ways to move.
- Harvick has Naeroth or Naeroth-adjacent body-training knowledge that helps connect Davian's martial background, Archeon physical foundations, and later magic integration.
- Harvick has a special sword and bracers developed by Zirene that let the weapon be summoned back to his hand. This technology should only be hinted at before the Treasury fight, where Harvick summons his sword as Davian attacks with a lethal weapon.
- Davian stops Harvick's heart with blunt force trauma during the Treasury confrontation. Niall revives him by shocking him with lightning, though characters do not understand or name the medical mechanism in modern terms. From Davian's perspective, the event may only register as Niall shooting lightning through Harvick.
