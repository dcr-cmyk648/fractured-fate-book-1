# Entity Review: Kelyra

## Proposed identity

- Kelyra is Davian's mother, Jalen's partner, and a central Concord/underclass figure tied to Archeon lineage, Subversion, martial training, family protection, and the Heart-theft aftermath.
- She is a dangerous martial combatant and strategist who understands Registry risks, aura/name constraints, and the practical mechanics of the Heart infiltration plan.
- She is directly descended from Archeon breeding programs. The exact number of generations between Kelyra and Serathis is unclear.

## Manuscript-established information

- Kelyra is Davian's mother. She commands the family space, sends Kael and Lirien away from adult business, and calls Davian and Jalen into the central hall after they return from the Syndicate. Provenance: `imports/normalized/melissa-copy.md:1707-1746`, Chapter 3, paragraphs 793-805.
- When Jalen says they must steal the Heart, Kelyra is shocked, then immediately pivots to planning because there is no time to waste. Provenance: `imports/normalized/melissa-copy.md:1752-1779`, Chapter 3, paragraphs 807-816.
- Kelyra sharply criticizes Davian for volunteering to kill Niall without asking them, reading it as martyrdom, proving a point, or an excuse to chase glory and kill a High Clade. Provenance: `imports/normalized/melissa-copy.md:1800-1848`, Chapter 3, paragraphs 823-838.
- Kelyra knows enough about Rezin security and Registry magic to explain the aura/name problem, the passive-mode requirement, and the danger of active Registry magic burning Davian out of existence. Provenance: `imports/normalized/melissa-copy.md:1854-1915`, Chapter 3, paragraphs 841-861.
- Kelyra recognizes Maya's emergence as a new Rezin heir/magus as strategically important and understands High Clade magic as generationally trained and bred for dominance. Provenance: `imports/normalized/melissa-copy.md:1857-1878`, Chapter 3, paragraphs 842-849.
- Aldira says Kelyra claims Archeon lineage and never manifested a Shroud, while Davian has manifested a Shroud and demonstrated basic magical manipulation. Author clarification: Kelyra is directly descended from Archeon breeding programs. Provenance: `imports/normalized/melissa-copy.md:20305-20312`, later manuscript material, paragraph 7087; author feedback on 2026-06-23.
- Davian says his mother called their magic Subversion and used it to drain zan that powered defenses during break-ins. Author clarification: Kelyra's "Subversion" is her misnomer for Archeon hereditary kinesis. Provenance: `imports/normalized/melissa-copy.md:20428-20452`, later manuscript material, paragraph 7128; author feedback on 2026-06-23.
- Harvick says some of the things Davian learned from Kelyra feel like precursors or preparatory movements related to Archeon/Naeroth-style foundational techniques. Provenance: `imports/normalized/melissa-copy.md:20509-20525`, later manuscript material, paragraphs 7155-7157.
- In Chapter 35 material, Kelyra fights effectively against multiple opponents in an Archeon arena and is described by Serathis as a genius for martial combat. Provenance: `imports/normalized/melissa-copy.md:20650-20725`, Chapter 35, paragraphs 7206-7229.
- Kelyra's Chapter 35 internal memory states that Archeon children are not raised by mothers, are taken at birth, and are trained rather than raised; this is why she ran when she realized she was pregnant with Selina. Provenance: `imports/normalized/melissa-copy.md:20743-20767`, Chapter 35, paragraphs 7237-7245.
- Kelyra recognizes Lethira and remembers correcting her stance when Lethira was a child. Lethira later accuses Kelyra of abandoning her. Author clarification: Lethira was likely an Archeon child of another parent whom Kelyra helped raise before escaping, but this remains to be checked. Provenance: `imports/normalized/melissa-copy.md:20743-20883`, Chapter 35, paragraphs 7237-7283; author feedback on 2026-06-23.
- Serathis calls Kelyra daughter, granddaughter, and descendant, says Kelyra was released on purpose after receiving instruction, and says Kelyra is bursting with talent but lacks motivation. Provenance: `imports/normalized/melissa-copy.md:20716-20731`, `imports/normalized/melissa-copy.md:20949-20985`, Chapter 35.
- Kelyra has old Ink scars or old Ink on her arm that reacts near Serathis, and she hears or feels a whisper telling her to kill Serathis. Provenance: `imports/normalized/melissa-copy.md:21135-21150`, Chapter 35, paragraphs 7368-7374.

## Consistent information

- Kelyra is practical, sharp, family-protective, and willing to pivot quickly from shock into planning.
- She is critical of Davian's self-sacrificial or glory-seeking impulses and understands his pattern of acting alone to "protect" others.
- She is directly descended from Archeon breeding programs, but the exact number of generations between Kelyra and Serathis remains unresolved.
- Her Shroud is blocked by trauma and deeply ingrained suppression.
- She is an expert martial combatant whose training shaped Davian's fighting style.
- Her relationship to Archeon includes trauma around children being taken, trained, and used.

## Contradictions and version conflicts

### Archeon lineage: claim vs direct descent

- Version A: Aldira states Kelyra claims Archeon lineage.
- Version B: Serathis calls Kelyra daughter/granddaughter/descendant, though she also manipulates language and may be using lineage expansively rather than literally.
- Consequence: Kelyra's accepted file needs careful wording unless the author wants direct descent accepted now.
- Resolution: Kelyra is directly descended from Archeon breeding programs. The exact number of generations between Kelyra and Serathis remains unclear.

### Shroud status

- Version A: Aldira says Kelyra never manifested a Shroud.
- Version B: Chapter 35 suggests a latent or suppressed Shroud-adjacent possibility: Serathis says all Kelyra needs is a Shroud, Kelyra reaches inward, feels pressure/heat, and panics because a Shroud would let Archeon keep her and take her child.
- Resolution: Kelyra has a Shroud, but it is blocked by trauma and deeply ingrained suppression. She knows it is there, but the idea that she must suppress it to escape Archeon is so ingrained that even thinking about manifesting it is very unpleasant.

### Kelyra's agency in the Heart theft

- Version A: Kelyra pivots to planning once Jalen and Davian return with the mission, and planning says she delivers the Heart because their position is exposed and they want promised power.
- Version B: Davian's approved arc emphasizes his agency in choosing to steal the Heart, not merely family coercion.
- Consequence: Kelyra's record should include her role without shifting Davian's culpability away from him.

### Motherhood and children

- Version A: Current manuscript family-home scene includes Kael and Lirien and identifies Kelyra as Davian's mother; Chapter 35 says she ran when pregnant with Selina.
- Version B: Later normalized manuscript passages spell the younger sister's name as `Lirin`.
- Resolution: Accepted spelling is `Lirien`. Kelyra's children are Davian, Kael, Lirien, and Selina, deceased. Lethira was likely an Archeon child of another parent whom Kelyra helped raise before escaping, but this remains candidate until notes are checked.

## Proposed accepted record

Proposal only:

- Kelyra is Davian's mother, Jalen's partner, and a central Concord/underclass figure tied to the Heart theft.
- She is practical, dry, sharp, and strategically fast: after shock, she pivots into planning because there is no time to waste.
- She criticizes Davian's self-sacrificial and glory-seeking patterns and sees his willingness to kill Niall as part of a dangerous pattern.
- She understands the Registry, aura/name matching, passive mode, and the dangers of active Registry magic well enough to plan Davian's infiltration.
- She is directly descended from Archeon breeding programs and has Archeon-linked training.
- Her Shroud is blocked by trauma. She knows it is there, but suppressing it is tied to survival and escape from Archeon, making even the idea of manifesting it deeply unpleasant.
- She taught Davian martial and kinesis/Subversion-adjacent foundations; Davian identifies their magic as Subversion, but this is Kelyra's misnomer for Archeon hereditary kinesis.
- Kelyra is a brilliant martial combatant and a major source of Davian's body-based fighting framework.
- Kelyra's talents are strong Archeon hereditary kinesis and genius martial art talent, linked to speed of mind. The speed-of-thought aspect should be shown rather than codified or quantified.
- Kelyra's trauma around Archeon includes children being taken from mothers, trained instead of raised, and used by the Clade. She ran when pregnant with Selina.
- Kelyra's children are Davian, Kael, Lirien, and Selina, deceased. Lethira remains unresolved.
- Detailed Serathis/Kelyra generation count, Lethira parentage, and Ink cooption remain unresolved.
