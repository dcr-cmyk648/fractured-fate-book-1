# Entity Review: Heart / Seraph Heart

## Proposed identity

This packet treats `object-seraph-heart` as the Heart / Seraph Heart object: physical identity, containment, power source role, Treasury/Registry relationship, theft/removal consequences, Ink/Skein/fate implications, and object-specific future-plan material.

It should not own:

- `loc-treasury`: Treasury chamber placement, sealed entrance, gravity/weightlessness as a location effect
- `object-tzorrax-sphere`: the sphere/containment object as a separate artifact if kept separate
- `org-registry`: Registry organization, modes, and operational procedures
- `magic-zan-lattices`: zä lattice theory and Heart-powered inefficiency model
- `magic-fateweaving-skein`: broad Skein/fate mechanics
- `thread-davian-identity-guilt`: Davian's resurrection, death/return arc, and relationship consequences

## Established constraints from approved records

- The Treasury is high up in the Rezin citadel and contains the Tzorrax Heart/Sphere containment setup. Provenance: `bible/locations/loc-treasury.md`.
- Treasury weightlessness is a side effect of the Heart interacting with its containment armature; it is not operationally controlled by the Registry. Provenance: `bible/locations/loc-treasury.md`.
- Weightlessness ends when Davian shatters the sphere and removes the Heart. Provenance: `bible/locations/loc-treasury.md`.
- Major Rezin zä systems work but shrink/fail after the Heart is stolen because they were overbuilt around near-limitless input. Provenance: `bible/magic/magic-zan-lattices.md`; `CONSISTENCY_QUEUE.md`.
- Davian's end-book resurrection is accepted future-plan material, but exact cost and Heart/Skein/reality mechanics remain unresolved. Provenance: `bible/magic/magic-fateweaving-skein.md`; `CONSISTENCY_QUEUE.md`.
