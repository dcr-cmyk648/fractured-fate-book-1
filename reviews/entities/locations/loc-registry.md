# Entity Review: Registry Chamber

## Proposed identity

This packet treats `loc-registry` as the physical Registry chamber inside the Rezin citadel: its placement, doors, room layout, visible interface, staff/guard arrangement, and user-facing chamber behavior.

It should not own:

- `org-registry`: Registry as institution/security system
- `magic-zan-lattices`: true-name/aura/zä-lattice technical mechanics
- `loc-rezin-citadel`: overall citadel map and fortress-city structure
- `loc-treasury`: Treasury/vault chamber and high-up Heart infrastructure
- `object-seraph-heart`: exact Heart-to-Registry power path
- later revision files: scene choreography and manuscript cleanup

## Manuscript-established information

- Contractors are brought into the citadel while the Registry is off or in a safe processing state, then are added before the Registry comes back online. Provenance: `imports/normalized/melissa-copy.md:2670-2720`.
- The route from initial contractor holding areas to the Registry passes through Rezin citadel corridors with polished stone walls and protective zä. Provenance: `imports/normalized/melissa-copy.md:2670-2755`.
- The Registry entrance has towering/heavy double doors and guards. Provenance: `imports/normalized/melissa-copy.md:2750-2765`; `imports/normalized/melissa-copy.md:15156-15163`.
- The chamber is circular or wide and circular, built of pale/smooth stone, with walls etched in glowing or luminescent zä/sigils. Provenance: `imports/normalized/melissa-copy.md:2766-2805`; `imports/normalized/melissa-copy.md:15156-15172`.
- The chamber has a central platform/interface, described in the current manuscript as an arcane circle, pedestal, and later an aurametric array/projection sphere. Provenance: `imports/normalized/melissa-copy.md:2766-2805`; `imports/normalized/melissa-copy.md:15166-15183`.
- A translucent/projection map of the citadel appears in the central interface and can zoom/refine to show rooms, locations, names, and auras. Provenance: `imports/normalized/melissa-copy.md:2775-2805`; `imports/normalized/melissa-copy.md:15172-15183`; `imports/normalized/melissa-copy.md:15940-15955`.
- Registration can inscribe a name into the chamber stone and show a matching mote/name in the projected citadel. Provenance: `imports/normalized/melissa-copy.md:2790-2805`; `imports/normalized/melissa-copy.comments.md:368-380`.
- Maya can use the chamber to verify a registered name and locate a registered person if that person is in the citadel. Provenance: `imports/normalized/melissa-copy.md:15172-15234`.
- If a person is registered but not locatable, the chamber can confirm the entry while showing the person is outside the citadel. Provenance: `imports/normalized/melissa-copy.md:15186-15234`.
- Later, the Registry emits a softer location pulse/chime, and the projection refines inward until a Treasury corridor and multiple auras/names are visible. Provenance: `imports/normalized/melissa-copy.md:15940-15955`.

## Existing approved constraints

- The Registry chamber is below ground in the Rezin citadel. Provenance: `bible/locations/loc-rezin-citadel.md`; `bible/organizations/org-registry.md`; author feedback on 2026-06-24.
- The Treasury is high up. There are no elevators or similar magical vertical-transport solutions in the accepted direction. Provenance: `bible/locations/loc-rezin-citadel.md`; author feedback on 2026-06-24.
- The Registry is Rezin-specific, ancient, true-name/aura-based, and central to Rezin security and movement control. Provenance: `bible/organizations/org-registry.md`.
- Passive mode identifies and alarms without full lethal response; active mode identifies and triggers lethal magical response, with limited discharge capacity. Provenance: `bible/organizations/org-registry.md`.
- Major Rezin Registry/Treasury infrastructure uses ancient high-power zä lattices and is overbuilt around Heart input. Provenance: `bible/magic/magic-zan-lattices.md`.
- Davian's accepted heist path is that he adds Arrek's name and relies on the Ink Pact/aura bypass. He does not add and later remove his own true name. Provenance: `bible/organizations/org-registry.md`; `imports/normalized/book-1-outline.md:4111`.

## Consistent information

- The chamber can be below ground while still feeling grand, old, and high-status.
- The physical room contains both administrative/security staff and ancient magical infrastructure.
- The chamber doubles as registration station, verification station, surveillance map, and security control point.
- The projection/map behavior aligns with the accepted Registry function of locating registered people inside the citadel.
- The two-place inscription behavior appears intentional: a registered name can be present in the physical stone and represented in the citadel model/projection.
- The Registry's location-map interface supports the "enhance/refine" behavior described in outline notes.

## Contradictions and version conflicts

### Registry height / high-window escape

- A manuscript comment flags that if Davian keeps a high-window escape from the Registry, the Registry position must change or a magical elevator may be needed.
- Current accepted citadel direction rejects that path: Registry is below ground, Treasury is high up, and no elevators or similar magical vertical transport are accepted.
- Recommendation: record high-window Registry escape as obsolete or revision-problem material, not present-canon chamber geography.
- Provenance: `imports/normalized/melissa-copy.comments.md:45-47`; `bible/locations/loc-rezin-citadel.md`.

### Exact chamber terms

- Current prose uses a mixture of `zän`, `zanwork`, `sigils`, `aurametric array`, `projection sphere`, and `ghost-map`.
- Recommendation: accept the broad physical/interface model while leaving exact final terminology to later terminology/revision cleanup.
- Provenance: `imports/normalized/melissa-copy.md:2766-2805`; `imports/normalized/melissa-copy.md:15156-15234`; `bible/magic/magic-zan-lattices.md`.

### Passive-mode raid response

- Outline notes say the Registry is far enough from the entryway that staff would not hear the entry fight, and that passive tracking can be overwhelmed by multiple fast unregistered attackers.
- This is compatible with accepted Registry passive/active constraints, but it affects scene logistics and should be author-confirmed for the location record.
- Provenance: `imports/normalized/book-1-outline.md:1509-1516`; `bible/organizations/org-registry.md`.

## Proposed accepted record

### present-canon

- The Registry chamber is a below-ground chamber inside the Rezin citadel.
- It is reached through guarded heavy/double doors.
- It is circular or broadly circular, with pale/smooth stone, zä-etched walls, and visible ancient magical infrastructure.
- It contains a central operating interface: platform/circle/pedestal/aurametric array/projection sphere language can be harmonized later, but the accepted model is a central magical control surface.
- The chamber projects or models the citadel and can show registered names, locations, rooms, and aura markers.
- Registration can bind a true-name/aura entry into the chamber system and represent that entry both physically in the stone and in the citadel projection/map.
- Maya and authorized Rezin personnel can use the chamber to verify and locate registered people, refine map results, and read location pulses.
- The chamber is a staffable administrative/security room, not a solitary artifact: scribes, guards, and Registry personnel can work there.
- The chamber is sufficiently interior/remote from the entryway that a rapid entry assault can happen without Registry staff immediately hearing the fight, if the author accepts the outline direction.
- The high-window Registry escape and magical-elevator solution are not accepted geography.

## Questions for the author

Resolved by author feedback on 2026-06-25:

1. The accepted chamber baseline is below ground, guarded heavy/double doors, circular pale/smooth stone room, zä-etched walls, central platform/pedestal/aurametric interface, and citadel projection/map.
2. The "one in the stone and one in the model" idea is accepted: registration appears both as a physical stone inscription and as a marker/name in the citadel projection.
3. The map/projection is accepted as a functional targeted-location interface that can verify, locate, pulse, zoom, and refine results inside the citadel, but it must require someone to be named or otherwise specifically called out. It does not show every person all the time.
4. The chamber is far enough from the main entryway, and below ground, so staff would not hear the initial Archeon entry fight in time.
5. Exact Registry access routes, depth, stairs, and relation to the high-up Treasury remain deferred for later map/revision work.
6. Exact active-mode attack/discharge geometry remains deferred to zä/Heart/revision work rather than this location file.
7. The high-window Registry escape and magical-elevator solution are obsolete/rejected. Davian no longer goes back to the Registry after stealing the Heart.
8. Post-Heart repair ideas stay candidate/future repair material, not realized Book 1 chamber canon.
