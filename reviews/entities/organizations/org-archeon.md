# Entity Review: Archeon

## Proposed identity

Archeon is a current High Clade organization tied to Serathis, hereditary kinesis, Shroud-linked physical/martial methods, Ink Pacts, breeding programs, brutal child training, gauntlet/anchor technology, covert politics, and Kelyra's trauma history.

Archeon is a High Clade because it holds one of the six citadels that form the core of a city-wedge in the Six Cities. It achieved that status by killing off the prior occupying High Clade. Before that rise, Archeon was a Low Clade, like most mid-tier magic families.

Archeon should be reviewed as an organization here. Its physical base belongs to `loc-archeon`, exact magic mechanics to `magic-subversion`, `magic-shroud`, and related magic files, and Serathis's personal motives to `char-serathis`.

- Aldira concludes that Kelyra's escape may not have been accidental: she learned foundational techniques that would support Archeon blood in manifesting and controlling hereditary magic later, provided the right linchpin instruction. Provenance: `imports/normalized/melissa-copy.md:20280-20535`, paragraphs 7158-7164.
- Kelyra is shown in an Archeon arena fighting repeated opponents while Serathis observes her as an experiment. The arena is a stone pit with iron rings, torches, and grates. Provenance: `imports/normalized/melissa-copy.md:20640-21160`, Chapter 35, paragraphs 7206-7223.
- Serathis says Kelyra is a genius for martial combat, never manifested a Shroud while at Archeon, and was let go because doing so produced the result Serathis wanted. Kelyra rejects this as manipulation. Provenance: `imports/normalized/melissa-copy.md:20640-21160`, paragraphs 7223-7233.
- Lethira appears as a young Shrouded Archeon fighter trained in the same rigid forms. Kelyra remembers Archeon children being taken at birth, handed to training halls, returned to the compound at five, and trained rather than raised. - Lethira's Shroud-backed movement is extremely fast and physically dangerous. Kelyra recognizes the classic Archeon forms as clean, efficient, and predictable. Provenance: `imports/normalized/melissa-copy.md:20640-21160`, paragraphs 7247-7263.

## Consistent information

- Archeon is currently a High Clade because it holds one of the six citadels, after rising from Low Clade status by killing the prior occupying High Clade.
- Serathis is Archeon's current Head Magus and leader, and is mother/grandmother/etc. of virtually all current members.
- Archeon uses dark breeding programs and breeding contracts, treating descendants/children as assets for magical development.
- Archeon children are not raised by mothers in a normal family structure; they are taken, trained, and shaped through training halls/compound systems.
- Archeon's public branding emphasizes unusually powerful Shrouds that boost martial ability and strength beyond normal Shroud enhancement.
- Archeon's true power stack combines short-range kinesis rooted in original pre-refinement/pre-original-sin magic, Ink Pacts for faster movement, Shroud use, martial arts, and, for less accomplished practitioners, technology derived from Zirene's designs to buffer and transfer force.
- Archeon methods are powerful but potentially script-bound, predictable, or dependent on physical/mental patterns.
- Kelyra's trauma and Davian's abilities are direct consequences of Archeon lineage/training.
- Kelyra and Davian are the heirs Serathis has been looking for: potential users able to fully combine kinesis, Ink Pacts, Shroud use, and martial arts.
- Archeon is politically connected to Drakhal and moderately influenced by Elric, but Elric does not run Archeon day to day.

## Contradictions and version conflicts

### Archeon label and magic type

- Version A: Older outline shorthand calls Archeon "Transcendence" and describes them as summoners of spirits with ethereal/otherworldly aura.
- Version B: Current manuscript and approved Kelyra material define Archeon around hereditary kinesis/Subversion, Shroud-backed martial/physical technique, gauntlets/anchors, breeding programs, and Serathis.
- Source of each: `imports/normalized/book-1-outline.md:3236-3300`; `imports/normalized/melissa-copy.md:20280-21160`; `decisions/0006-kelyra-archeon-lineage-shroud-and-family.md`.
- Resolution: the `Transcendence` / spirit-summoner label is obsolete. Archeon's powers are the kinesis/Ink/Shroud/martial/technology stack described above.

### Did Kelyra escape, or was she released?

- Version A: Kelyra escaped Archeon, and that escape defines her trauma and family-protection arc.
- Version B: Serathis and Aldira's later inference suggest Kelyra was allowed to leave on purpose after receiving key foundational instruction.
- Source of each: `bible/characters/char-kelyra.md`; `imports/normalized/melissa-copy.md:20280-20535`; `imports/normalized/melissa-copy.md:20640-21160`.
- Likely consequence: This changes whether Kelyra's agency around escape remains straightforward or becomes part of a larger Archeon/Elric breeding plan.
- Resolution: Kelyra was truly allowed to escape. Archeon has a long "release and catch" practice used to keep techniques from becoming stale. Serathis herself was part of an allowed escape, through which she met Elric. Serathis calculated that letting Kelyra go after foundational instruction, eventually to encounter Elric and receive an Ink Pact, was the best use of resources because Kelyra was a martial/magic genius but blocked from forming a Shroud.

### Archeon location and attack geography

- Version A: Notes imply an Archeon presence in a central tower.
- Version B: Notes also question how to reconcile the central-tower Archeon with an Archeon being far away.
- Source: `imports/normalized/book-1-outline.md:1410-1640`, paragraph 585.
- Likely consequence: belongs to `loc-archeon`, raid revision, and chronology consistency, not the organization file.

### Jalen versus Kelyra ownership of Archeon material

- Version A: Older notes assign Archeon-derived magic, Shroud inability, Trial of Dominion, and training roles to Jalen.
- Version B: Author decision transfers those roles to Kelyra, leaving Jalen nonmagical support/logistics.
- Source: `reviews/entities/characters/char-jalen.md`; `decisions/0010-jalen-kelyra-concord-role-swap.md`; `CONSISTENCY_QUEUE.md`.
- Likely consequence: this review should not promote old Jalen Archeon material into Archeon canon except as suspect/obsolete-transfer material.

## Proposed accepted record

Proposal:

- Archeon is currently a High Clade because it holds one of the six citadels that form city-wedge cores in the Six Cities. It rose from Low Clade status by killing the prior occupying High Clade.
- Serathis is Archeon's current Head Magus and leader, and is mother/grandmother/etc. of virtually all current members.
- Archeon publicly brands itself as having exceptionally powerful Shrouds that boost martial ability and strength beyond ordinary Shroud enhancement.
- Archeon's true power stack combines short-range kinesis rooted in original pre-refinement/pre-original-sin magic, Ink Pacts for speed, Shroud use, martial arts, and Zirene-derived force-buffering/transfer technology for less accomplished practitioners.
- Archeon uses brutal breeding and child-training systems. Children born in Archeon are taken from Archeon mothers at birth, trained rather than raised, and shaped through training halls/compound systems.
- Archeon uses release-and-catch practices to keep techniques from becoming stale, usually on members who have not yet manifested a Shroud and are therefore treated as disposable.
- Kelyra was truly allowed to escape as part of this system. Serathis calculated that Kelyra's genius, blocked Shroud, and eventual contact with Elric/Ink made release a better use of resources than keeping her.
- Serathis herself was part of an allowed escape and met Elric that way.
- Elric's influence in Archeon is moderate: he set Serathis up, provided Archeon's first Ink Pact, continues marking Archeon members including Kelyra, and occasionally meets with Serathis, but he does not affect day-to-day operations directly.
- Archeon methods combine physical foundations, martial forms, Shroud use, kinesis, anchors/tools/gauntlets, speed, and cognitive load.
- Archeon combat is powerful at close range but can become predictable or script-bound; forcing an Archeon fighter off script may be a weakness.
- Kelyra is directly descended from Archeon breeding programs and carries Archeon trauma. Her Shroud suppression and martial/kinesis talents should be linked to Archeon, but her personal arc remains in `char-kelyra`.
- Davian's Subversion-related ability and body-training foundations descend through Kelyra and should be linked to Archeon without defining all mechanics here.
- Archeon is politically involved with Drakhal and influenced by Elric/Harbinger threads, but exact alliance and manipulation mechanics remain for later organization/thread reviews.
- Treat older `Transcendence/summoner of spirits` labeling as obsolete.
- Treat old Jalen-centered Archeon notes as suspect/obsolete unless transferred through Kelyra review.
