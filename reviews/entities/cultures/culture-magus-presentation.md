# Magus Status / Presentation Review Packet

## Source Summary

Approved records already establish:

- Presentation is a High Clade legitimacy institution.
- Presented/unPresented status is a core hierarchy pressure point.
- A High Clade that does not produce enough safely Presented magi shows weakness and may invite attack.
- Presenting a magus also publicly exposes that the Clade has a valuable capable asset.
- More Presented magi is generally better only when they are strong and controlled enough to hold their own.
- Shroud manifestation is central to magus legitimacy, Presentation, bloodline status, and battlefield usefulness.
- Until a High Clade child can form and maintain a Shroud, they are dangerous to themself, politically vulnerable, and potentially embarrassing to the family.
- Presentation is not just social introduction; for Maya, it publicly demonstrates that the Rezin line remains strong while concealing enough tactical information to preserve advantage.

Current source and planning add:

- The Presentation ceremony uses High Clade ritual language around heirship, strength, worth, balance, and honoring the Clades.
- A mediator role rotates among High Clades in current manuscript, with Velkor Nyctaris serving at Maya's Presentation.
- The current/possible revision direction includes a power-display component, where Maya may shock the other Clades by showing more raw strength than Niall wanted.
- Sleeveless High Clade fashion symbolizes reliance on one's power.
- Maya's specific speech, fire/ice power display, and challenge to the other Clades belong to `event-maya-presentation` and later scene/outline work.

## Likely Accepted Core

- Presentation is the formal public recognition of a High Clade heir or magus as politically viable, controlled, and useful.
- It is both ritual and power politics: a Clade uses Presentation to prove strength, continuity, and legitimacy to the other High Clades.
- Presentation requires enough Shroud control and magical stability that the candidate can be safely announced.
- Being unPresented is not just being young or private; it can signal danger, weakness, instability, hidden failure, or strategic concealment.
- Presenting someone creates both deterrence and vulnerability: rivals see the Clade's strength, but they also learn that a valuable target exists.
- The cultural pressure around Presentation is severe because Clade status depends on producing magi who can survive, fight, and represent the family.
- The detailed event sequence for Maya's Presentation belongs to `event-maya-presentation`.

## Checkpoint Questions

1. Should Presentation be the formal public recognition that a High Clade heir/magus is strong, controlled, useful, and politically viable?
2. Is reliable Shroud control the main safety/status gate for Presentation, even though hereditary magic quality and family politics also matter?
3. Should unPresented status carry real political risk: possible weakness, hidden instability, strategic concealment, or vulnerability to rivals?
4. Should a power display be part of Presentation generally, or should Maya's major fire/ice display remain specific to her revised event?
5. Should sleeveless High Clade fashion be accepted here as symbolic reliance on one's power, with Niall's jacket/conduit exception deferred to Niall/object mechanics?

## Author Decisions

- Presentation is the formal public recognition that a High Clade heir/magus is strong, controlled, useful, and politically viable.
- Reliable Shroud control is a main safety/status gate.
- The candidate also needs hereditary magic potent and flashy enough that the ceremony's `show your power` component is not embarrassing for the Clade.
- High Clades generally keep unPresented magi hidden because exposed unPresented magi become targets.
- If an unPresented magus cannot make a Shroud yet, even an ordinary assassin with a knife can kill them.
- If an unPresented magus is not developing further, they are an embarrassment and a sign that the Clade produced a magus not powerful enough to be Presented.
- A power display is always part of Presentation.
- Maya's revised fire/ice display is over the top; most Presented magi do not have her level of power and control.
- Maya's display pushes her physically to the limit and cements her as an extreme threat on Niall's level.
- Niall would have warned Maya not to be over the top because he did not want her stepping into that responsibility and danger.
- Sleeveless High Clade fashion is universal here and symbolizes reliance on one's own power.
- Niall would have worn sleeveless High Clade fashion when he was Presented.
- Evana or Caius may comment offhandedly on Niall's military jacket during the pre-Presentation period; exact scene use remains candidate.
