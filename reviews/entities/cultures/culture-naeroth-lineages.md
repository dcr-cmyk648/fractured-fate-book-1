# Naeroth and Non-Human Lineages Review Packet

## Source Summary

Approved Maya and magic records already establish:

- Alara is from Naeroth.
- Alara would have had fully purple eyes.
- Maya has dark eyes with subtle purple rings around the pupil and outer edge of the iris, reflecting dual heritage through Alara.
- Maya receives racist sentiment outside Rezin influence because of her Naeroth / non-human heritage.
- People are afraid to go too far because of Niall and because insulting Maya implies insulting Alara.
- Davian has heard enough racist/cultural claims about Naeroth demi-humans to worry that his attraction to Maya might be unnatural or magically compelled.
- Naeroth lineages are a rare exception to the dominant High Clade cultural assumption that hereditary magic and Shrouds belong only to humans.
- Naeroth heritage can rarely grant fateweaving and Convergence heat transfer.
- Alara had fateweaving, Convergence-associated abilities, and a Shroud.
- Maya has emerging fate/Skein perception through Naeroth heritage, but exact mechanics remain unresolved.

Open source/candidate material adds:

- Other non-human or demi-human groups may exist, possibly with subtle physical traits and extensive human interbreeding.
- Outside humans and Naeroth, other non-human lineages may generally not manifest magic.
- Selwin may have an unusual mixed-heritage ability combination.
- Non-human lineage is generally low-status, except Naeroth have a special position because they can rarely manifest magic.
- Demi-human peoples may be disproportionately used in mercenary forces and death-payment systems.

## Likely Accepted Core

- Keep this record focused on Naeroth lineage and its relationship to High Clade assumptions, Maya, Alara, and fate/Convergence exceptions.
- Alara is Naeroth; Maya and Selwin are mixed Rezin/Naeroth descendants.
- Naeroth are non-human or demi-human enough to trigger prejudice in High Clade society.
- Naeroth are exceptional because they can rarely produce Shrouds and hereditary magic-adjacent abilities despite the dominant human-only assumption.
- Alara had a Shroud plus fateweaving and Convergence heat transfer.
- Maya's purple eye rings and fate/Skein perception are tied to Naeroth heritage at the high level.
- Exact mechanics of fateweaving, Convergence, Shroud inheritance, and Selwin's unusual ability should remain deferred.
- Broader demi-human taxonomy should remain candidate material for `culture-demi-human-peoples`.

## Candidate / Deferred Material

- Exact Naeroth origin, homeland, culture, social organization, language, and isolationism.
- Whether Naeroth should be called demi-human, non-human, lineage, people, or another in-world term.
- Exact physical traits beyond purple eyes / Maya's purple eye rings.
- Exact Fateweaving / Convergence / Shroud inheritance mechanics.
- Exact relationship between Alara's abilities, Maya's abilities, and Selwin's Dissolution.
- Other non-human groups, including dwarf/orc-like candidate peoples, subtle traits, number of races, and their social roles.
- Demi-human mercenary forces and death-payment economics.
- Exact racist rumors about Alara bewitching Niall and Davian hearing those claims.

## Author Decisions

- This record should focus narrowly on Naeroth lineage, leaving broader demi-human peoples/races for `culture-demi-human-peoples`.
- Alara is Naeroth.
- Maya and Selwin are mixed Rezin/Naeroth descendants.
- Naeroth are a rare exception to the dominant human-only magic assumption because they can produce Shrouds and rare fateweaving / Convergence-associated abilities.
- Alara's accepted high-level ability package is Shroud + fateweaving + Convergence heat transfer.
- Exact Alara, fateweaving, and Convergence mechanics remain deferred.
- Maya's purple eye rings and emerging fate/Skein perception stay tied to Naeroth heritage at the high level.
- Selwin's mixed-heritage and Dissolution implications remain deferred.
