# Prologue Revision: Maya Shroud Attack

This is approved revision direction, not drafted manuscript prose.

## Accepted Direction

Partway through the prologue, Maya is attacked by a Drakhal-affiliated magus who is far away and whom she cannot see directly.

Maya reacts frantically to this attack. Her reaction causes widespread destruction that affects Davian's combat.

After combat is completely over, Maya should have some dissociation, flashbacks, or numbness. This should be barely shown in the actual prologue and is more likely to come up in later chapters.

This framing should make Maya's first real combat and imperfect Shroud reflex clearer, without turning the prologue into a full explanation of her trauma or magic system.

## Boundaries

- Do not draft or alter prologue prose until manuscript revision work is explicitly authorized.
- Do not resolve the larger Drakhal plot mechanics here.
- Do not treat this as a full prologue outline.
