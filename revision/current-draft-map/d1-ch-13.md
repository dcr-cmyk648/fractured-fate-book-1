- Maya exits the vision breathless and feels her room as smaller/duller than the memory's clarity and purpose.
- Maya thinks about young Niall's smile and the staggering beauty/precision of his power.
- She goes to the training courtyard.
- Maya finds Selwin trying to train by focusing on a pebble.
- Selwin says maybe Niall does not want to play anymore because Selwin should focus on getting stronger so he can protect people too.
- Maya starts to reassure him but sees too much of herself and Niall in his frustrated determination.
- Maya tells Selwin focus is hard, that trying/failing/trying again is part of it, and that the trick is choosing attention rather than pushing everything away.
- Selwin settles and tries again more calmly.
- Maya tells Erynn she felt clarity and purpose in Alara.
- Maya says she needs something that reminds her why all this matters and why she matters.
- Erynn says to find it, even if it is just a pebble.
- Maya reflects on the courtyard as a place to train herself and the guards around High Clade magic.
- Maya asks Erynn what Niall's strategic battle weakness is.
- Maya identifies that Niall cannot be everywhere and that distributed attacks exploited that limitation.
- Maya begins theorizing how she might move faster, be wherever needed, and stop every breach.
- Erynn challenges the impossible control logic.
- Maya cites the memory of young Niall stopping a war alone and says she is his daughter.
- Erynn says Maya does not have to become Niall.
- Maya insists she should be able to, because it is in her blood and she cannot have limits.
