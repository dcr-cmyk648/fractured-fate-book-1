## Actual Events

- Maya walks the citadel halls after reviewing a newly appointed supervisor's proposed worker assignments and shift rotation.
- She notes the supervisor's plan is rough but serviceable.
- She returns to her quarters and sees that Alden/Aldira has delivered the requested decryption and codex-reference books.
- The ornate chest at the foot of her bed contains Alara's journal.
- Maya opens the journal and finds a faintly shimmering inscription/key inside the front cover.
- Maya questions whether the inscription was there before or whether she missed it because she was exhausted.
- The inscription feels like a guide for opening the memories rather than a normal cipher.
- Maya recites the phrase and is pulled into a memory.
- In the memory, Maya experiences heat, noise, battle, and Alara's body as though she is inside Alara's perspective.
- Alara confronts an attacking force and a Low Clade magus with earth-shaping magic.
- Alara freezes the incoming earth wave and burns through the opposing magus's Shroud.
- Alara declares that Elarion's children will not take her home while she lives.
- Maya returns to her room shaken, with smoke curling from her fingertips and foreign pride/joy/purpose lingering.
- She considers opening another page but chooses sleep.
- Erynn wakes Maya at dawn.
- Erynn presents a formal tiara that Niall has sent, one day late, which Maya reads as a possible apology.
- Maya has breakfast with Niall.
- Maya asks why Alara came to Elarion.
- Niall asks why Maya is asking and then tells her not to pursue it.
- Maya says he cannot avoid talking about Alara forever.
- Niall says Maya reminds him of Alara and then shuts down the conversation.
- Maya later tells Erynn she found her mother's journal and that it contains memories.
- Maya explains that the memory showed Alara fighting against Elarion and that it felt right, purposeful, and meaningful.
- Erynn dryly asks whether Maya is planning to go rogue and attack other High Clades.
- Maya says she is tired of games, political posturing, and power for power's sake.
- Maya meets with five zänwright supervisors.
- She explains that the Treasury/vault wards are interlocking defensive systems tied to Registry aura identification.
- She explains passive alerts, active lethal responses, risks, hazard pay, and the right to annul the contract and leave.
- She says Group One will begin with the Treasury overhaul, then the results will be evaluated before proceeding to the vault.
- She says the Treasury wards are older but structurally similar to the vault wards, while the vault wards are more dangerous.
- She limits work hours, requires daily updates, and confirms exit searches.
- After the meeting, Maya tells Erynn she will do key vault-ward work herself and quietly identify any workers competent enough to assist.
