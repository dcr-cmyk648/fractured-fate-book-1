- The text says "seven citadels" from the sky. Accepted Six Cities geometry has the central Spire plus six High Clade citadel towers; later cleanup should avoid implying seven High Clade citadels.
- The chapter identifies "Masked Clades" in dialogue. Accepted culture record treats this as criminal/unofficial terminology, which is compatible if character POV language stays clear.
- The phrase "fate as inevitable as gravity" is emotionally apt but should not imply deterministic fate as objective truth; accepted theme is anti-trauma-determinism.
- This chapter should not imply Antithesis has a unique fate-locating power. Maya's fateweaving should be the operative system; the dagger is an anchor/object link.
