- Erynn asks what happened and whether Maya is hurt.

- Erynn frames Maya's action as saving Rezin soldiers.

- Tarran agrees to talk.
- Alden/Aldira appears to handle interrogation.
- Maya leaves feeling the weight of political decisions before Presentation.
