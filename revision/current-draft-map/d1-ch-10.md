## Actual Events

- Davian wakes after a restless night and avoids breakfast to escape small talk.
- He overhears that supervisors are meeting with Maya and the rest of the workers have time before work begins.
- Davian scouts the Rezin citadel grounds, noting walls, raised walkways, outbuildings, practice yards, storage, barracks, and the central spire.
- He studies the spire's defensive design and remembers the Registry route below ground.
- He guesses the vault/Treasury is likely higher in the spire.
- He considers unauthorized scouting inside the spire but rejects the risk because work will eventually bring him there.
- He returns to his room and reviews a zänwright manual, aware his cover has knowledge gaps.
- Davian decides he needs to work near someone talkative and distracted enough to cover for him.
- He recognizes that Tanelle fits that need.
- Davian enters the contractor meeting outbuilding and joins Tanelle, Kolvar, and Jessa.
- Tanelle gushes about the citadel, spire, walkways, wards, and project.
- Five supervisors enter.
- Marek introduces himself as Group One supervisor and begins explaining the Treasury-ward project from schematics.
- Davian finds the schematics technically useful but not useful as a layout map.
- Tanelle asks about the vault.
- Marek explains that the vault wards are deeper/more dangerous and that the group will start with the Treasury wards before moving inward.
- Marek divides workers into teams.
- Davian positions himself next to Tanelle so he can rely on her chatter and expertise.
- Marek leads the group into the spire through guarded double doors and up a long spiral staircase.
- Tanelle talks through the climb despite growing breathless.
- The group reaches the Treasury chamber, a huge domed, zän-lit space containing a massive floating stone sphere.
- Current prose shows a dark doorway cut into the sphere's surface.
- Marek identifies the sphere as the Treasury itself.
- Marek demonstrates the chamber's weightlessness by jumping with a rope and floating.
- Current prose says the Registry can enable/disable weightlessness and target individuals or objects.
- Marek explains ropes, anchors, slow movement, and avoiding uncontrolled drifting.
- Davian adapts to weightlessness quickly.
- Tanelle overdoes it and Davian redirects her twice before she crashes.
- Davian uses Tanelle's movement as cover to float close to the sphere.
- Davian feels a faint Heart-like pulse or call from within the sphere.
- Marek calls everyone back down and begins real assignments.
- Davian and Tanelle are assigned to work on the sphere.
- Tanelle proves much more technically skilled than Davian expected and teaches while talking.
- Maya enters the chamber, using small flame bursts to move gracefully through the weightlessness.
- Davian interprets her movement and inspection as flaunting power and distrusting workers.
- Tanelle gushes at Maya.
- Davian stares at Maya, remembering her on the wall, and feels a confused physical reaction alongside hatred of her family.
- Maya notices him staring; Davian realizes he has been glaring and stiffly bows.
- Davian notices Niall below near Marek and another supervisor.
- Seeing Niall and the sphere together sharpens Davian's interest in the sphere as the likely route to whatever the Rezins most want protected.
- Current prose has Davian ask Tanelle how many zä would need to go inactive before the sphere fell, but accepted revision direction removes this early "drop the sphere" thought because it no longer becomes relevant and is too obviously suspicious.
- Accepted revision direction instead has Davian focus on access questions: what is stored in the sphere, how anyone gets inside, what the workers are actually allowed to know, and whether the surface zä/lattice work reveals anything useful.
- The workers can acknowledge that the sphere's inner contents and access system are far beyond their purview/pay grade; they are there to re-etch zä and maintain assigned lattices, not solve the Treasury's true architecture.
