## Actual Events

- Davian leaves the supervisor-room aftermath in a state of narrowed lethal intent.
- He steals/borrows a cloak near the barracks because he has no time to return to his quarters.
- He follows the scorched Registry path toward the gate.
- A guard recognizes him as Arrek and asks whether he is heading out.
- Davian lies that he is getting a drink.
- The guard invites him to the Inn of the Brass Moon with others, then misreads Davian's hooded tension as sexual rather than violent intent.
- Davian tracks Caius by guessing that a High Clade magus walking with an active Shroud will create deference and open space through the city.
- He cuts through alleys and finds Caius on the Spoke.
- Davian recognizes a blade merchant as an underworld/Veiled-Clade-connected vendor.
- He signs that he needs a mask immediately.
- The vendor recognizes the danger, warns that Davian will die and others will die, and gives him a mask only after Davian insists he will act regardless.
- Davian pays three copper-haloed gold coins.
- Caius continues through the city in a visible way, drawing attention.
- Davian follows with Antithesis under the cloak.
- He imagines using focused kinesis/Antithesis precision to puncture Caius's Shroud and kill him.
- Caius turns down a route that is not the fastest way to Drakhal holdings and not a good show-off route.
- Davian notices this but shoves the question aside.
- He puts on a black ceramic mask from the vendor.
- The mask sharpens him into movement and intent.
- Davian attacks Caius from behind with Antithesis, aiming under the jaw.
- Caius turns with delight rather than surprise, showing he expected/recognized the attack.
- Caius raises force magic and Davian barely redirects with Antithesis/kinesis.
- Caius releases a pressure blast that damages the street and throws bystanders.
- Davian splits enough of the blast to survive but is still hurled backward.
- Caius laughs with joy at the destruction.
- Caius says he held back because he did not want to kill the Shade before everyone got a look and because he wants self-defense optics.
- Caius frames the Shade's Low/Masked Clade killings as useful culling that looks bad politically.
- Davian realizes this was bait and that he gave Caius exactly what he wanted.
- Davian's Ink Pact pressure rises, pushing him toward another attack.
- Seeing injured/dead civilians breaks the narrow dissociative kill focus.
- Davian recognizes people are dead because he gave Caius the excuse.
- Caius accuses Davian of jealousy and wanting to kill someone stronger while pretending to be a hero.
- Maya arrives with fire/heat and frost, striking Caius and forcing him to defend.
- Caius complains about his damaged vest.
- Maya says she realized Caius was up to something because his public walk was too much effort even for him.
- Maya says she did not think it would be this bad.
- Caius compares the civilian harm to Maya's and Niall's battlefield killing.
- Maya distinguishes armed soldiers from civilians and says the only reason High Clades have the right to rule is if they protect people.
- Maya tells Caius that if he remembers Presentation, he should remember she held back.
- Caius is humiliated/frightened but exits by pretending to worry about his vest.
- Davian nearly thinks about attacking again.
- Maya stops him, says she could take him in for what he did or nearly did, but saw him hesitate and knows he does not want anyone else to die.
- Davian signs "Thank you" under his cloak and leaves before Maya can question him.
- Davian slams Antithesis into a wall repeatedly in an alley.
- He realizes he was played and that civilians died, not Caius.
- He acknowledges he had not even hesitated until bodies started falling.
- He wonders why Maya saved and spared him.
- Maya's action challenges his belief that High Clades do not protect people like him.
- He sees Maya as magnificent: powerful, controlled, protective, and beautiful.
- He hides the black ceramic mask behind a loose stone before returning to the citadel.
- Harvick is waiting alone at the gate.
- Harvick says he heard Davian/Arrek left alone and waited to say hi.
- Harvick assumes Davian was paying off his cousins' debt contracts and does not expose him.
- Harvick tells him to let someone watch his back next time.
- Davian asks why Harvick helps him.
- Harvick says Davian reminds him of himself when younger, has potential, and gives Harvick something to do.
- Davian says everyone is determined to help him and warns Harvick he will let him down.
- Harvick says that may be Davian's choice, but helping Davian is Harvick's.
- Davian returns to his room, checks the Ink Pact/aura lines that maintain Arrek's cover, and senses the Arrek signature fading.
- He recognizes he killed Arrek and stole his name/life.
- He wishes he could really be Arrek: trusted, respected, with a place, friends, and a path toward something better.
- The chapter ends on the Ink Pact caring only what Davian owes.
