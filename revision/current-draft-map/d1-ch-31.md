## Actual Events

- Davian wakes in the Concord manor the morning after the Heart theft, physically restored but wrong all over.
- His Ink has faded to dark thinning veins and no longer gives him clear certainty, erasure, or easy movement.
- Daylight strips away the dark-thread certainty and leaves Davian with memory, guilt, and self-justifications that sound false to him.
- Davian remembers standing over Niall with Antithesis, Maya stopping his arm, the Heart's alien cold, the sphere attack, Harvick, Niall surviving, and betraying Maya.
- He identifies the pressure he acted under but also begins to feel that "I did what they told me" is not enough.
- Davian leaves the room to find the manor quiet and partly abandoned.
- In the dining/meeting area he finds a cipher note from `Ka`, pinned with a knife.
- The note says Archeon has his parents, the Harbinger deal went wrong, his brother and sister are safe, Archeon will arrive today, he should leave now, and he should burn the note.
- Davian burns the note and notices the knife's carved Concord-cipher good-luck message.
- Archeon magi Nire and Gyre blow through the dining hall doors.
- Nire and Gyre reveal through dialogue that Brann is dead, that Presented Archeon magi have higher status, and that unPresented or lower-tier Archeon magi resent being hidden.
- Davian tries to access the Ink, but it is distant and unavailable.
- Davian uses Harvick's body-awareness lesson to slow his panic and think tactically.
- Davian forms a weak, unstable Shroud and recognizes that he lacks training to sustain Shroud use and speed of thought at the same time.
- Nire attacks; Davian tries full Shroud defense and is thrown hard.
- Davian baits Nire with talk about unPresented Archeon magi and begins trying to make his Shroud work with the combat instincts he already has.
- Davian uses Shroud-backed martial timing to survive Nire's force and turn the exchange into a punch/knife strike.
- The good-luck knife shatters, but Nire is hurt and briefly surprised.
- Davian realizes Nire and Gyre lack Brann's Presented-level speed and cognitive response.
- Davian thinks he is going to die or be taken because the dark thread brought him here, then recognizes the dark thread did not force him; he chose to follow it step by step.
- Maya's accusation that he had to plan the theft and Harvick's lesson that he does not have to do anything crystallize the agency realization.
- Davian bluffs confidence, charges, and uses Nire's anger to make her collide with Gyre.
- Davian flees through the Concord manor, using its improvised, maze-like architecture.
- The Archeon magi damage the already unstable manor as they pursue him, and parts of the structure begin to collapse.
- Davian escapes through a rear window but finds more Archeon magi stationed outside.
- He uses Shroud around his foot/legs for improvised propulsion, reaches the roof, and tries to escape over the wall.
- The roof fails under his push-off, killing his upward momentum and dumping him into the courtyard.
- Eight Archeon magi surround him, including Nire and Gyre.
- Davian notices their weaker movement compared to Brann and cheaper/corner-cut gauntlets.
- Davian chooses to keep fighting even though he knows he cannot win.
- Maya arrives from above, slams into Nire, and kills her instantly with heat/flame.
- Davian starts to apologize, but Maya cuts him off, presses Antithesis to his chest, threatens to kill him if he runs again, and tells him to make himself useful.
- Maya gives Antithesis back to Davian.
- Davian admits he cannot fight three Archeon magi because his Ink effects are not available to him the way they were while he was following Elric's dark thread.
- Gyre tries to talk Maya into surrendering Davian to avoid further casualties.
- Maya creates an ice wall / cold barrier and a contained orb of fire or heat energy.
- Davian recognizes she is performing a vastly cleaner version of the force/energy-transfer work that exhausted him.
- Maya gives Davian a five-second lesson: Shrouds work from proprioception/body awareness, and his immediate task is getting the Shroud to work with the martial arts and Ink-altered movement/perception he already has.
- Maya breaks the wall/attacks; Davian uses the pressure of her belief and presence to form a coherent Shroud.
- Davian fights two Archeon magi and initially struggles because Shrouded opponents lack ordinary weak points and recover fast.
- Davian realizes Maya and Niall win by setting up a moment and ending the fight with one decisive strike.
- Davian controls positioning, breaks balance, and uses Shroud-backed martial fundamentals with Antithesis.
- Davian kills one Archeon magus, briefly seeing him as someone who could have been Davian: hidden, striving for legitimacy, and dying unrecognized.
- Davian is hit from behind, loses Antithesis, and nearly collapses.
- Davian wants to give up but sees the remaining magus might join the fight against Maya.
- Davian forces himself up because he still needs to apologize/explain to Maya and cannot let himself be taken.
- Davian fights the second magus in a cleaner state that is explicitly not Ink dissociation but sharper embodied focus.
- He recognizes the Archeon fighters as having neglected fundamentals beneath their magic/technique/reputation stack.
- Davian kills the second magus with Antithesis.
- Maya has already killed the remaining Archeon magi and appears almost impossibly composed.
- Davian asks to go after his parents because Archeon has them.
- Maya tests his Shroud, physically closes in on him in a way he misreads as possible intimacy, knocks him unconscious, and takes him back to the citadel.

## Magic and Worldbuilding Introduced

- Davian's Ink effects should not be treated as a simple fuel meter. Some effects, especially healing, work when Davian is following Elric's dark thread / intended path and do not reliably work when he is not.
- Ink healing is a forcing-to-a-thread function rather than a consumable resource.
- Ink movement through the Skein is less a discrete power than a brain/perception change that lets Davian perceive or take shorter paths through reality; it manifests as speed that looks strange to observers while he remains fully present in observed reality.
- Davian's Shroud use is tied to body awareness/proprioception and must be integrated with whole-body combat reflexes rather than treated as a finicky local-body-part mechanic in this chapter.
- Shroud-to-Shroud contact is framed as a contest where force can be changed, redirected, or resisted.
- Davian's Chapter 31 growth should focus on integrating Shroud with martial arts and existing Ink-altered movement/perception, not on detailed localized-Shroud or kinesis mechanics.
- Davian's accepted broad ability progression is: strong martial arts, then Ink, then Shroud, then later kinesis. The full Archeon combat stack is martial arts + Ink + Shroud + kinesis, and this chapter mainly adds the Shroud component.
- Antithesis can channel or focus redirected force as a schema sink/anchor, but should remain a dagger rather than a special destiny weapon.
- Archeon has hidden unPresented magi and lower-tier fighters who use cheaper or less refined gauntlets.
- Archeon ranking depends partly on Presentation status, Serathis's favor, and usefulness to the project.
- The Concord manor's improvised architecture and poor structural integrity become tactically relevant.
