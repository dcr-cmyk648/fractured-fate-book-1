## Actual Events

- Several days of Treasury work have passed.
- Davian is working without Tanelle nearby and has learned enough from Tanelle and night study to function as a zänwright.
- He studies the Treasury sphere's dense redundancy and recognizes how difficult sabotage would be.
- Current prose still frames the lattice as supporting the sphere's weightless suspension.
- Davian uses Subversion/kinesis against a smaller supporting zä, trying to drain it without triggering alarms.
- The zä resists because surrounding lattice structures feed energy back into the weakened node.
- Davian forces more magic through the system than intended and finally kills one zä.
- He realizes disabling enough zä to make the sphere fall would be too slow and obvious.
- Lunch break begins.
- Davian leaves the Treasury workers because he needs space from Tanelle, the worksite, and his failed plan.
- He descends the spire stairs, thinking about being trapped in the citadel and worrying about Jalen/the Concord.
- Davian accidentally reaches a restricted lower area and is poked by Selwin.
- Selwin introduces himself and asks if Davian is one of Niall's friends.
- Two elite guards flank Selwin and challenge Davian's presence.
- Davian explains that he is a Treasury zänwright who wandered too far during break.
- Selwin insists on talking to him, and the guards defer.
- Davian realizes Selwin is a High Clade scion whose blood alone commands others.
- Davian tries to see Selwin as a future High Clade monster but struggles because Selwin is open and childlike.
- Davian gives the cover name Arrek and says he does not know Selwin's father.
- Selwin says Niall is the strongest and smartest man in the world.
- Selwin's simple faith reminds Davian of Kael.
- Davian crouches to talk to Selwin.
- Selwin says Niall used to say importance comes from protecting the people you love.
- Selwin admits Niall has not said that since he "got sad."
- Selwin tells Davian he can tell Davian is sad too and hopes he feels better.
- Selwin leaves with the guards, and Davian returns upstairs shaken.
- After work, Davian avoids eating with the contractors and goes alone to a training courtyard.
- He thinks about failed Treasury work, being trapped, and worry that someone could challenge Jalen while he is away.
- Harvick appears, jokes about dancing, and tosses Davian a practice sword.
- Harvick attacks immediately and pushes Davian into real sparring.
- Davian is distracted and stiff; Harvick points out he is overthinking and not present.
- Harvick teaches Davian to notice grip, stance, balance, breath, and the current moment instead of trying to solve everything in advance.
- Davian initially mocks the instruction but tries it.
- When he lets go of future-planning and reacts in the moment, he improves dramatically and eventually disarms Harvick.
- Davian panics that he has revealed too much and claims luck.
- Harvick says Davian does not have to hide, names his rough life and hidden burden, and suggests the Rezin guards could give him a permanent place and protection.
- Harvick leaves Davian alone with the weight of that offer.
