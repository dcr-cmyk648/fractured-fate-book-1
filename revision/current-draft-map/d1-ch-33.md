## Actual Events

- Davian wakes in a Rezin underground cell.
- He fixates on footsteps/impact as heartbeat-like signals, preserving the rumination/vibe without needing to make this a major new magus-sense layer.
- Harvick arrives; Davian thinks he should be dead.
- Harvick jokes that he is a ghost, then clarifies he survived through "Lightning. Heart stuff."
- Davian cries because he thought he would never tell Harvick he was sorry and feared Harvick died blaming himself.
- Harvick acknowledges Davian's culpability for killing or nearly killing him while refusing to indulge pure self-condemnation.
- Davian asks what he is supposed to do and how to make up for everything.
- Harvick admits he does not know, refuses to become another person telling Davian what to do, and says Davian needs to choose.
- Davian says the High Clades are people/children and questions what his killings mean if his whole frame was wrong.
- Harvick challenges Davian defining himself entirely by what he has already done.
- Harvick says there is no secret, everything is hard, and getting up is Davian's choice.
- Niall and Maya arrive; Harvick pretends he convinced Davian to cooperate.
- Davian sees Maya and is distracted by memory of their kiss.
- Niall's presence and the Ink trigger a dark-thread imperative to kill Niall.
- Davian resists the thread, tells himself it is his choice, and admits the Harbingers did something to him with Ink.
- Davian offers to help and asks to be trained so he can stand with them.
- Niall dismisses him as untrustworthy, marginally talented, violent, and potentially disposable.
- Davian argues that the Harbingers gave him enough power to steal the Heart and briefly beat Niall, making him the only lead they have.
- Harvick vouches for him; Maya says that is not enough and proposes bringing in Zirene.
- They leave the citadel secretly under cloaks and back routes.
- Zirene greets them with a weapon, jokes about Davian's looks, and expects payment for the broken hatch/ceiling.
- Zirene initially wants to build something that makes Davian stronger; Maya clarifies they need something that lets them trust him.
- Zirene cannot make a truth-forcing device but proposes a device that lets Niall/Maya kill him instantly if he becomes a threat.
- Zirene examines Davian's faded Ink, says it looks like it ran out, then identifies an Ink layer over an Archeon pattern.
- Zirene infers Davian is one of Serathis's descendants and that they need a way to kill him if he learns to use Shroud and magic properly.
- Maya privately tells Davian she will not force him but cannot trust/train him without a control mechanism.
- Davian consents because he wants to stay close to Maya and earn trust again.
- Zirene clears a table and begins measuring/working.
- Zirene privately signs to Davian asking if he really wants this.
- Davian asks how she knows Concord sign; Zirene says the masked codes are all related and based on zän.
- Davian signs that he consents and needs to prove himself.
- Zirene explains miniaturized zä and tooling/vision limits.
- Zirene says a zä means or represents something, and that she must draw Davian's zä to make the device work.
- Zirene connects people, names, auras, identity, and body-attached lattice function.
- Maya asks whether Zirene did this for Niall; Zirene says yes.
- Zirene says the lattice needs to understand the person it is attached to.
- Zirene references newer zä names: the Blade, the Deep, and the Crown.
- Davian asks whether these are city-wedge names; Maya says only two are, and Zirene says the writing system and hand language come from these forms.
- A few hours later, black iron bands are locked around Davian's upper arms.
- Zirene says the bands are anchored to skin like Niall's metals, difficult to remove, and will kill Davian if he tries to remove them.
- Zirene explains the bands do the opposite of what Niall did to Harvick's heart.
- The bands are black iron with circuitry etched into Davian's skin, conceptually adjacent to painful gold strike-line healing and Ink Pact body inscription.
- The bands can be activated by passing electricity through either band or by rapid cooling, with failsafes specific to Niall and Maya.
- Harvick asks why heart-affecting bands are on Davian's arms; Zirene says because it looks cooler.
- On the walk back, Davian and Maya fall behind Niall/Harvick.
- Davian asks whether the bands look cool; Maya lightly says Zirene should not switch careers to clothing.
- Davian talks about his echoing force/footstep perception, but later prose should keep this light or frame it as something natural to him rather than a large new magic-system layer.
- Maya explains that magi have amplified senses for energy aligned with their hereditary magic because they need to identify where to draw energy from.
- Maya says Davian's perception may tell them something about Serathis/Archeon hereditary magic.
- Davian asks how inheritance can prove that when Maya's magic differs from Niall's.
- Maya says she has her mother's magic and Niall did not have a birthing contract.
- Davian realizes his earlier assumptions about Maya and attraction/magic were wrong and apologizes.
- Maya says Alara died long ago and she barely remembers her.
- Davian realizes Maya had to figure out much of her magic alone.
- Maya laughs when Davian implies heat transfer and electricity are similar.
- Maya explains mental carving, energy behavior, Niall's simple/efficient lightning, and her more layered Convergence heat-transfer work.
- Davian recognizes that Maya had to figure this out as a child under Niall's pressure.
- Maya says having Niall as a father made some things easier and others harder, and closes the topic of early Shroud manifestation.
- Davian tells Maya his mother made him learn martial arts young and that the core probably came from Archeon.
- Davian accepts that charging after Serathis was reckless but insists they need to act before something happens to his parents.
- Maya says Archeon wanted Kelyra alive and that rushing without information could put her in more danger.
- Mention of Elric/Maya's grandfather triggers Davian's Ink to urge him toward killing Niall again.
- Davian admits to Maya that since the Heart theft he feels like killing Niall is inevitable, echoing what the Harbinger told him about fate.
- Maya sharply tells him he is still thinking like he has no choice and must admit he had and has choices.
- Harvick drops back, tells Davian Maya is not only a High Clade symbol but a girl he likes, and silently mouths "deal with it."
