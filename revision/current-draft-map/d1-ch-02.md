## Actual Events

- Davian and Jalen leave the Sorevin raid aftermath while the rest of the Concord disperses.
- Jalen sees bruising on Davian's forearms from the fight with the Low Clade magus.
- Jalen says the backlash could have shattered Davian's bones and says Davian should have taken the offered Boon.
- Davian reacts angrily to being lectured about restraint after what the Clades have done to their family.
- Jalen says hate is easy, but revenge without discipline will get Davian and the rest of them killed.
- They reach a Syndicate meeting entrance marked with faint zän.
- A hooded guide leads them through confusing underground passages and old carved zän.
- They emerge into a different hidden meeting place with five hooded figures on a dais.
- Jalen gives the stolen contractor records to the central figure.
- The central figure reviews the documents through magic that does not show an ordinary facial sigil.
- The central figure says the records are not the prize but the key to putting a hired hand into Rezin.
- The figure demands that Jalen wear another worker's name, skin, and aura, infiltrate the Rezin citadel, steal the Heart, and bring it back.
- Jalen objects that the Heart may not exist and that the mission is a death sentence.
- The figure threatens Jalen's position and implies a Trial of Dominion vulnerability.
- Davian panics at the thought of Jalen being sent to die and volunteers himself.
- Davian offers to steal the Heart, kill Niall Rezin, and do whatever is necessary to keep his family safe.
- The figure asks whether Davian will kill Maya too.
- Davian says he will kill whoever he has to if it keeps his family safe.
- The figure reveals an unexpectedly young face and brief radiant geometry across his cheek.
- The current prose triggers an Ink Boon ritual beneath Davian.
- Ink binds Davian, brands him, and gives him a mimic/identity effect originally prepared for Jalen.
- Current prose also applies a second effect to Antithesis, reshaping it from sword to workman's knife.
- Davian loses consciousness.
- Davian wakes or half-wakes while Jalen drags him through the catacombs.
- Jalen refuses to leave Davian behind.
- After reaching the exit, Davian discovers Antithesis is now a small knife.
- Jalen explains that the second Boon lets Davian restore it to full sword form once, after which it cannot switch back.
- Jalen helps Davian limp home.
