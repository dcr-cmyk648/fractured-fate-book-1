# Candidate Material: Selwin Rezin

This file preserves Selwin material that is useful but not fully settled as exact execution.

## Dissolution and Shroud Mechanics

- Exact Dissolution mechanics remain for later magic review.
- Selwin currently cannot form a Shroud at all.
- It may never be possible for Selwin to form a normal Shroud because of his heritage or materially different abilities, but this does not become very relevant in Book 1.
- Selwin's weird combo/crossbreed ability material should be held for later Alara, Naeroth, demi-human, and magic reviews.

## Series-End Role

- Selwin will ultimately be important to the final resolution of the full series.
- His abilities are what Elric originally hoped to breed as a way to kill the extraplanar deity.
- Elric's Ink specter beneath the citadel references this direction when he mentions the arrow of time.
- Do not over-define this in Book 1 entity files yet.

## Family and Alara Material

- Niall subconsciously blames Selwin for Alara's postpartum psychosis, but Niall does not consciously understand this.
- Exact Alara postpartum psychosis, post-birth decline, and family-history details remain for `char-alara` and related event reviews.

## Future-Book Training

- Selwin may want martial or hand-to-hand training because he admires Davian.
- This likely belongs to future-book material rather than the current book.
- If used, it may create social or political tension because High Clade martial training can imply magical weakness.
