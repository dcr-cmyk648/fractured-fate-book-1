## Boundary

Lethira is not Kelyra's child. She is another Archeon child whom Kelyra trained and helped raise in the normal Archeon manner before escaping.

Lethira's accepted Book 1 role is primarily the Archeon arena emotional-manipulation cameo. Do not promote her into an independent subplot unless later outline work explicitly needs that.
