# Maya Rezin Candidate Material

This file preserves useful but not fully approved Maya material. It should not be treated as accepted canon unless later reviewed and promoted.

## Deferred Arc Material

Current candidate direction:

- Maya becomes more flexible and less perfectionistic.
- Maya accepts Niall as flawed instead of needing him to embody perfection.
- Maya learns to use help and ground herself rather than trying to win alone.
- Maya's magical effectiveness may become tied to more efficient use of attention and emotional regulation.
- Maya may defeat or survive Vorren by using Davian-taught martial arts rather than trying to be a perfect High Clade magus who wins through pure magical force.

This direction should wait for later plot review before becoming an approved arc record.

## Superseded or Historical Visual Alternatives

Preserve these as source history, not accepted appearance canon:

- The current manuscript describes Maya with golden eyes.
- A comment anchor describes her as having dark eyes.
- Bullet notes proposed purple eyes.

Approved direction is dark eyes with subtle purple rings around the pupil and outer edge of the iris.
