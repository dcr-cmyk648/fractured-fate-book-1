# Davian Candidate Material

This file preserves useful but not fully approved Davian material. It is not accepted canon unless later reviewed and promoted.

## Late Romance and Death/Return Beats

- Davian may tell Maya, or try to tell Maya, that he has basically been in love with her since seeing her on the wall.
- He may want her to know he sees her as a person now, not as a dangerous goddess or beautiful High Clade symbol.
- Late-book beats include Davian smiling genuinely at Maya before being killed and saying what he wanted to say when he returns.

These belong primarily to `thread-maya-davian-romance` and later raid/climax reviews.

## Magic and Combat Candidates

- Ink/Shroud interaction remains unsettled, including whether Ink must be received before Shroud manifestation and whether Ink-adjacent abilities interrupt Shroud.
- Davian and Kelyra may have tremorsense as an extension of kinesis and martial arts.
- Zirene may etch silver zan lattices into Davian's skin or build further body-linked zanwork.
- Davian using Antithesis to part a force blast may function as a physical image of being the stone in the river.

Route to `magic-ink-boons`, `magic-shroud`, `magic-subversion`, `object-antithesis`, and `object-davian-mask`.

## Family and Intel Candidates

- Davian may have lied or altered intel about the Low Clade magus being out at Sorevin because he wanted to kill him.
- Alternatively, Kelyra may have altered intel, with Davian noticing a pattern.
- Davian returning to his family after the theft should show that their vengeance plan will not bring peace, happiness, or resolution.

Route to `char-kelyra`, `char-jalen`, `org-concord`, and `thread-davian-identity-guilt`.
