# Niall Rezin Candidate Material

This file preserves useful but not fully approved Niall material. It should not be treated as accepted canon unless later reviewed and promoted.

## Late-Book and Arc Candidates

- Exact Vorren fight execution remains unapproved, including Maya's flame guiding Niall, Niall drawing charge from ground or sky, and the final sequence of Niall/Maya/Davian cooperation.
- The experiential "brine" scene, in which Niall struggles through ink/brine and reaches toward Maya and Selwin, remains candidate material.
- Niall giving Davian a sword at the end of the book, with embarrassment about Maya knowing, remains a candidate relationship beat.
- Niall/Serathis dialogue about godhood, Death, Sheol, and fulfillment remains candidate thematic material.

## Magic and Technology Candidates

- Niall's hidden silver zán-strike-line-etched conduits are accepted as technology/modifiers for lightning, but their exact mechanics remain unresolved.
- Heart overcharging of the silver conduits, post-Heart self-charging, charge storage, and underground limitations remain for later magic/object reviews.
- The exact relationship between Niall's lightning, the silver conduits, zán-strike-line technology, and the Seraph Heart should be reviewed during `magic-zan-lattices`, `object-seraph-heart`, or another relevant magic/object review.

## Background Candidates

- Possible older-brother or early-Presentation backstory remains speculative.
- Detailed Alara/Niall history remains for `char-alara` and `event-alara-death-war-memory`.
