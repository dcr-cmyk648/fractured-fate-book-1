# Magus Status / Presentation Candidates

## Resolved by Approved Culture Record

- Presentation is the formal public recognition that a High Clade heir or magus is strong, controlled, useful, and politically viable.
- Reliable Shroud control is a central safety/status gate.
- The candidate also needs hereditary magic potent and flashy enough for the ceremony's `show your power` component.
- High Clades generally keep unPresented magi hidden because exposed unPresented magi are targets.
- If an unPresented magus is not developing toward Presentation, they become an embarrassment and political weakness.
- A power display is always part of Presentation.
- Maya's major fire/ice display is over the top and cements her as an extreme threat on Niall's level.
- Sleeveless High Clade fashion is universal in Presentation-era High Clade culture and symbolizes reliance on one's own power.
- Niall would have worn sleeveless High Clade fashion when he was Presented.
