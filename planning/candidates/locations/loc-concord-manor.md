# Candidate Material: Concord Manor

## District and City Placement

Accepted direction: the manor is in an old, decrepit part of town with ramshackle buildings, smoke, alleys, and surveillance risk.

Deferred:

- exact district name
- whether it is Redbridge-adjacent, Sorevin-adjacent, or in another named slum/district
- exact relationship to major roads, markets, Harbinger routes, and Archeon attack approach

## Former Low Clade History

Accepted direction: the manor was once Low Clade property and was abandoned before the Concord repurposed it.

Deferred:

- exact former Low Clade owner
- whether anyone in-world still knows who owned it
- exact conflict or collapse that left it abandoned
- whether the Concord seized, squatted in, bought, inherited, or otherwise acquired it

## Compound Layout

Accepted direction: the manor/hideout/abandoned house is one primary compound with gates, enclosed grounds, a courtyard, central/family meeting space, maze-like additions, back routes, roof, outer wall, and nearby alleys.

Deferred:

- exact map and floor plan
- exact family/domestic rooms
- exact member/family living quarters
- exact guard positions and escape routes
- exact back-window / narrow-corridor / roof geometry used in the Archeon attack

## Concord Population

Accepted direction: Concord members and any family they have live there communally; this likely includes younger people beyond Kael and Lirien who have not yet been named.

Deferred:

- exact number of residents
- named younger people or families
- how communal versus private the living arrangements are
- how much Kelyra/Jalen's family space is separated from general Concord space

## Compromise, Evacuation, and Collapse

Accepted direction: the manor is compromised after Davian returns with the Heart; Kelyra worries about exposure and goes to the Harbingers hoping for more power. When Kelyra is captured, Karra immediately moves everyone but Davian out. The eventual collapse of the manor during the Archeon attack is canon.

Deferred:

- exact sequence of evacuation
- why Davian remains when others leave
- exact state of the manor between Heart return, Kelyra's capture, evacuation, and Archeon attack
- exact extent of collapse and whether any part is later recoverable
