# Candidate Material: Archeon Location

## Map and City-Wedge

Accepted direction: Archeon controls one of the six High Clade citadels and its attached city-wedge / legally distinct city. The final attack approach goes through slums and neglected city-wedge-boundary areas before entering Archeon territory and moving toward the central tower.

Deferred:

- exact Archeon city-wedge name
- exact map position relative to Rezin, Drakhal, the Spire, Spoke roads, boundary districts, Harbinger tunnels, and Concord manor
- exact outer wall, gate, checkpoint, and public approach layout
- exact prior High Clade occupant of the Archeon citadel

## Citadel Architecture

Accepted direction: all High Clade citadels have central towers. The central Spire/amphitheater is surrounded by the six High Clade citadel towers in a hexagon-like geometry.

Deferred:

- exact Archeon tower design
- exact outer defenses, including whether Archeon has expanded walls, multiple wall rings, or a hard inner keep
- exact relationship between the throne room, arena, training halls, public halls, and lower complex
- exact visual distinction between prior-occupant Creator-worship remnants and later Archeon modifications

## Subterranean Areas

Accepted direction: Kelyra's captivity sequence occurs in lower/subterranean Archeon citadel areas. Many citadels have long-forgotten or recently reclaimed underground zones that are sprawling and poorly characterized.

Deferred:

- exact underground map
- exact relationship to citywide tunnels, catacombs, Harbinger routes, and old citadel foundations
- exact way the throne-room floor trap drops Niall into Vorren's waiting area
- exact access points and who knows them

## Arena, Training Halls, and Breeding Pits

Accepted direction: the internal arena is deep stone with a sand pit, iron rings/grates, torches, and Serathis observing from the lip. Training halls, child compound, and breeding-pit concept are all part of the Archeon citadel/compound.

Deferred:

- exact final terminology for `breeding pits`
- exact layout of training halls, child quarters, institutional child-raising spaces, and testing/punishment rooms
- exact number and status of un-Presented Archeon magi kept inside
- exact guard/healer/messenger routes

## Final Battle and Raid Choreography

Accepted direction: Serathis's final conflict takes place in the central-tower throne room with stained glass Creator-worship remnants. The trap collapses the floor and drops Niall into subterranean areas below, where Vorren waits. The current plan is for all main attackers to approach from aboveground.

Deferred:

- exact approach sequence through the slums
- exact who-fights-who matchups
- exact movement through the citadel to the central tower
- exact throne-room staging, trap mechanism, and fall path
- exact timing of Niall/Vorren separation from the rest of the fight

## Obsolete or Suspect Material

- Treat the older conflict where Archeon was "obviously" in the central tower versus far away as resolved: Archeon has its own central tower inside its own citadel, while the shared central Spire/amphitheater remains a separate central city location.
- Treat the earlier Davian/Harvick underground approach as superseded by the current aboveground approach unless later reapproved for a specific infiltration route.
