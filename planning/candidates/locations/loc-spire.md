# Candidate Material: Spire / Central Amphitheater

## Naming

Accepted direction: in-world characters commonly call the location the Spire or Central Spire. `Presentation Spire` can be casual descriptive speech because Presentations happen there, but it is not the official name.

Deferred:

- exact formal name, if any
- whether different social groups use different names
- how often narration should use `Spire` versus `Central Spire`

## Open-Air Amphitheater Design

Accepted direction: the site is an open-air amphitheater or arena built into an old blast hole at the center of the Six Cities. It is not an intact tower, and old rubble/remnants have been removed.

Accepted direction from later `loc-archeon` review: all six High Clade citadels have central towers, which stand around the central Spire/amphitheater in a hexagon-like geometry.

Deferred:

- exact visual form of the blast-hole amphitheater
- exact sightline/scale relationship between the central Spire/amphitheater and the six surrounding citadel towers
- whether the bowl feels like a polished civic arena, a sunken forum, a crater refined into ceremonial architecture, or another final image
- exact materials, scale, zán lighting, acoustics, and ceremonial engineering
- exact way the site handles weather, shade, sound, and crowd control

## Approach and Security

Accepted direction: plaza/checkpoint/market approach and shared High Clade security survive the revision. The Spire is one of the few places the High Clades truly collaborate.

Deferred:

- exact map from each city-wedge/citadel to the central site
- exact checkpoint, gate, arch, and plaza layout
- exact guard composition and how the High Clades share responsibility
- exact public access rules on ordinary days versus Presentation or emergency meetings

## Presentation Staging

Accepted direction: Maya's Presentation happens in the open-air amphitheater version.

Deferred:

- exact choreography of Maya entering, taking the dais, and displaying power
- exact staging of the vertical fire display in open air
- exact location of Niall, Erynn, guards, mediator, and each High Clade section
- exact floor/map symbolism and zán response

## Obsolete or Revision Material

Treat as obsolete unless later revised into compatible architectural features:

- intact literal gleaming tower
- grand doors into a tower
- vaulted halls and corridors inside a tower
- Presentation chamber atop the spire
- open crown of a tower
- obvious surviving old rubble/remnants dominating the site
- one-off "seven citadels" language
