# Kinesis Candidate Material

This file preserves useful but not fully approved kinesis material. It is not accepted canon unless later reviewed and promoted.

## Subversion Terminology

Accepted direction: `Subversion` is Kelyra's misnomer for kinesis.

Preserve for later revision and character work:

- where Kelyra and Davian naturally use `Subversion`
- when other characters should correct or reinterpret the term
- whether the manuscript should ever use `kinesis` in dialogue or keep it mostly as reference terminology

## Shroud Interaction

The relationship between kinesis and Shrouds remains candidate.

Possibilities to preserve:

- kinesis may consciously interrupt or redirect transformations Shrouds normally automate
- kinesis and Shrouds may be partially antagonistic because one requires conscious redirection and the other relies on automatic sublimation
- Shroud use may protect the user from kinesis backlash without fully integrating with it

Route to later Shroud, magic-combat, Archeon, and Davian training reviews.

## Tremorsense / Kinetic Sensitivity

Kelyra and Davian may be more keyed into kinetic manifestations while using kinesis.

This remains candidate because it may make the magic system too confusing.

## Broader Energy-Transformation Limits

Accepted direction is short-range, primarily kinetic redirection.

Preserve for later:

- whether kinesis can transform non-kinetic energy in limited ways
- whether it can intercept magic generally or only its kinetic/externalized component
- whether it can create stored diffusion or redirective effects in objects

## Antithesis and Zirene Designs

Accepted direction: Antithesis is an Archeon rip-off of Zirene's older/prototypical designs, and Kelyra stole it.

Still unresolved:

- exact object mechanics
- whether it functions as sink, anchor, focus, anti-Shroud tool, or prototype gauntlet technology
- how it relates to Archeon gauntlets and later Zirene technology

Route to `object-antithesis`, `char-zirene`, `magic-zan-lattices`, and Archeon technology reviews.

## Ink / Kinesis / Shroud Integration

Accepted direction: kinesis can combine with Ink Pact movement to allow more outward/aggressive manifestations.

Still unresolved:

- whether kinesis is required to survive or optimize Ink Pact acceleration
- how kinesis, Ink Pact movement, and Shroud maintenance compete for attention
- whether using Ink and kinesis together explains Davian's Treasury injuries
- how Kelyra and Davian's versions differ in practice

## Serathis and the Archeon Stack

Accepted direction: Serathis is a near-perfected Archeon-kinesis user whose total power comes from mastery of the Shroud / martial arts / Ink Pact / kinesis stack.

Preserve for later:

- exact limits of Serathis's kinesis alone
- what aspects of Kelyra's martial arts diverge from stagnant Archeon formal training
- how Kelyra and Davian working together surpass Serathis
