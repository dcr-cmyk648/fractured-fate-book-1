# Fateweaving / Skein Candidate Material

This file preserves useful Fateweaving/Skein material that is not fully approved. It is not accepted canon unless later reviewed and promoted.

## Possible Later Split

The current accepted record remains one umbrella file.

Preserve the possibility of later splitting:

- Naeroth fateweaving
- the Skein as cosmology/reality structure
- Elric's dark-thread trauma-path manipulation
- fate ties as narrative/relationship mechanics

## Source of Elric's Fate Sight

Elric's fate sight is accepted as real, but its source is unresolved.

Candidate contributors:

- Heart communion
- Ink or older Ink systems making fate threads easier to see
- original-sin / first-magus / reality-fracture knowledge
- Elric's research into fate, lineages, cognition, and reality
- some combination of the above

## Ink and Fate Threads

Ink and fate/Skein mechanics overlap, but the exact technical relationship is unresolved.

Preserve for later:

- whether Ink lets Elric see threads more easily
- whether Ink Pact rapid movement uses the same Skein layer Maya perceives
- whether Davian's dark Ink-thread pressure is literally a fate thread, an Ink compulsion visualized as a thread, or both
- whether the Harbinger leader's possession depends on fate-thread access, Ink control, or both

## Heart and Reality Fracture

The accepted file can store original sin / first magus / fractured reality at high level, but exact mechanics remain candidate.

Preserve for later:

- whether the Heart amplifies or damages fate sight
- whether uncovering or misusing the Heart weakens reality
- whether Elric's Heart exposure created his fate sight, worsened it, or only confirmed his beliefs
- how the Heart relates to resurrection and reality damage

## Resurrection Mechanics and Cost

Davian's end-book resurrection is canon, but exact execution remains unresolved.

Candidate questions:

- whether Maya directly rewrites death
- whether she enters the Skein and follows a path back
- whether she tears strands, ties strands, or forces convergence
- what cost lands on Maya, Davian, the Heart, the Skein, or reality
- whether Elric needs the resurrection itself, the tear it creates, or Maya's willingness to cross the limit

## Larger Skein Cosmology

The Skein is accepted as real, but larger cosmology remains candidate.

Preserve:

- beach/shore imagery
- moon/eye imagery
- dark ocean imagery
- threadblights
- whether the Skein is only one layer or one "grain" in a larger reality
- whether larger cosmology belongs in Book 1 or later books

## Fateweaving and Convergence

Fateweaving and Convergence heat transfer are both rare Naeroth-linked abilities, but their exact relationship is unresolved.

Possibilities to preserve:

- related schema styles
- shared dimensional or thread-based cognition
- distinct abilities that Maya happens to approach through similar mental imagery
- a broader Naeroth training foundation that can express in multiple abilities

Route detailed mechanics to `magic-convergence-heat-transfer`, `char-alara`, and Naeroth culture reviews.

## Niall's Fear

Niall treats fate/Skein interaction as dangerous.

Preserve the unresolved question of whether his fear is:

- correct
- partly correct
- shaped by Alara trauma
- shaped by Elric trauma
- projection from grief, rigidity, and control
- some combination

## Determinism Language

Accepted direction: fate sight must preserve agency.

Preserve for revision:

- older deterministic-sounding language may need adjustment
- Elric's voice can still make fate feel inevitable
- characters may experience threads as compulsion or inevitability even when the story's truth preserves choice
