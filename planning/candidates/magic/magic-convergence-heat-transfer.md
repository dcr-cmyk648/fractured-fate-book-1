# Convergence Heat Transfer Candidates

## Deferred Technical Language

The following can remain behind-the-scenes explanatory language unless later chapter work needs it:

- negative conductivity
- entropy channels
- inverted thermal diffusion
- nonlinear radiative loss
- ionization limits
- formal heat-sink/source terminology

## Candidate Techniques

- Maya using Convergence for rapid movement through heat discharge, ice/friction reduction, and Shroud cushioning.
- Body-as-focus or Naeroth stance work quickening close-range Convergence.
- Heat/charge interaction and possible plasma/charge synergy with Niall.
- Extreme short-lived hold-and-discharge techniques.

## Deferred Relationships

- Whether Convergence and Fateweaving are sibling abilities, layered expressions of Naeroth perception, or separate but adjacent phenomena.
- Exact relationship among Convergence, Fateweaving, Shroud access, Maya's emerging Skein perception, and Selwin's Dissolution.
- Exact pre-original-sin natural version of Convergence and how much weaker it would have been.
- Exact details of Alara's Convergence usage.

## Candidate Scene and Prose Use

- Favor carving/surface/conduit descriptions when Maya is consciously using the ability.
- Use thread/ribbon/strand imagery sparingly when it helps show heat flow or links visually to Maya's broader fate perception, without treating the systems as identical.
- Maya's Convergence can mirror her psychology: drawing pressure into herself, trying to hold it perfectly, then releasing it.
