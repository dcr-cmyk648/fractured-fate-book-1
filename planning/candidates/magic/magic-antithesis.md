# Antithesis Boundary Candidates

## Superseded Book 1 Material

- Current manuscript and older notes showing Antithesis as a sword in Book 1 are obsolete.
- Current manuscript and older notes showing Antithesis transforming between knife and sword forms in Book 1 are obsolete.
- Current manuscript implications that Antithesis has unique anti-Shroud properties should not be treated as accepted canon unless later object review re-establishes a non-unique, kinesis-compatible explanation.

## Kinesis Candidates

- The "stone in a river" model remains candidate as an extension of Archeon kinesis: incoming force, violence, or magic may be parted, diffused, or routed around the user rather than stopped directly.
- Weapon-channeling, gauntlet behavior, anchor behavior, and force-buffer technology remain candidate Archeon/Zirene-derived technology material.
- Kelyra, Serathis, or Archeon using kinesis through weapons remains candidate until later Archeon combat or object review.

## Deferred Object and Technology Material

- Exact Antithesis construction, zänwork, material, damage, concealment, and combat behavior belong to `object-antithesis`.
- Exact relationship between Antithesis and Archeon gauntlets belongs to object/technology and Archeon reviews.
- Exact Zirene design lineage belongs to `char-zirene`, `org-archeon`, and technology/object reviews.
- Exact Paradox construction and properties belong to `object-paradox`.

## Not Accepted

- Antithesis is not a standalone magic system.
- Antithesis is not a separate magical principle.
- Antithesis has no accepted direct link to a zän of negation or `nïzä`.
- Antithesis has no accepted unique anti-Shroud behavior.

## Thematic Material

- The line or concept of Maya as the product of the system and the Shade as its antithesis is useful and can be retained for thread/ending planning.
- This thematic use should not be mistaken for an in-world technical category of magic.
