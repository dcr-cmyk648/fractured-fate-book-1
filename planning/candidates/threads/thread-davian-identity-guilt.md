# Davian Identity, Guilt, and Agency Arc Candidates

## Endgame and Relationship Candidates

- Exact Vorren fight execution.
- Davian death/return/confession beats.
- Final relationship resolution with Maya.
- Whether Davian explicitly tells Maya he has loved her since the wall, or whether that remains subtext/near-confession.
- Exact Paradox handoff / Niall-Davian sword beat.

## Family and Consequence Candidates

- Full Kelyra/Jalen family reckoning.
- Arrek-family restitution or cousin consequences.
- Davian returning to his family after the theft should show that vengeance will not bring peace, happiness, or resolution.
- Davian may have lied or altered intel about the Sorevin Head Magus being out because he wanted to kill him.
- Alternatively, Kelyra may have altered intel, with Davian noticing a pattern.

## Magic and Combat Candidates

- Exact mechanics of Elric's Ink/fate manipulation of Davian.
- Exact late Shroud/kinesis/Ink integration.
- Ink/Shroud/kinesis interaction remains unsettled.
- Davian and Kelyra may have tremorsense as an extension of kinesis and martial arts.
- Zirene may etch silver zän lattices into Davian's skin or build further body-linked zänwork.
- Davian using Antithesis to part a force blast may function as a physical image of being the stone in the river.

## Boundary

The approved arc is Davian moving from negation/threat/agency-through-destruction toward values, protection, and preserving what he chooses to care about.

Do not use pressure, manipulation, or Ink influence to erase Davian's culpability; doing so would also erase his agency to make good choices.
