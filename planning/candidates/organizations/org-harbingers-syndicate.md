# Candidate Material: Harbingers / Syndicate

## Leader Details

Accepted high-level facts:

- the leader was once much more minor than he is now
- he was up-and-coming because of tactical genius and an ability to make bargains
- Elric selected him because his trajectory made the takeover convenient
- Elric's Ink Pact allows the slow possession that transforms the Syndicate into the Harbingers

Preserve for later review:

- exact leader name
- exact pre-Ink smuggling ring or syndicate details
- how much of the leader remains by each phase of the book
- whether any subordinates notice the psychological takeover

## Hooded Figures

The five hooded figures from the early meeting can remain canon, but their exact identities and structural role are unresolved.

Possibilities to preserve:

- Archeon-aligned figure
- Vorren
- Drakhal-aligned figure
- other Elric allies
- staged council/front used for intimidation

## Concord Pressure and Trial Mechanics

Accepted direction:

- the threat should now point at Kelyra's position as Concord leader, not Jalen's seat
- Kelyra received an Ink Pact and killed the prior Concord leader in a Trial of Dominion
- the implied danger is that someone else can be granted similar power to challenge her, and eventually is

Preserve for later `org-concord` and revision review:

- exact Concord hierarchy and title structure
- exact Trial of Dominion rules
- who later challenges Kelyra
- what older Jalen-seat scenes need in manuscript revision

## External Coordination

Accepted high-level facts:

- Elric directly coordinates with Drakhal, Vorren, and Archeon through the Harbingers
- the masked figure beside the Harbinger leader during Kelyra's Heart delivery is Vorren
- Elric's identity inside the leader is not generally known, though Serathis may suspect it

Preserve for later:

- what Vorren knows about Elric's identity
- what Evana knows
- what Serathis suspects
- how Drakhal maintains deniability
- whether Harbinger actions are routed through Drakhal, Archeon, or both in specific scenes

## Ink and Boon Mechanics
