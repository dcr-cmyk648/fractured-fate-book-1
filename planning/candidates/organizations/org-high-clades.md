# High Clades Candidate Material

## Names and Magic Labels Needing Review

The current canonical High Clades are Rezin, Archeon, Nyctaris, Drakhal, Cryndor, and Lyranth.

Only Rezin, Archeon, and Drakhal have approved organization files so far. Nyctaris, Cryndor, and Lyranth need later review before leadership, magic, political alignment, and manuscript presentation are treated as fully settled.

Older or candidate magic labels include:

- Incarna for Archeon
- Nocturne for Nyctaris
- Aegistry for Drakhal
- Severance for Cryndor
- Resonance for Lyranth

Archeon and Drakhal approvals already supersede or complicate the older labels, so do not use this list as accepted magic taxonomy.

## Central Spire / Amphitheater Cleanup

Accepted direction: the Central Spire is at the center of the Six Cities, but `Spire` is now a historical reference. It is currently an amphitheater built into a blast hole from a long-ago High Clade war cataclysm.

`loc-spire` approval adds:

- common speech still calls it the Spire / Central Spire
- `Presentation Spire` can be casual descriptive speech but is not the official term
- the old rubble/remnants have been removed
- Maya's Presentation happens in the open-air amphitheater version
- there are six High Clade citadels, with the Spire at the center of the Six Cities

Later map/revision work should decide:

- how the amphitheater looks on page
- how the old blast hole relates to roads, citadels, Presentation, and central arena references
- exact staging, checkpoints, and seating geometry
- which existing manuscript descriptions need revision

## Governance Details

Accepted direction: city-wedges are governed by Low Clade councils and proxy structures reporting to High Clades.

Later culture / location reviews should define:

- Low Clade council composition
- whether each High Clade's city-wedge is legally a separate city
- how city-wedge boundaries, spoke roads, and central districts are named
- how Masked Clades fit into the official and unofficial system
- how Rezin centralization differs from the norm
