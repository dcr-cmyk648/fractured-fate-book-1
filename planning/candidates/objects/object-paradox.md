# Paradox Candidates

## Resolved by Approved Object Record

- `Paradox` is the durable in-world name.
- Maya names Paradox.
- Maya designs Paradox.
- Zirene makes Paradox from the ruined Antithesis.
- Paradox is a normal sword.
- Paradox is not bound to Davian's aura or Arrek's aura.
- Paradox inherits and improves Antithesis's schema-sink/anchor role.
- Paradox has greater mass and surface area than Antithesis, giving it better capacity to absorb, redirect, buffer, or burn off kinesis backlash.
- Returning/thrown behavior and stabilization zä are not accepted.
- Niall gives Paradox to Davian in the end-book courtyard scene.
- The core symbolism is accepted: inherited false-sacred tool remade into Paradox, and Davian moving from knife-in-the-dark toward sword/openly chosen identity.

## Remaining Candidate Details

- Exact forging scene, timing, materials, and technical process.

## Explicitly Candidate / Not Accepted for Book 1

- Greatsword form.
- Shotel-like form.
- Returning weapon behavior like Harvick's sword.
- Thrown-weapon use.
- Stabilization zä for flight.
- Aura binding to Davian.
