# Alara's Journal Candidates

## Accepted Core

- Durable object name: `Alara's journal`.
- Technical type: auratic codex.
- This is Alara's specific auratic codex, separate from Niall's own codex.
- Elric developed auratic codex technology from fateweaving work and provided journals to Alara, Niall, Harvick, and himself under the benign pretext of preserving valued or important memories.
- Maya's discovery sequence is accepted at the high level: dream with inky faceless figure, old tunnel alcove, presence/whisper, hidden stone hollow, and physical book marked `Alara`.
- Alara does not guide Maya. Any perception of Alara guiding Maya is part of Elric's trap.
- The corrupted key enables Elric's bridge.
- Maya hides and uses the codex, experiences idealized/distorted Alara/Niall memories, triggers Elric's bridge, and Niall takes the codex afterward.

## Deferred Details

- Exact maker of the physical codex object, beyond Elric's accepted role in developing and distributing the technology.
- Whether Alara received the journal directly from Elric or indirectly.
- Exact identity and meaning of the footprints at the discovery site.
- Whether Maya sees Elric near her room before the key appears.
- Exact staging of the key's appearance or visibility.
- Exact truth, distortion, selection, and final prose content of each Alara/Niall memory.
- Whether the journal ever returns to Maya after Niall takes it.
- Exact relationship among Alara's journal, Niall's codex, Harvick's codex, and Elric's own codex.
