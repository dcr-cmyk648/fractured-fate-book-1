# Haloes Candidates

## Resolved by Approved Object Record

- Haloes are the main coin currency of the setting.
- Haloes have a precious-metal center and a cheaper-metal halo around that center.
- A central zán marks value from 1 to 6 within a metal tier.
- The central zán can be activated to verify authenticity.
- Values follow base 6: 6 copper = 1 silver, 6 silver = 1 gold.
- A 6-gold halo is worth 36 silver and can be called a Moon because it represents a 36-day month of normal low-level wages.
- Normal low-level labor is about 1 silver halo per day, or about 0.5 to 1 copper per hour.
- Fractional copper coins exist, but they are not haloed because only values worth testing receive the zán authenticity mark.
- Minting is controlled by a High Clade, but not Archeon, Drakhal, or Rezin.
- A Low Clade proxy handles day-to-day minting operations.
- The mask vendor tests Davian's payment because Davian gives him a very large amount of money.
