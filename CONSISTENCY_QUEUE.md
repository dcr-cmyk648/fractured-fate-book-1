# Consistency Queue

System-managed queue for cross-file consistency issues that may require later checking or author judgment.

Do not use this file as a general relationship index. Add only items that may require later consistency review, conflict resolution, or dependent-file updates.

Allowed severity:

- `link-only`
- `minor-summary`
- `possible-conflict`
- `direct-contradiction`
- `broad-retcon`

Allowed status:

- `pending-block-review`
- `resolved-automatically`
- `needs-author-decision`
- `deferred`
- `resolved`
