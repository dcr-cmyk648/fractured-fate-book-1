# 0034: Paradox Name, Maker, Form, and Symbolism

Date: 2026-06-25

Status: accepted; stored in approved `object-paradox` record

## Decision

`Paradox` is the durable in-world name of the weapon made from ruined Antithesis. Maya names it.

Maya designs Paradox, and Zirene makes it from the ruined Antithesis.

Paradox is a normal sword.

Paradox is not bound to Davian's aura and is not explicitly bound against Arrek's aura or identity.

Paradox inherits and improves Antithesis's schema-sink/anchor role. Its larger mass and surface area give it greater capacity to absorb, redirect, buffer, or burn off kinesis backlash.

Returning/thrown-weapon behavior and stabilization zä remain candidate material, not accepted Book 1 object canon.

Niall gives Paradox to Davian in the end-book courtyard scene.

The accepted symbolism is that Antithesis, an inherited false-sacred tool, is remade into Paradox, and Davian moves from knife-in-the-dark toward sword/openly chosen identity.

## Rationale

This preserves the accepted Antithesis-to-Paradox future event while avoiding obsolete Antithesis sword/transformation implications. It gives Paradox a clear object identity and maker chain without prematurely resolving the full end-book emotional sequence.

## Implications

- `object-paradox` owns the remade weapon.
- `object-antithesis` remains the source object and should link to Paradox once the file exists.
- Later ending/outline work should preserve Niall's handoff and the accepted symbolism while deciding exact dialogue and emotional sequence.
- Later Zirene review should preserve that Zirene makes Paradox from Maya's design.
