# Chapter 31 Map and Davian Ability Progression

Date: 2026-06-30

## Status

Accepted current-draft map guidance and future revision direction.

## Decision

Chapter 31's current-draft map is approved with the following clarifications:

- Ink should not be treated as a fuel meter.
- Ink healing works as a forcing-to-a-thread function when Davian is following Elric's dark thread / intended path, not as a consumable reserve.
- Ink movement through the Skein is less a discrete power than a brain/perception change that lets Davian perceive or take shorter paths through reality. To observers, it manifests as speed that looks strange while Davian remains fully present in observed reality.
- Maya can return Antithesis to Davian in this chapter because she used it to find him.
- Davian's ability progression is martial arts first, then Ink, then Shroud, then later kinesis.
- Chapter 31 should focus on Davian making his Shroud work with his martial arts and Ink-altered movement/perception, not on complicated local-Shroud mechanics or early kinesis integration.
- Maya should knock Davian unconscious at the end when he thinks she may kiss him, supporting the next chapter's opening where she drags his unconscious body back to the citadel.

## Rationale

This preserves the chapter's function as Davian's first post-betrayal agency and Shroud-integration chapter without turning Ink into a simple resource, making Davian an instant full magus, or overcomplicating Shroud mechanics.
