Date: 2026-06-27

## Decision

Niall's high-level arc is approved as movement from grief, rigidity, isolation, and self-proving toward accepting help, living with Maya and Selwin, protecting the living, and committing action toward what he still has rather than remaining focused on the past and things he cannot change.
