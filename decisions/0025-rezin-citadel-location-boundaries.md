# 0025: Rezin Citadel Location Boundaries

Date: 2026-06-24

Status: Accepted

## Decision

`loc-rezin-citadel` is the umbrella location record for the whole Rezin citadel. `loc-registry`, `loc-treasury`, and `loc-spire` remain separate sublocation records.

The Rezin citadel is a massive fortress-city complex with walls, towers, bridges, courtyards, old staging areas, and later expansions.

The citadel was designed to shelter the older city-wedge population in wartime, but it cannot house the much larger modern city population.

Contractors and personnel can leave, but are searched and tracked or monitored by Registry/security rather than imprisoned.

Old tunnels beneath the main spire and Elric's hidden chamber are part of the citadel's ancient layer. The exact map is deferred.

The Registry is below ground. The Treasury is high up. No elevators or similar vertical-transport solution are accepted. Specific maps and routes are deferred.

The Rezin citadel has a central spire as an architectural feature, but it is not named or special by itself. The Presentation Spire is a separate location.

## Consequences

- Later Registry and Treasury reviews must preserve the accepted vertical relationship unless explicitly revised.
- `loc-spire` should focus on the separate Presentation Spire location, not treat the Rezin central spire as the same entity.
- Later manuscript cleanup should avoid solving Registry/Treasury access with elevators.
