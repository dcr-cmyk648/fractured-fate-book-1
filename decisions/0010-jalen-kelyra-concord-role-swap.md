# Jalen and Kelyra Concord Role Swap

Date: 2026-06-24

Status: accepted

## Decision

Later planning swapped Jalen and Kelyra's Concord roles.

Kelyra is the de facto primary leader of the Concord. She has magical abilities from Archeon and major martial arts prowess. She used those abilities, along with an Ink Pact, to kill the prior Concord leader and become the primary leader.

Jalen is Kelyra's husband. He primarily acts in a support, planning, and logistics role. He is respected as an intelligent asset, but he is not the martial leader.

Jalen has no magical abilities. His combat contribution is primarily support and ranged weapons.

The broad arc around revenge and survival focus endangering the family now belongs more to Kelyra. Jalen acts as a tempering force but remains anti-Clade because of Selina's death.

Kelyra, not Jalen, uses Archeon training techniques to train Selina and then Davian into killers.

Kelyra canonically fights in the Trial of Dominion. Jalen talks with her beforehand, gives advice, and supports her, but does not participate.

Jalen and Kelyra met after Kelyra escaped Archeon, when Jalen tried to rob her and she broke his arm.

## Rationale

This resolves the role conflict between older notes and later planning by assigning Concord leadership, primary martial prowess, Archeon-derived ability, assassin-training responsibility, and the Trial of Dominion fight to Kelyra. Jalen remains important as husband, father, planner, support figure, and anti-Clade emotional force without duplicating Kelyra's role.
