# Decision 0006: Kelyra Archeon Lineage, Shroud, and Family

Date: 2026-06-23

Status: accepted; stored in approved `char-kelyra` record

## Decision

Kelyra is directly descended from Archeon breeding programs. The exact number of generations between Kelyra and Serathis remains unclear.

Kelyra's Shroud is blocked by trauma. She knows it is there, but the idea that she must suppress it to escape Archeon is deeply ingrained. Even thinking about manifesting a Shroud is very unpleasant for her.

Kelyra's broad arc should parallel Davian's movement from negation toward values: she must move from acting negatively by running away from Archeon toward acting positively to protect her family.

Kelyra's accepted children are Davian, Kael, Lirien, and Selina, deceased. Lethira was likely an Archeon child of another parent whom Kelyra helped raise before escaping Archeon, but that needs later checking.

Kelyra's talents are strong Archeon hereditary kinesis, which she calls Subversion, and genius martial art talent. These are linked to speed of mind, but the speed-of-thought aspect should be shown rather than codified or quantified in this draft.

## Rationale

The accepted record needs to preserve Kelyra's Archeon trauma and family-protection arc without prematurely resolving magic mechanics, Lethira's parentage, or exact Serathis lineage distance.

## Implications

- Kelyra's accepted file can state trauma-blocked Shroud status.
- Kelyra's `Subversion` terminology should be treated as her misnomer for Archeon hereditary kinesis until magic terminology is reviewed.
- Later reviews should reconcile `Lirien` with later source text that uses `Lirin`.
- Later `char-lethira`, `org-archeon`, `magic-subversion`, and `magic-shroud` reviews should use this decision.
