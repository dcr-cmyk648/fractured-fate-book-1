# Decision 0007: Elric Rezin Remnant and Reality/Skein Role

Date: 2026-06-23

Status: accepted

## Decision

Elric Rezin is the hidden primary cause of almost everything that happens in Book 1, although this remains hidden from the main characters until essentially the very end.

Niall destroyed Elric's original body, but Elric is still alive by taking over the mind and body of the Harbinger leader. The leader received an Ink Pact from Elric before the book begins. After Elric's body was destroyed, Elric slowly used the pact to take over the leader of the Syndicate criminal organization and transform it into the Harbingers.

By the start of the book, the Harbinger leader is almost completely controlled by Elric. The takeover completes at the end of the book after Maya enters the Skein to resurrect Davian.

Elric sees fate strands tied to people acting out trauma or negative impulses and uses them to further his goals.

Elric's fall is the end result of idealism, perfectionism, and rigidity being twisted until he becomes psychotically depressed. He originally thought he could reform society, then learned that purity/perfection was impossible and that reality was fractured after the original sin of the first magus. He came to see suffering as the ultimate state of existence and chose negation.

Store an Elric reality/Skein endgame arc seed now. The events of Book 1 happen against the backdrop of Elric's manipulations toward his endgame.

Store Harvick's relationship to Elric in the Elric record. Harvick's persistence at helping people overcome past traumas is the main fly in the ointment of Elric's plan, rooted in Harvick's self-blame at being unable to help Elric's depression despite loving and admiring him.

## Rationale

This decision makes Elric's hidden antagonist role durable before later organization, Ink, Heart, and Skein reviews. It also preserves the distinction between Davian's agency and Elric's manipulation: Elric leans on trauma and circumstances, but approved character arcs still depend on meaningful choices.
