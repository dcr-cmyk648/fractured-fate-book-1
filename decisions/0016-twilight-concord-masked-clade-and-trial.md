# Decision 0016: Twilight Concord, Masked Clades, and Trial of Dominion

Date: 2026-06-24

Status: accepted; stored in approved `org-concord` record

## Decision

The formal name is the Twilight Concord. `Concord` is the short form.

The Twilight Concord is a Masked Clade. Masked Clades are mafia-esque criminal organizations, though groups such as the Concord may see themselves as resistance groups against the High Clade class structure.

High Clades consider the term `Masked Clade` a joke. They find it funny, but also wrong, that people without high-caliber magic would call themselves a Clade.

Kelyra took Jalen's surname after marriage in the hope that it would help disguise her from Archeon, so she is canonically Kelyra Virellis.

Kelyra's Concord role is probably called `boss` or `leader` in the less rigidly structured Masked Clade system rather than a formal High-Clade-style title.

The Trial of Dominion is the way the Concord and other Masked Clades determine their leader.

Karra challenges Kelyra to the Trial of Dominion. This is also part of the Harbinger punishment for failing to kill Niall and part of one of Elric's plans.

## Rationale

This resolves the old Jalen-seat material in favor of Kelyra's accepted leadership role and establishes the Concord's place in the broader Masked Clade system without overdefining detailed Trial mechanics before the relevant character, culture, and revision reviews.

## Implications

- `org-concord` owns the Twilight Concord identity, leadership, and high-level Trial of Dominion role.
- `culture-clade-hierarchy` or a Masked Clade culture record should own broader Masked Clade social meaning.
- `char-kelyra` owns Kelyra's personal history and leadership implications.
- `char-karra` should own Karra's role in the Trial challenge.
- Older manuscript references to Jalen's seat or Jalen as Trial participant are suspect and need revision planning.
