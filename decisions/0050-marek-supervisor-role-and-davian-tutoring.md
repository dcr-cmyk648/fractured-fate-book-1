# Marek Supervisor Role and Davian Tutoring Function

Date: 2026-06-26

Status: accepted; stored in approved `char-marek` record

## Decision

Marek is a brief-depth but dedicated supporting character, not folded into the generic contractor group.

He is the Group One zänwright supervisor for the Rezin citadel Treasury restoration project. He is practical, clipped, dry, competent, efficient, and work-focused.

Sorevin vetting specifically selected some supervisors because they were solid and dependable without being brilliant zänwrights. Marek fits that profile.

Davian's promotion under the Arrek cover is a real promotion. He receives it because he saves Selwin and because the project needs another supervisor to help Marek. Marek probably had been asking Maya for another supervisor, but Maya had deferred the issue because of other work.

Davian then relies on Marek's tutoring, plans, schematics, and practical supervisory competence to function in the role. Davian likely offers Marek part of the supervisor bonus in exchange, and Davian may over-read Marek's judgment of him.

Marek does not know the Heart or true Treasury secret. He is not smart enough to infer it. He received and accepted the official weightlessness explanation from Maya, who also does not know the secret. The accepted truth remains that Treasury weightlessness comes from the Heart interacting with its containment armature.

## Deferred

- Exact background, age, family, and prior work history.
- Exact degree of suspicion toward Davian/Arrek.
- Exact payment terms with Davian.
- Exact relationships with Tanelle, Kolvar, Jessa, and other workers.
- Exact post-Heart-theft fate.
- Whether Marek's candidate line about Davian probably not being fired survives.

## Rationale

This keeps the Treasury worksite credible without overbuilding Marek. He gives Davian's promotion practical consequences, provides a believable support mechanism for Davian's supervisory cover, and preserves a worker-level realist voice around Rezin power.
