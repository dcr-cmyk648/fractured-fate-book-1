# 0031: Sorevin Manor and Redbridge Candidate

Date: 2026-06-25

Status: Accepted

## Decision

Redbridge remains candidate-only location material.

Sorevin is a Low Clade that acts as one of the Rezin proxy administrators. Sorevin primarily vets contractors and handles other contracts, along with other local tasks around the Sorevin manor.

The Sorevin manor is the Low Clade manor from the prologue. The magus Davian fights there is the Sorevin Head Magus.

The Sorevin job was the specific Concord operation to retrieve contractor records. The later Arrek / Rezin-citadel contractor infiltration is an add-on from the Harbingers.

Redbridge's exact physical form remains deferred. It is probably a district named for a landmark.

Redbridge is not Sorevin-associated territory in accepted canon; it is just where Arrek happened to drink.

The Sorevins were likely not completely wiped out, but the loss of their Head Magus / only significant magic user along with most personnel accomplishes essentially the same plot function.

Arrek's fate and other details remain for his eventual record and other more plot-relevant files.

## Consequences

- Later prologue revision should identify the Low Clade manor as Sorevin manor and the Low Clade magus as the Sorevin Head Magus.
- Later Low Clade / caste-system work should preserve Sorevin as a Rezin proxy administrator and contractor-vetting house.
- Later map work should keep Redbridge flexible and not treat it as Sorevin territory unless separately approved.
- Later Arrek work should own his final fate and replacement details.
