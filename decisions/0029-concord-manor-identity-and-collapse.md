# 0029: Concord Manor Identity and Collapse

Date: 2026-06-25

Status: Accepted

## Decision

The Concord manor is the Concord's hideout. References to the Concord manor, Concord hideout, and abandoned house all point to the same primary compound.

The manor is a formerly abandoned Low Clade manor in an old, decrepit part of town. Exact prior owner and exact abandoned-conflict history remain deferred and may be unknown in-world.

The manor is both the Concord operational base and the communal home/base for Concord members and any family they have. This likely means younger people besides Kael and Lirien live there, even if they have not been mentioned yet.

Selina's shrine is in the family meeting space.

The maze-like additions and questionable structural integrity are accepted features of the manor.

After Davian returns with the Heart, Kelyra is worried the manor is compromised. She does not have a solid plan beyond going to the Harbingers and hoping for more power.

When Kelyra is captured, Karra immediately moves everyone but Davian out.

The eventual collapse of the manor during the Archeon attack is canon.

## Consequences

- Later revision should treat the manor/hideout/abandoned-house language as one compound unless a separate safehouse is explicitly introduced.
- Later Concord population work can include unnamed families and younger people living at the manor.
- Later Archeon-attack revision should preserve the manor collapse.
- Later map work should keep exact district name, former owner, floor plan, evacuation sequence, and damage state flexible.
