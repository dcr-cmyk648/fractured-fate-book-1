# Tanelle Zánwright Competence and Worker-Space Function

Date: 2026-06-26

## Status

Accepted.

## Decision

Tanelle is a standard-depth recurring supporting character, not folded into a generic contractor group.

She is enthusiastic, talkative, socially forward, starstruck by High Clades, and genuinely kind enough to pull Davian into worker belonging.

She is unusually skilled and pattern-obsessed at zánwright work. She notices the Treasury sphere's containment-like lattice behavior and loves hands-on etching more than supervising.

Tanelle was likely hired "accidentally." The Rezin contract specifically wanted reliable but not highly talented zánwrights for Treasury work because Niall did not want workers inspecting the containment sphere and realizing something was unusual. Tanelle may not have had glowing references from prior jobs because supervisors viewed her as annoying, so Sorevin vetters would not have known how talented she was.

Tanelle does not know the Heart or full Treasury secret. She does not guess the truth, but she is the only worker who suspects anything unusual beyond the baseline oddity of a giant floating sphere.

## Deferred

- Root-chewing habit.
- Exact background, training, age, and prior references.
- Whether she recognizes Antithesis's zán as expensive or unusual.
- Exact post-Heart-theft fate.
- Later Maya/Davian observer use.
