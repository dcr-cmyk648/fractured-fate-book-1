# Karra Concord Challenge and Evacuation Role

Date: 2026-06-26

Status: accepted; stored in approved `char-karra` record

## Decision

Karra receives a dedicated standard-depth character record. She is not very deep, but she needs a persistent record across the book because her role connects the Sorevin job, Heart handoff, Trial of Dominion, Ink Pact healing, and Concord manor evacuation.

Karra is a young, powerful Concord bruiser/operator. Davian saved her life during the Sorevin job, giving her a personal debt to him. During the Heart aftermath she distrusts Kelyra's direction and becomes the Harbinger-backed Trial challenger.

Her motive for taking Ink and challenging Kelyra is a mix of:

- ambition
- fear about Kelyra spiraling into dangerous and unrewarding missions that seem more related to revenge and destruction than Concord gain
- intentional Harbinger feeding of both ambition and fear

Karra has deeper forced-restructuring Ink healing. Kelyra does not.

After the Trial, everyone in the Concord respects the rules enough and is frightened enough after Davian's explosive reappearance to obey Karra. When Kelyra is captured, Karra immediately moves everyone but Davian out of the compromised Concord manor. She likely has to lie to Davian's siblings to get them out.

No significant post-Book-1 or endgame role is currently planned for Karra. A possible later-book return, especially because Kelyra as the new head of Archeon would likely not be pleased with her, remains candidate material.

## Rationale

Karra is most useful as a practical, understandable internal challenger rather than a purely malicious traitor. Her ambition and fear make the Harbinger manipulation credible, and her post-Trial evacuation role keeps the Concord fallout functional after Kelyra's capture.
