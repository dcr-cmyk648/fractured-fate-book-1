# Decision 0005: Niall Grief Arc and Lightning Conduits

Date: 2026-06-23

Status: accepted; stored in approved `char-niall` record

## Decision

Niall's broad grief/recovery arc seed is accepted now. His grief state is clearly shown in the prologue as affecting his actions, and it is a main factor shaping his interactions throughout the book.

Use neutral event language for Niall killing retreating enemies after they have broken. Do not label the action as a massacre or atrocity in the accepted record. In battle contexts, such actions may remain morally gray when tactically sound and in the context of war, especially because mercenary armies are often full of people without other options who sign up in exchange for death money paid to their families.

Niall wears hidden silver zan-strike-line-etched conduits throughout the book. This is why he constantly wears a military jacket despite accepted High Clade sleeveless fashion. The conduits are technology or modifiers for his lightning ability, not a separate innate ability.

Alara material in Niall's character record should stay minimal for now: widower status, grief, misrecognition, and emotional wound. Detailed Alara history belongs in later `char-alara` and `event-alara-death-war-memory` reviews.

## Rationale

The accepted Niall record needs enough information to support future character and continuity work without prematurely resolving detailed Alara history or magic-system mechanics.

## Implications

- Niall's accepted file can store his grief/recovery arc as an approved seed.
- `outline/arcs/thread-niall-grief-recovery.md` can be created as an approved-seed arc file.
- Silver conduit mechanics should be queued or preserved for later magic/object review rather than finalized here.
- Later battle and mercenary-worldbuilding reviews should preserve the moral-gray framing around retreating soldiers and death-money mercenary practices.
