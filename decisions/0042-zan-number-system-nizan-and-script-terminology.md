# Zán Number System, Nizán, and Script Terminology

Date: 2026-06-26

Status: accepted

## Decision

Accepted in-world spelling uses accents on the `a`:

- `zán` is the singular/root term for a meaningful magical sign, script element, or component.
- `zá` is the plural for multiple signs/components.
- `zánwright` is the profession term.

The prior `zä` / `zän` / `zänwright` convention is superseded. Unaccented forms such as `zan`, `za`, and `zanwright` may still appear in notes and control-file IDs for ease of typing.

The base-6 number system is culturally central and structurally linked to zán lattice design through the strike-line.

Nizán is accepted at the high level as zero / negation / void. In the written numeric system, Nizán means absolute zero: a value of nothing.

Nizán has no strike-line. It is visually two sets of overlapping parallel lines leaving a diamond-shaped empty central area. When Maya sees the `eye` of the extraplanar deity at the conclusion of Book 1 while resurrecting Davian, it appears as a diamond-shaped eye.

Nizán is not routinely used as a placeholder inside larger base-6 numbers. A value equivalent to decimal 6 would be conceptually equivalent to `10` in base-6 Arabic numerals, but the in-world notation would be closer to a first position marked for value 1 followed by an intentionally blank second position, using either no strike-line continuation or a small notch rather than Nizán.

Monad/monadic terminology is accepted for single-digit numbers and single-handed signs. Dyad/dyadic, triad/triadic, and related terms are accepted for multi-digit numbers and multi-handed signs by count/structure.

## Deferred

- Exact reproduction of the author's digit chart.
- Exact cross-line shapes for values 0-5.
- Exact digit names, two-digit day names, phonemes, dialect variants, hand-shape inventory, and Concord cipher grammar.
- Specific archetypal zán such as the Tyrant.
- Exact cosmological relationship among Nizán, zero, negation, void, Sheol, the extraplanar deity, the Heart, and the Skein.

## Rationale

This resolves a project-wide spelling and plurality conflict while preserving the deeper visual chart and cosmology for later use. It gives enough canon to support haloes, zán lattices, Davian's mask, Concord cipher/sign language, and future manuscript cleanup without requiring a full script reference now.
