# Endgame Archeon Assault and Character Agency

Date: 2026-06-30

## Status

Accepted future endgame architecture.

## Decision

The Book 1 endgame Archeon assault should use this high-level sequence:

1. Jalen is left for dead during Kelyra's capture. Archeon assumes he is dead or irrelevant.
2. Jalen reaches the Rezin citadel essentially dying, collapses near the gate, is dragged in, and receives severe painful gold strike-line healing.
3. Jalen does not physically join the final assault. He becomes a support/logistics/intelligence bridge, giving Rezin and Aldira concrete information about Harbinger-Archeon collaboration, Heart delivery, and criminal-network access.
4. Davian already knows Archeon has Kelyra from Karra's message; Jalen does not need to know Kelyra's exact location.
5. Niall, Maya, and Davian attack Archeon violently from aboveground through the neglected city-wedge/slum boundary and into the citadel.
6. Early resistance can seem oddly weak because Serathis has held back valuable Archeon magi/heirs for the interior trap.
7. Davian splits off under time pressure to rescue Kelyra, fearing Serathis may execute or use her before the main objective can be completed. Serathis can exploit this split because removing Davian leaves one fewer observer for her traps and deceptions.
8. Davian fights the remaining valuable Archeon heir / Presented-magis threat positioned near Kelyra. Kelyra manifests a Shroud and helps him.
9. Maya and Niall reach the central tower/throne room. Niall kills the apparent/fake opposition before Serathis triggers the floor trap and drops him toward Vorren.
10. Serathis and held-back Archeon magi pin Maya in an attrition fight to keep her from reaching Niall until Vorren can kill him. Maya kills many Archeon magi but cannot quickly end the fight or reach Niall alone.
11. Davian and Kelyra enter after the rescue and relieve pressure on Maya. Maya goes down to help Niall.
12. Maya and Niall fight Vorren together while Davian and Kelyra fight Serathis.
13. Davian and Kelyra kill Serathis. Kelyra does not want Davian to go help Maya and Niall, but Davian goes anyway.
14. Davian cannot defeat Vorren alone. He reduces pressure, blocks Vorren's attack, and ruins Antithesis protecting Maya and Niall.
15. Maya opens the ceiling, Niall brings lightning through the opening, and Maya, Niall, and Davian together defeat Vorren.

Exact route choreography, combat choreography, dialogue, timing, and low-level magic mechanics remain deferred for later chapter architecture and detailed outline work.

## Rationale

This resolves the web-app ticket concern that the endgame could over-center Davian and leave Maya with too little meaningful action. The accepted structure gives:

- Jalen a useful support/logistics role without requiring him in the final assault;
- Davian a family-rescue role and a later protective support role;
- Kelyra a Shroud breakthrough and Serathis confrontation;
- Maya an active attrition-trap survival role and a decisive Vorren-fight action;
- Niall a trap/recovery structure where accepting help matters;
- Serathis a coherent interior trap using held-back forces;
- Vorren a final pressure point that requires Maya, Niall, and Davian together.
