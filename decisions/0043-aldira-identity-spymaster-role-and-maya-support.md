# Aldira Identity, Spymaster Role, and Maya Support

Date: 2026-06-26

Status: accepted

## Decision

Aldira is the accepted current character. All old `Alden` references are now references to Aldira.

Aldira is a Rezin inner-circle intelligence, records, and codex specialist with spymaster-like duties. Characters may colloquially refer to her as `spymaster`, but the exact formal office name remains unresolved.

Her main Maya function is a dry, question-based corrective and mentor role around control, self-blame, Niall, Davian, and bright-thread / fate overwhelm.

It is also accepted that Aldira actively tries to trust and support Maya while Maya is still growing into her role.

## Deferred

- Exact formal office title.
- Exact information-network organization.
- Exact age, origin, family, and training path.
- Exact knowledge boundaries around Niall's silver conduits, Zirene, Elric, and hidden Rezin history.
- Manuscript cleanup of old `Alden` references.

## Rationale

This resolves the Alden/Aldira continuity conflict and gives Aldira enough stable function for future outlining without overdefining her backstory or office structure. It preserves her as a reusable Rezin analyst and Maya-support figure while keeping formal hierarchy and biography flexible.
