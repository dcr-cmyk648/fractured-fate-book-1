# Convergence Heat Transfer Model

Date: 2026-06-27

Status: accepted; stored in approved `magic-convergence-heat-transfer` record

## Decision

Convergence heat transfer is accepted as a rare Naerothi-linked ability associated with Alara and Maya.

Convergence manipulates heat flow rather than creating heat from nothing. Cold effects generally come from heat being removed from an area or object.

Convergence is affected by the same reality distortion that affects Clade hereditary magic, allowing it to be much more powerful than it would have been before the original sin damaged reality.

Maya receives her Shroud ability from Niall, along with some of the mental abilities that let her be so powerful and engage reality directly. Maya receives her Naerothi Convergence inheritance through Alara.

Maya's working model is accepted at high level: she perceives heat/cold overlays and uses schemas, mental circuits, carvings, surfaces, and conduits to gather, compress, constrain, redirect, hold, transfer, and release heat in useful battle patterns.

The preferred on-page descriptions should lean toward carving-based language and surfaces. Thread, ribbon, strand, or channel imagery can remain visually useful, but should not imply Convergence is identical to Fateweaving.

High-level limitations are accepted: distance, complexity, containment surface, and high-energy concentration increase cognitive and technical load. Extreme heat is unstable and should usually behave as a brief hold-and-discharge effect rather than indefinite storage.

## Deferred

- Formal thermodynamic language and exact physical model.
- Body-as-focus / Naeroth stance techniques.
- Heat/charge or plasma synergy with Niall.
- Exact Fateweaving / Convergence / Selwin Dissolution relationship.
- Exact pre-original-sin natural version of Convergence.
- Exact Alara combat usage and terminology.
