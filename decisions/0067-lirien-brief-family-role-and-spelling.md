# Lirien Brief Family Role and Spelling

Date: 2026-06-27

Status: accepted

## Decision

Lirien receives a brief dedicated character record.

Accepted spelling is `Lirien`. `Lirin` is stale manuscript/source spelling and should be treated as cleanup material for later manuscript or chapter-map work.

Lirien is Kelyra and Jalen's living child, Davian's younger sister, and Kael's sibling. In Book 1, her role is family-stakes and domestic texture beside Kael, with Lirien more composed and serious than Kael.

Exact age, nickname, knowledge state, evacuation scene, magic/training, and future-book role remain candidate or deferred.

## Rationale

Lirien is not currently deep enough to need a major independent arc, but she has enough continuity value to deserve a durable record. She helps make Davian's family stakes specific and preserves the useful sibling contrast with Kael.
