# Magus Presentation Status, Power Display, and Fashion

Date: 2026-06-25

Status: accepted

## Decision

Presentation is the formal public recognition that a High Clade heir or magus is strong, controlled, useful, and politically viable.

Reliable Shroud control is a central safety/status gate, and the candidate must also have hereditary magic potent and flashy enough that the ceremony's `show your power` component is not embarrassing for the Clade.

High Clades generally keep unPresented magi hidden. If exposed, unPresented magi become targets, especially if they cannot yet make a Shroud. If they are not developing toward Presentation, they are an embarrassment and a sign of weakness.

A power display is always part of Presentation. Maya's revised fire/ice display is over the top, pushes her physically to the limit, and cements her as an extreme threat to other Clades on Niall's level. Niall would have told Maya not to be over the top because he did not want her stepping into that responsibility and danger.

Sleeveless High Clade fashion is universal in this context and symbolizes reliance on one's own power. Niall would have worn that style when he was Presented. His current military jacket is an exception tied to his hidden conduits and later object/magic mechanics.

## Deferred

- Exact ritual script, mediator rules, rotation rules, and ceremony variants.
- Exact power-display format and acceptable display levels.
- Exact consequences for failed, delayed, or premature Presentation.
- Exact Maya Presentation speech, choreography, exhaustion, and fallout.
- Evana or Caius possibly commenting on Niall's military jacket.
- Niall older-brother / early-Presentation candidate backstory.

## Rationale

This keeps Presentation as a durable culture/status institution while leaving Maya's specific ceremony and prose-facing spectacle to the event review. It also clarifies why Presented/unPresented status matters for Clade politics without overdefining every ritual detail.
