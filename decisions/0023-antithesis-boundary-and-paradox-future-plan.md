# 0023: Antithesis Boundary and Paradox Future Plan

Date: 2026-06-24

Status: Accepted

## Decision

Antithesis is not an independent kind of magic or a separate magical principle.

In Book 1, Antithesis refers only to Davian's knife. It remains a knife throughout Book 1. Older transformation or sword-form Book 1 material is obsolete.

Antithesis is an Archeon rip-off of Zirene's older/prototypical designs, stolen by Kelyra. It can be effective while still being crude compared with Zirene's intended work.

Force-parting, diffusion, or "stone in a river" behavior remains candidate material for an extension of Archeon kinesis, not something special about the knife itself.

Antithesis has no accepted direct link to a zä of negation or `nïza`, and no accepted unique anti-Shroud behavior.

Paradox will canonically be made from the ruined Antithesis at the end of Book 1. Exact object mechanics belong to `object-antithesis`, `object-paradox`, and later ending/outline reviews.

The thematic language of Maya as the product of the system and the Shade as its antithesis is approved as useful thread material, but it does not create a separate Antithesis magic system.

## Consequences

- `magic-antithesis` should be treated as a boundary record, not as a normal magic-system file.
- Object details remain with `object-antithesis`.
- Paradox details remain with `object-paradox`.
- Archeon force-parting or diffusion belongs to kinesis/Archeon combat candidates.
- Later manuscript cleanup must remove Book 1 Antithesis transformation and sword-form material.
