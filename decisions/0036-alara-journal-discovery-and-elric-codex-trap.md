# 0036: Alara Journal Discovery and Elric Codex Trap

Date: 2026-06-25

Status: Accepted

## Decision

The durable object record is `Alara's journal`. Its technical type is auratic codex.

Alara's journal is Alara's specific auratic codex, separate from Niall's own codex / memory book.

Elric developed auratic codex technology from his forays into fateweaving and provided journals to everyone known to have one: Alara, Niall, Harvick, and himself. He presented them as benign ways to preserve valued or important memories.

Maya's discovery sequence is accepted at the high level: dream with an inky faceless figure, old tunnel alcove, presence/whisper, hidden stone hollow, and physical book marked `Alara`.

Alara does not guide Maya. Any perception that Alara guides Maya is part of Elric's trap.

The discovery/key/bridge is Elric-linked trap activity. The corrupted key enables Elric's bridge.

The Book 1 object function and sequence are accepted: Maya hides and uses the codex, experiences idealized/distorted Alara/Niall memories, triggers Elric's bridge, and Niall takes the codex afterward.

## Deferred

The following remain candidate/deferred: who originally made or physically gave Alara the codex, who made the footprints, exact memory truth and distortion, key-appearance staging, whether Maya sees Elric near her room before the key appears, whether the codex returns to Maya, and the exact relationship among Alara's journal, Niall's codex, Harvick's codex, and Elric's own codex.
