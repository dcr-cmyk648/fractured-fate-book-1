## Decision

Approve the four story-direction items from web-app ticket `WC-2026-06-29-004`.

1. Kelyra may challenge Davian after he wants to go help Maya, framing his hope of coming out of the shadows into the light with Maya as dangerous or false. This supports Kelyra as a possible later antagonist toward Maya.
2. Maya had some administrative familiarity before Presentation, but Presentation makes that role newly public-facing and politically consequential.
3. Sorevin had no children capable of forming Shrouds and none who could be Presented. Sorevin survivors may exist, but Rezin can route surviving personnel into another proxy Low Clade and designate that house as the new contract handler rather than simply elevating a Sorevin heir.
4. Niall may warn Maya after Presentation that she has no idea of the political ramifications of what she did.

## Rationale

These items clarify later character pressure, Rezin public-role development, Low Clade proxy succession mechanics, and Presentation fallout. They are accepted direction but still leave exact scene placement, wording, and chapter architecture for later outline work.
