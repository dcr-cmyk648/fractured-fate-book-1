# 0030: Archeon Citadel, Subterranean Complex, and Final Tower

Date: 2026-06-25

Status: Accepted

## Decision

`loc-archeon` owns the physical Archeon citadel plus its city-wedge/territory. `org-archeon` owns the Clade, leadership, breeding program, and power stack.

The Archeon citadel is canonically one of the six High Clade citadels, seized from a prior High Clade. The prior occupant is deferred.

Kelyra's arena/chained-chamber/corridor sequence takes place inside the Archeon citadel in lower/subterranean areas. Many citadels have long-forgotten or recently reclaimed subterranean areas that are sprawling and poorly characterized, similar to the Rezin citadel's forgotten underground areas and the Harbinger tunnels beneath the city.

The internal Archeon arena is canon: deep stone, sand pit, iron rings/grates, torches, and Serathis observing from the lip.

Training halls, the child compound, and the breeding-pit concept are all parts of the Archeon citadel/compound. Exact layout and final terminology remain deferred.

Archeon keeps many un-Presented magi and sensitive descendants mostly inside the citadel. Access in and out is heavily restricted, as with most High Clade citadels.

All High Clade citadels have a central tower. The central Spire/amphitheater is surrounded by the six citadel towers of the High Clades, which stand at the points of a hexagon-like geometry around the Six Cities.

Serathis's final conflict will take place in a throne room in Archeon's central tower. The throne room has stained glass windows along the sides, remnants from the prior High Clade occupants' Creator-worship. The room has a trap that collapses the floor and drops Niall into the subterranean areas below the Archeon citadel, where Vorren waits.

The final attack approach will be through slums and neglected city-wedge-boundary areas. Once inside the Archeon citadel, the main characters proceed to the central tower for the battle in and under the throne room.

The current final-attack plan is for all main attackers to approach from aboveground.

## Consequences

- Later Six Cities map work should show the central Spire/amphitheater surrounded by six High Clade citadel towers in hexagon-like geometry.
- Later Archeon revision work should preserve the aboveground approach, central-tower throne room, Creator-worship stained glass remnants, floor-collapse trap, and Niall/Vorren subterranean separation.
- Later location and culture work can define exact prior High Clade occupant, tower design, city-wedge name, boundary slums, underground layout, and final `breeding pits` terminology.
- The old "Archeon central tower versus far away" issue is resolved by distinguishing the shared central Spire/amphitheater from the central tower inside Archeon's own citadel.
