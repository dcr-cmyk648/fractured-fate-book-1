# Selina Death, Family Myth, and Object Links

Date: 2026-06-26

Status: accepted; stored in approved `char-selina` record

## Decision

Selina receives a dedicated standard-depth character record because she will probably matter in later books and already affects multiple Book 1 arcs.

Accepted core:

- Selina is Kelyra and Jalen's deceased child and Davian's sibling.
- Kelyra ran from Archeon while pregnant with Selina because Archeon would take children from mothers and train them rather than raise them.
- Kelyra trained Selina before Davian.
- Selina's death drives Kelyra's Ink bargain, Concord takeover, and false family mythology.

Selina's death is accepted at high level: Selina went off to kill Vorren because of Kelyra's anti-Clade/destiny framing and was killed by overwhelming High Clade/Archeon-level power.

Davian does not know the truth. Kelyra tells Davian that Selina left to work for the High Clades, passed her knife to him as a keepsake because she did not want to fight and kill anymore, and was later killed by Vorren for no reason. In reality, Selina was already dead by the time Kelyra later tells Davian about Vorren.

Object links:

- Davian's original mask is not Selina's.
- The later Nizän-marked Concord mask Kelyra gives Davian to encourage him to kill Niall is Selina's old Concord mask with the Nizän added.
- Selina used Antithesis before Davian.
- No other Selina keepsake is currently necessary.

Kelyra's internal framing is that Davian could be the weapon Selina was not. Kelyra believes her mistake was letting Selina get away on her own to attack Vorren too early, instead of training her more and making a better plan to kill him.

## Rationale

This preserves Selina as a person-shaped wound and hidden-truth engine rather than a vague dead sibling. It also clarifies which object links are accepted now and which exact event details remain for later outline/revision work.
