# Clade Hierarchy Categories and Presented Status

Date: 2026-06-25

Status: accepted

## Decision

The only real formal Clade categories needed for Book 1 are High Clades and Low Clades.

Masked Clades are criminal, unofficial, underworld, or revolutionary terminology rather than a legally equivalent category. High Clades treat `Masked Clade` as mockery, but Masked Clades are still buying into Clade language and might-makes-right culture even when they see themselves as revolutionary.

Finer social distinctions should generally be handled through family names and reputation rather than new formal tiers. Everyone can know the Rezins and Drakhals are especially powerful without needing a separate tier label.

High Clade status is formally defined by control of one of the six citadels / city-wedges. Archeon is the accepted example of violent Low-to-High mobility.

Low Clades are subordinate formal houses that can serve as councils, proxies, contractors, administrators, and mint managers under High Clade authority. Low Clades have Shroud-producing users of hereditary magic.

People outside High or Low Clade structures have little access to important formal social roles. If they have no magic or no protected Clade affiliation, they generally either subordinate themselves to Clade authority or become criminals.

Presented/unPresented status is important enough to include in the hierarchy record. A High Clade that does not produce enough safely Presented magi shows weakness and may invite attack from another Clade, High or Low, if it controls something worth taking. Presenting a magus also exposes that asset publicly. More Presented magi is generally better only if they are strong and controlled enough to hold their own.

## Deferred

- Exact Low Clade council structures and titles.
- Exact legal status of Masked Clades.
- Exact birth-contract rules and Presentation ritual details.
- Exact city-wedge maps, slums, boundary districts, and Spoke road geography.
- Exact High Clade / Low Clade minting authority for Haloes.

## Rationale

This keeps the hierarchy useful for the story bible without inventing a complicated caste taxonomy. The record captures the power logic that matters for plot, politics, and character pressure while routing detailed Presentation, city-map, and economy questions to later owner files.
