# Decision 0002: Naeroth Demi-Human Worldbuilding

Date: 2026-06-23

Status: accepted direction; pending later entity/world-system reviews

## Decision

Naeroth demi-humans are an exception to the broader cultural assumption that only humans have access to hereditary magic and Shrouds.

Naeroth heritage can rarely grant access to fateweaving and Convergence heat transfer. Alara had both fateweaving/Convergence heat transfer and a Shroud.

High Clade culture includes racist anti-demi-human sentiment. Maya should receive racist sentiment due to her dual heritage from people outside the Rezin sphere of influence, though people are afraid to go too far because Niall might kill them, especially because of the implied insult to Maya's mother.

Davian should have heard enough about Naeroth demi-humans to wonder whether his attraction to Maya is somehow unnatural or magically forced.

## Rationale

The author is shifting the worldbuilding toward more plot-relevant demi-human races and toward making anti-demi-human prejudice part of the bad/racist High Clade culture.

## Implications

- This decision affects `char-maya`, `char-alara`, `culture-naeroth-lineages`, `culture-clade-hierarchy`, `magic-fateweaving-skein`, and likely `thread-maya-davian-romance`.
- Other demi-human names, origins, abilities, and social roles still need development.
- Demi-humans may be part of disposable mercenary forces, including attacks on the Rezins in exchange for death money paid to their families.

## Open follow-up

- Develop other demi-human groups, names, origins, and social impact.
- Clarify how hereditary magic, Shrouds, fateweaving, and Convergence interact in non-human and mixed-heritage people.
