# Davian Identity, Guilt, and Agency Arc

Date: 2026-06-27

Status: accepted

## Decision

Davian's high-level arc is approved as movement from negation, threat, and agency-through-destruction toward values, protection, and preserving what he chooses to care about.

His culpability remains central despite Harbinger, Elric, Kelyra, and Ink pressure, especially for Arrek's murder, the Heart theft, lying to Maya, harming Harvick, and nearly killing Niall.

Absolving Davian from culpability for bad decisions would also absolve him from having real agency to make good or bad decisions. The arc should preserve that his choices matter.

The Maya/Davian romance remains both real and betrayed. The relationship matters, but it does not erase the damage he caused.

Exact Vorren fight execution, death/resurrection mechanics, confession/final relationship beats, Paradox handoff, Arrek-family restitution, and late magic integration remain candidate/deferred rather than blocking outline readiness.
