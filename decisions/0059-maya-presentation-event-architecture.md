# Maya Presentation Event Architecture

Date: 2026-06-27

Status: accepted

## Decision

Maya's Presentation occurs after the Drakhal-backed wall attack. Maya helps defend Rezin, Aldira/Alden reports insufficient-but-real evidence connecting the attack to Drakhal field agents, and Maya then goes to Presentation before all six High Clades.

At Presentation, Maya publicly answers Drakhal's test by appearing as a new Rezin apex threat. Her decision is counter to Niall's instructions. Niall has hoped, unrealistically, that he and Maya can back away from functioning as Rezin deterrents.

The ceremony should mostly survive in revised form: all six High Clades represented, mediator / ritual acknowledgment, High Clade seating around a central dais, and collective acknowledgment, adapted to the open-air amphitheater Spire. Mediator details remain candidate.

The ceremony should include brief ritualistic, self-congratulatory discussion of the first magus, bloodline, and how inherited magical bloodline supposedly gives High Clades the right to rule.

Maya's speech/display is accepted at the conceptual level. She asks what gives High Clades the mandate to rule, recognizes that the room only truly respects power, and performs the over-the-top fire/ice display.

The display's durable canon is high-level for now:

- fire/ice Convergence
- far beyond normal Presentation expectations
- physically exhausting
- politically Niall-level threatening

Exact choreography and wording remain deferred.

Niall may or may not be proud, but he is definitely worried. Maya receives that worry and does not understand why he acts this way when she believes she did what was right. Because Niall withholds the information she would need to understand his worry, she may interpret his reaction as a fault on her end.

Caius becomes jealous and obsessed. He gets a toxic/narcissistic version of Davian's wall reaction: Davian sees Maya and admires her, while Caius feels threatened and belittled.

Drakhal already planned to move against Rezin and already had some idea of Maya because of the attack they sponsored. This event gives them additional ammunition for backroom conversations with the other High Clades: if Rezin continues unchecked, Rezin will keep lording power over everyone and could decide to kill another Clade on a whim.

## Consequences

- Create approved `bible/events/event-maya-presentation.md`.
- Preserve exact speech, choreography, ritual script, mediator mechanics, and detailed fallout in `planning/candidates/events/event-maya-presentation.md`.
- Update direct dependents for Maya, Niall, Caius, Drakhal, Presentation culture, the Spire, and High Clade politics.
- Later chapter architecture should treat this event as a triumph/trap: Maya succeeds politically by performing the most dangerous version of the Rezin role.
