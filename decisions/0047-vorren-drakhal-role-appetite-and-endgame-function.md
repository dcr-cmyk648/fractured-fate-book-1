# Vorren Drakhal Role, Appetite, and Endgame Function

Date: 2026-06-26

Status: accepted

## Decision

Vorren is a deep structural character and major final-act antagonist, even though he also serves a generic "evil last boss" role.

Vorren is Caius Drakhal's older brother. He was publicly banished from Drakhal at Niall's insistence, which made Caius heir. He secretly remains useful to Drakhal-aligned interests as a disavowable weapon and is likely the strongest current Drakhal combatant.

Vorren is fulfilled by destruction, dominance, and killing powerful people. He is not a second Elric and does not need a reform, despair, or nihilism ideology. He is too experience-focused to care about philosophical problems. His main regret in dying is that he will die before experiencing everything.

Vorren may say, correctly or incorrectly, that he was called `Vorren` to give him an endless desire to devour.

Vorren waits below the Archeon throne-room trap, becomes Heart-empowered, and is killed by Maya, Niall, and Davian together. Exact choreography remains deferred.
