# Chapter 36 Map, Harvick Prototype Ink, and Selwin Training

Date: 2026-06-30

Status: accepted current-draft map guidance

## Decision

The `d1-ch-36` current-draft chapter map is approved.

The main chapter purpose is practical Davian training, Harvick Ink reveal, Davian's ability-stack focus, lattice/team realization, and Selwin apology/shared Shroud training.

Harvick's explanation is accepted at the high level: he has a prototype Elric Ink Pact with no voice and no command pressure.

Davian should already be able to do faster/right movement because that is the basic ability provided by Ink. Harvick's reveal is not that the ability exists, but that Harvick has a prototype Ink Pact at all.

Harvick's prototype can explain why his Pact is not exactly the same as Davian's or Kelyra's and why it does not let him move as fast. It may be designed for a normal human without a Shroud, making it safer if used appropriately, while newer Ink Pacts are riskier to the body and intended to operate with a Shroud or Archeon stack.

Karra can be injured by newer Ink-Pact movement and healed by her deeper healing Pact. Kelyra can operate on the edge of what would ruin her body because of experience.

The current-draft "Ink left / ocean dried up" wording should likely be removed or revised later in favor of the newer basic Ink-power model.

The Shroud training model is approved at the broad level: body-awareness/proprioception, openness under pressure, similar baseline capacity, and throughput/sublimation as practical strength.

Davian's apology to Selwin and Selwin's "not as sad / you care" beat are acceptable for now for the Davian-Selwin mirror.
