# Decision 0001: Maya's Naeroth Heritage and Appearance

Date: 2026-06-23

Status: accepted; stored in approved `char-maya` record

## Decision

Maya has dark eyes with subtle purple rings around the pupil and outer edge of the iris.

Maya's mother, Alara, is from Naeroth and would have had fully purple eyes. Maya's appearance reflects dual heritage rather than a simple human High Clade presentation.

Maya's recurring Presented-magus visual direction includes a high ponytail with a rose-gold ring and an expensive sleeveless magus battle outfit with rose-gold accents and boots.

## Rationale

The author chose this to support a broader shift toward making demi-human peoples more interesting and plot-relevant, and to connect Maya visibly to Alara and Naeroth.

## Implications

- Supersedes earlier candidate options of fully golden, fully purple, or simply dark eyes for Maya.
- Current manuscript references to golden eyes should be treated as revision targets, not accepted physical canon.
- Store the magus outfit as recurring intended presentation/role attire rather than a one-off garment.
