# Naeroth Lineage and Magic Exception

Date: 2026-06-25

Status: accepted

## Decision

The Naeroth lineage record should focus narrowly on Naeroth material. Broader demi-human peoples, race taxonomy, mercenary roles, and social structures belong to `culture-demi-human-peoples`.

Alara is Naeroth. Maya and Selwin are mixed Rezin/Naeroth descendants.

Naeroth are a rare exception to the dominant High Clade cultural assumption that Shrouds and hereditary magic belong only to humans. Naeroth can produce Shrouds and rare fateweaving / Convergence-associated abilities.

Alara's accepted high-level ability package is:

- Shroud
- fateweaving
- Convergence heat transfer

Maya's purple eye rings and emerging fate/Skein perception are tied to Naeroth heritage at the high level.

Selwin's mixed-heritage and Dissolution implications remain deferred.

## Deferred

- Exact Naeroth culture, homeland, language, physical traits, and in-world terminology.
- Exact Fateweaving / Convergence / Shroud inheritance mechanics.
- Exact relationship among Alara's abilities, Maya's abilities, and Selwin's Dissolution.
- Broader demi-human peoples, race taxonomy, mercenary roles, and death-payment economics.
- Exact anti-Naeroth rumors and scene-level prejudice details.

## Rationale

This preserves the story-relevant lineage facts for Maya, Alara, and Selwin while avoiding premature demi-human taxonomy or magic-system overdefinition. The mechanics and broader peoples are already queued under more specific owner files.
