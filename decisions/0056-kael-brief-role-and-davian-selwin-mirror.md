# Kael Brief Role and Davian-Selwin Mirror

Date: 2026-06-26

Status: accepted; stored in approved `char-kael` record

## Decision

Kael receives a brief dedicated character record rather than being folded into a grouped "younger Virelli siblings" file.

Accepted core:

- Kael is Davian's younger brother and one of Kelyra and Jalen's living children.
- Kael lives in the Concord manor family space with Lirien and the wider Concord household.
- Kael is a trusting, eager, wooden-sword-coded child who idolizes Davian.
- Kael sharpens Davian's guilt because he assumes Davian's missions are right while Davian knows the reality is darker.
- The Kael/Selwin mirror is accepted as a durable Davian-arc function: Selwin reminds Davian of Kael, making Selwin harder to dehumanize as only a future High Clade enemy.
- Kael should stay mostly a family-stakes pressure point for Book 1. Any independent future-book role remains candidate material.

## Rationale

Kael's useful Book 1 function is focused and consequential: he gives Davian's protective motive a living face, exposes the moral cost of Davian's self-mythology, and strengthens the Selwin comparison. He does not need a deep independent subplot during the initial story-bible pass.
