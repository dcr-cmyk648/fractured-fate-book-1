# 0035: Davian Mask Zan Marks and Shade Symbolism

Date: 2026-06-25

Status: accepted; stored in approved `object-davian-mask` record

## Decision

Davian's durable mask is a matte black Concord / Masked Clade mask.

The mask always has a zan mark on it. At the start of Book 1, Davian has carved the zan for equality / equilibrium into it. Kelyra criticizes this mark in the revised direction.

Later, when Kelyra wants Davian to return to the Rezin citadel, take the Heart, and kill Niall, she gives him a Concord mask with the Nizan on it as a toxic supportive gesture.

The Nizan is the zan of zero / negation / void. It is made with two sets of overlapping parallel lines, leaving a diamond-shaped empty section in the middle.

The prologue mask break/shatter is deferred to later prologue revision.

Kelyra gives the later Nizan-marked mask back to Davian, or gives him a different new Concord mask with the Nizan. Which version it is remains unresolved.

Selina would have worn a Concord mask, but whether Davian's mask is hers remains unresolved.

Exact Treasury theft / Niall confrontation mask timing remains for later revision. Current likely direction is that Davian puts it on after Maya confronts him as he is about to kill Niall.

The core symbolism is accepted: the mask represents wanting to be seen but not known, the Shade persona, and Davian's movement from performative notoriety toward an integrated identity.

## Terminology Note

The author raised a possible broader terminology change: `zan` may become singular and `za` plural, because `Nizan` sounds better. Current approved magic records still use `zä` singular and `zän` plural. This should be resolved in later terminology/culture review before broad replacement.

## Implications

- The older five-scratch mask design is not the durable object concept unless later revision deliberately reuses it.
- The revised prologue should route mask criticism through Kelyra, not Jalen.
- The Nizan-marked mask should function as a concrete object expression of Kelyra's destructive/toxic encouragement.
- Exact mask scene timing during the Heart theft remains deferred.
