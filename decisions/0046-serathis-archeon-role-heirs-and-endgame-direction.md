# Serathis Archeon Role, Heirs, and Endgame Direction

Date: 2026-06-26

Status: accepted

## Decision

Serathis is a deep structural character and central plot element, not just an organization-facing villain. She is Archeon's Head Magus, the architect of the current Archeon system, Kelyra's ancestor/abuser, and a major final-act antagonist.

Serathis is part of Elric's `A Cord of Six Strands` frame: two intertwined three-stranded threads, Elric-Niall-Maya and Serathis-Kelyra-Davian. Elric reveals this framing only when Maya is about to resurrect Davian.

Serathis is an anomalous near-perfected Archeon stack user. She is High-Clade-level but below Niall/Maya top-end, does not primarily rely on gauntlets, and is ashamed that most descendants need tools. In the endgame, she uses dual returning daggers made by Zirene, returning to her pre-Shroud roots from the release-and-catch program and echoing Kelyra's preference for dual knives.

Serathis is a toxic gaslighting authority figure. She wants to further Archeon and mostly herself, but uses emotional manipulation whenever it is useful. She resents the Clade system because of her prior outsider status, and her secret long-term goal is to kill all High Clade magi and burn the system down. She shares that goal with Kelyra as Serathis dies.

Serathis intentionally allowed Kelyra's escape, uses the parallel to her own release to gaslight Kelyra, and sees Kelyra and Davian as the closest realized heirs to her project. This is why Serathis is desperate to bring Kelyra back to her side and later capture Davian. Current Archeon heirs may resent this and may want to kill Kelyra or Davian in the final conflict.

The accepted high-level endgame progression is:

1. Davian fights the remaining Presented Archeon magus to free Kelyra.
2. Kelyra manifests a Shroud to break out and help him.
3. Davian and Kelyra together fight and kill Serathis.
4. Kelyra stays to handle the remaining Archeon forces.
5. Davian goes to help Maya and Niall against Vorren.
