# Lethira Archeon Arena Function and Relationship Boundary

Date: 2026-06-27

Status: accepted

## Decision

Lethira receives a brief dedicated character record.

Lethira is not Kelyra's child. She is another Archeon child whom Kelyra trained and helped raise in the normal Archeon manner before escaping.

Her Book 1 function is primarily Serathis-controlled emotional manipulation in the Archeon arena, not an independent subplot.

Serathis's violent disposal of Lethira is accepted as part of the arena manipulation. The exact survival/death wording can remain flexible until chapter revision.

## Rationale

Lethira makes Archeon's child-taking system personal and gives Serathis a living emotional weapon against Kelyra. A brief record is enough to preserve that function without overbuilding a subplot the book does not currently need.
