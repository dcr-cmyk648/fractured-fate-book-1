# Heart Theft Event Architecture

Date: 2026-06-27

Status: accepted

## Decision

The Heart theft event boundary includes the full chain from Harbinger escalation / Davian volunteering through the Treasury breach, Maya's failed interception, Davian's escape, and Kelyra delivering the Heart to the Harbingers.

The Karra penalty / Trial of Dominion sequence remains a later event.

Davian is heavily pressured by family danger, Harbinger/Elric manipulation, Ink/dark-thread compulsion, and dissociation, but the theft remains his responsible choice. At any point he could have chosen something else, such as telling Maya and asking for help. The point is not the specific other options; it is that he doggedly persists without considering alternatives even though he knows it will hurt Maya, and he almost kills Niall.

The accepted Treasury confrontation beats are:

- Davian injures Harvick.
- Niall revives/protects Harvick, though Davian and the reader will not necessarily clearly see this.
- Niall attacks Davian.
- Davian uses Antithesis, kinesis, Ink, and the Treasury sphere's lattice energy to launch the sphere.
- Niall's lightning shatters the sphere.
- Gravity returns.
- Davian takes the Heart.
- Maya stops Davian from killing Niall.

Niall has to stretch himself to his diminished limits after Davian throws the sphere at him in order to protect both himself and Harvick. This leaves Niall unconscious, injured, and exposed.

Maya's event function is accepted: she initially tries to read the disaster as an accident/rescue, realizes Davian stole the Heart, accurately accuses him of sustained choice/lying, stops the Niall killing attempt, chases him, overchannels, and collapses.

Exact fight choreography, Niall/Davian dialogue, dark-thread mechanics, Heart/armature zän design, escape route, and exact Heart handoff staging remain candidate/deferred for later chapter architecture and magic/endgame reviews.
