# Source Intake Lore and Web-App Ticket Routing

Date: 2026-06-28

Status: accepted; stored across approved owner files and candidate files

## Decision

Approve the following lower-level routing from `imports/raw/Lore (running addended version).txt` and web-app comment batch `2026-06-28-001`.

Naerothi lost-lore boundary:

- Fateweaving taboo, old pictographic or conceptual writing, Alara's legal/voluntary departure, and Naerothi as a source of Convergence/Fateweaving are correct at the high level.
- Naerothi is a lineage among the six major lineages, not only a city or location label.

Cosmology boundary:

- Nizän as a hungry substrate is true at the high level.
- The Creator has not truly abandoned reality.
- Elric's belief that the Creator abandoned reality is canonically important to his character and worldview, but it is a flawed interpretation.

Magic-origin boundary:

- The original sin softened reality, allowing modern Clade magi to wield extremely overpowered and unbalanced magic.
- Older or primeval magic being more balanced is true at the high level.
- Detailed older-source taxonomies about original magic, Shroud architecture, Subversion/Kinesis, and lineages are dated and should not supersede current owner files unless separately reopened.

Candidate-only routing:

- `Abyssal`, `Unwoven`, `Eclipsed`, and `Threadblights` remain candidate-only labels.
- Elric as a combination of reality damage, unwoven/fate-corrupted state, and Ink persistence is directionally accurate, but exact taxonomy and cosmology remain speculative.
- The six-core zän component taxonomy remains candidate technical material.

Accepted zän / notation updates:

- Multi-digit zän numbers should not use a shortened strike-line for an empty place value if that would make values visually ambiguous. Empty places should use a lengthened blank segment or a small intentional notch.
- A six-day week may be called a `hexday`.
- Ancient-style, miniaturized, and Zirene-level zänwork are hard to reproduce because they require greater technical ability, smaller detail, specialized tooling, and expensive or exacting materials.
- Archeon knockoffs remain inferior to Zirene-level work; a possible later Archeon zänwright helping Kelyra make more anti-Clade zän-tech remains future/candidate material.

## Rationale

This preserves useful lower-level lore without treating the outdated lore source as definitive. It also resolves the immediate zän notation and terminology comments before additional chapter architecture or prose-facing zän explanation relies on them.
