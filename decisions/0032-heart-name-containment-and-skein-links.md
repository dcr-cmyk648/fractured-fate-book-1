# Heart Name, Containment, and Skein Links

Date: 2026-06-25

Status: accepted; stored in approved `object-seraph-heart` record

## Decision

The in-world name is just `the Heart`. Some old manuscripts may call it the `Tzorrax Heart`, but those manuscripts may not be referenced directly in Book 1. `Seraph Heart` is not the accepted in-world name.

The Heart appears as a perfectly dark sphere with light-bending edges and non-temperature cold/alien sensory qualities.

Niall removed the ability for anyone to access the Heart, although the Heart generally would have been held in the containment armature even before Niall removed access.

The exact origin or identity of the Heart remains unresolved.

The Heart itself canonically does something to the Skein, but the exact details remain unresolved.

Elric's fate sight is canonically at least somewhat linked to exposure to the Heart.

The Heart will be present when Davian is resurrected and will interplay with Maya's fateweaving, the Skein, and related mechanics. Specific technical details and costs remain unresolved.

The Heart object record owns the Heart and its containment/armature terminology. `object-tzorrax-sphere` is superseded as a separate queue item because `Tzorrax Sphere` is outdated terminology referring to the same object complex. The current Treasury sphere contains the Heart and the armature for containing it.

## Consequences

- `object-seraph-heart` is approved as the durable Heart record.
- `object-tzorrax-sphere` should not receive a separate review unless later manuscript work discovers a distinct object.
- Existing accepted location and magic records should refer to Heart containment and the Treasury sphere rather than maintaining a separate Tzorrax Sphere object boundary.
- Exact Heart/Skein/origin/resurrection mechanics remain deferred to later plot, cosmology, and thread review.
