# Erynn Companion Role and Maya Reality Check

Date: 2026-06-26

Status: accepted

## Decision

Erynn is Maya's personal attendant / ladies' maid-like companion and a Rezin inner-circle household figure, not Rezin blood family.

She was hired at a fairly young age to be a companion and attendant for Maya. This probably happened as soon as Maya was able to form a Shroud, because Maya was less likely to be dangerous to others and less vulnerable to being killed by someone exposed to her.

Erynn's main narrative functions are personal/practical emotional grounding for Maya, class-perspective voice, social reader, and Maya/Davian observer/tease.

Erynn actively helps Maya remain human and connected through rest, food, warmth, ordinary needs, and private teasing. She is a strong reality-check character who voices what Maya is doing in a way that can increase Maya's insight.

Her practicality is a direct counterpoint to Maya's internal voices, which are influenced by Niall's grief and over-compensatory perfectionism. Niall can swing between neglecting Maya and micromanaging her; Erynn repeatedly brings Maya back to ordinary human reality.

Any Erynn betrayal, secret-agent, Ink Boon, journal-planting, or similar secret-content direction is currently obsolete.

## Rationale

This gives Erynn a distinct story function from Aldira. Aldira is the strategic/dry corrective; Erynn is the personal/practical grounding figure who helps Maya stay connected to ordinary human needs and social reality.
