# Sorevin Gateway Zän Prologue Guidance

Date: 2026-06-30

## Status

Accepted future Prologue architecture guidance.

## Decision

Future Prologue architecture should consider giving the Sorevin manor back gateway a protective or alarm zän lattice that Kelyra disables or drains during the raid.

The intended function is to:

- give Kelyra meaningful technical or combat-adjacent work during the raid;
- justify Davian wanting to keep her out of the direct combat;
- show practical zän infrastructure organically in a Clade-associated location;
- make zän technology more visible as everyday architecture and security, not only elite combat or Treasury systems.

Exact map placement, lattice mechanics, wording, and whether the beat survives final pacing remain deferred to Prologue detailed outline or prose-preparation work.

## Boundaries

This does not authorize manuscript edits.

This does not lock the exact Sorevin manor map.

This does not require a full zän lecture in the Prologue. The beat should follow the accepted Prologue functional-worldbuilding principle: show practical worldbuilding through POV-relevant action rather than explanatory aside.
