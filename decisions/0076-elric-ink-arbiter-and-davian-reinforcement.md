# Elric Ink Arbiter and Davian Reinforcement

Date: 2026-06-30

## Status

Accepted.

## Decision

Elric / the Harbinger leader is the decisive arbiter and purveyor of Book 1 Ink Pacts. Major Pacts route through his direct touch or direct attention. Ritual chambers, zä, pooled Ink, tendrils, and other infrastructure can support delivery, but they should not read as independent machines that anyone can operate without Elric's authority.

Davian's initial Heart-theft Pact keeps the Chapter 2 ritual spectacle: Ink runs along the ground, grabs him, and burns into him. Elric / the Harbinger leader then approaches and touches him or otherwise directly focuses the transfer, making the Pact personal and invasive.

The later underground Ink-spectre lair can echo the Harbinger chamber as a prototype or related delivery system.

Elric's Rezin-citadel visit after Davian saves Selwin and is promoted remains important, but it does not grant Davian's deeper healing. That visit:

- shows Elric can enter and leave the citadel despite the Registry;
- shows Elric still wants to stay hidden from Niall;
- reinforces the Ink that lets Davian mimic Arrek's aura.

Davian's deeper forced-restructuring healing comes later, when Elric has almost completely taken over the Harbinger leader's body and touches Davian on the head. That final reinforcement pushes Davian into near-psychotic Ink levels: seeing dark threads, hearing the Ink whisper, and being forced along a path/thread strongly enough that the healing manifests as "he won't let me die" until Davian reaches the intended endpoint.

Karra's Chapter 29 healing advantage is personally granted by the Harbinger leader / Elric. Kelyra does not lose because she gave Ink to Davian, and the transfer/depletion explanation is not accepted. Kelyra loses because she reasonably believes her lethal blow ended the fight, has no reason to expect Karra's healing beyond the fresh but unexplained evidence of Davian's healing, drops her guard, and is defeated by the rigged hidden system.

## Rationale

This preserves the strong ritual imagery while clarifying that Elric is the active manipulator behind Ink distribution. It also removes the confusing Kelyra-to-Davian Ink transfer/depletion explanation and makes Karra's unfair advantage a deliberate Elric/Harbinger rigging choice.
