# Chapter 33 Map, Davian Bands, and Agency Boundary

Date: 2026-06-30

Status: accepted current-draft map guidance

## Decision

The `d1-ch-33` current-draft chapter map is approved.

Maya decides to break Davian out and take him to Zirene against Niall's wishes. This is important setup for the next chapter's conflict with Niall.

Davian's bands are black iron upper-arm bands with circuits etched into his skin beneath/through the metal. They are conceptually adjacent to painful gold strike-line healing and Ink Pact body inscription, and they are sensitive to Niall's electricity or Maya's rapid cooling. Zirene's private consent check in Concord/Masked sign is accepted. Exact low-level heart-kill mechanics remain for later object/technology review.

Writing and sign language deriving from zä forms is accepted at the high level. Specific labels such as `Blade`, `Deep`, and `Crown` remain candidate texture until archetypal zä are reviewed more fully.

Maya's agency challenge to Davian is accepted. She likes Davian, is still angry, and is annoyed that he keeps framing his poor choices as if he had no choice. Later prose can bring more of that conflict into how she says it.

Magus-sense should be simplified. It is enough that magi have amplified senses for energy aligned with their hereditary magic because they need to identify where to get or move energy from. Davian's heartbeat/footstep rumination can remain as character texture or as something natural to him, but should not become a major extra magic-system layer.
