# Karra and Harbinger Guide Lineage Assignments

Date: 2026-06-30

## Status

Accepted.

## Decision

Karra has Ghorreni lineage. Her overall non-human percentage is likely not high because people who live in the Six Cities usually have mostly human ancestry, but the Ghorreni contribution is significant enough to support her huge, muscular build.

The "acceptable" visible amount of demi-human lineage is slightly higher in the Masked Clades and criminal underworld than in mainstream Six Cities society. Masks help obscure features, and criminal/outcast status already places people outside respectable High Clade norms.

The massive Thaleni figure referenced in Chapter 29 notes is the recurring Harbinger guide. He escorts Davian and Jalen to the early Harbinger meeting, then reappears to escort Kelyra, Jalen, and Karra to the Heart handoff. He is clearly of Thaleni heritage: unnaturally large even beside Karra, with boulder-like features.

## Rationale

This gives Karra's physicality a worldbuilding anchor without making high-percentage demi-human ancestry common in the Six Cities. It also uses the Harbinger guide to show that visibly Thaleni people are more plausible in catacomb/underworld spaces than in mainstream High Clade society.
