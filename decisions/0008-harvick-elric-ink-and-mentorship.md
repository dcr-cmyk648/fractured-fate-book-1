# Harvick Elric Ink and Mentorship

Date: 2026-06-24

Status: accepted

## Decision

Harvick has an unofficial but central role in the Rezin sphere. He does not have a clean title and resists attempts to define one, but everyone knows him and treats him as part of the core group around Niall, Aldira, guard commanders, forerunners, and the old war network.

Because Harvick was close to Elric and was with Niall during the war and after, his place in the Rezin sphere is almost uncle-like.

Harvick has a hidden prototypical Ink Pact from Elric's early experimentation with the concept. It gives him bursts of unnatural speed and precision rather than a command voice like Davian's Ink.

Davian stops Harvick's heart with blunt force trauma during the Treasury confrontation. Niall revives Harvick by shocking him with lightning, though characters do not use modern medical terminology for this. Davian may only perceive Niall shooting a lightning bolt through Harvick.

The prior seed about Harvick having killed a man he regrets refers to Elric, not a separate victim. Harvick saw Elric descend into depression and madness, felt helpless to stop it, and worries this pattern may happen to Davian and is already happening to Niall. He voluntarily exposes himself to painful reminders because helping is what he needs to do long-term.

Harvick's special sword and bracers are canonical Zirene-developed returning-weapon technology. This should only be hinted at before the Treasury fight, where Harvick summons his sword as Davian attacks with a lethal weapon. The same technology returns during the final fight with Serathis, who has dual returning knives and bracers also developed by Zirene.

## Rationale

This preserves Harvick's role as a practical, unofficial member of the Rezin core rather than forcing him into a formal hierarchy. It also ties his mentoring of Davian and concern for Niall directly to his unresolved Elric wound, making his anti-trauma-script function durable without flattening him into a generic mentor.
