# Selwin Dissolution Shroud and Family Role

Date: 2026-06-24

Status: accepted

## Decision

Selwin's ability name `Dissolution` is accepted. Characters do not need to call individual magic types by name often in dialogue because there are not many people with the same specific abilities.

Selwin currently cannot form a Shroud at all. It may never be possible for him to form a normal Shroud because of his heritage or materially different abilities, but this does not become very relevant in Book 1.

Niall subconsciously blames Selwin for Alara's postpartum psychosis. Niall does not consciously know he is doing this; he may make excuses to avoid or not train Selwin because Selwin makes him uncomfortable.

Selwin's exact ability mechanics do not need to be fully defined for Book 1. Ultimately, Selwin will be important to the final resolution of the full series because his abilities are what Elric originally hoped to breed as a way to kill the extraplanar deity. Elric's Ink specter beneath the citadel references this direction when he says something about the arrow of time.

Selwin's possible martial-training thread with Davian is approved as likely future-book planning material.

## Rationale

This stores Selwin's current Book 1 function without over-defining long-series magic mechanics. Selwin can matter emotionally and thematically in Book 1 while the full implications of Dissolution, heritage, Shroud incompatibility, and Elric's breeding goal remain available for later review.
