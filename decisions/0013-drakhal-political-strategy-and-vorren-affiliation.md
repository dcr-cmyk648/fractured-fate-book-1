# Decision 0013: Drakhal Political Strategy and Vorren Affiliation

Date: 2026-06-24

## Status

Accepted; stored in `bible/organizations/org-drakhal.md`.

## Decision

Drakhal is accepted as a High Clade associated with force-magic. Exact `Aegistry` terminology remains candidate until magic taxonomy review.

Evana is the current Drakhal Head Magus and Caius's mother. `Ivana` and `Evand` are prior draft options, not current accepted names.

Caius is the main young Drakhal representative and marriage-candidate figure.

Drakhal's current plan is to propose Caius marrying Maya and Niall's banishment while knowing Niall is essentially certain to refuse. The proposal is a trap designed to goad Niall into either a lethal confrontation with Drakhal and allied High Clades where Drakhal expects to win, or into the Archeon citadel where Vorren waits with a lethal trap.

Vorren is Caius's older brother. He was publicly banished from Drakhal at Niall's insistence, which gave Caius the heir position. Vorren secretly still works for Drakhal-aligned interests because Evana and Elric manipulate his desire for chaos and death into situations aligned with their plans.

The Drakhal/Archeon breeding-contract deal where Drakhal agrees to let Archeon breed Kelyra with Vorren is a canon verbal agreement between the parties. Whether Vorren knows about it, would consent to it, or would follow through remains unresolved.

## Rationale

This stores the strategic purpose of Drakhal's post-Heart political moves without prematurely resolving Caius, Evana, Vorren, Harbinger, or final confrontation details.

## Consequences

- `org-drakhal` owns the high-level political plan and Drakhal/Archeon verbal agreement.
- `char-vorren` should later own Vorren's psychology and exact relationship to Drakhal, Evana, Caius, Elric, and the Harbingers.
- `char-caius-drakhal` should later own Caius's role as young representative and marriage-candidate figure.
- Drakhal magic taxonomy remains for later magic review.
