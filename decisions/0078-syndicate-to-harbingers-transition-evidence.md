# Syndicate to Harbingers Transition Evidence

Date: 2026-06-30

## Status

Accepted.

## Decision

Chapter 29's Kelyra POV / underworld lore about the Syndicate becoming the Harbingers is canonical evidence for the accepted organizational transition:

- the Harbingers are the current Ink-powered form of the older Syndicate;
- the name change is a proxy for Elric's takeover of the leader's psyche;
- people who knew the organization long ago may still call it the Syndicate;
- the organization remains one combined record unless future review requires a split.

Exact leader name, pre-takeover history, and fine-grained underworld organizational development remain unresolved.

## Rationale

This ticket does not create a new canon model. It preserves traceability from a web-app comment and Chapter 29 map back to the already accepted Harbingers / Syndicate owner file.
