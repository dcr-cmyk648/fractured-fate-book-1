# Harbinger Betrayal, Trial, and Kelyra Capture

Date: 2026-06-27

Status: accepted; stored in approved `event-harbinger-raid` record

## Decision

The stable entity ID remains `event-harbinger-raid`, but the accurate display name and accepted event scope is `Harbinger betrayal / Trial of Dominion and Kelyra capture`.

Earlier notes used `raid` for the broader sequence leading up to the Vorren fight. This event record owns the narrower source-backed sequence from the Harbinger leader's refusal of the bargain through Karra's Trial/penalty, Karra fleeing, Serathis appearing, and Kelyra being seized by Archeon.

The later Concord manor attack is separate fallout.

Karra's challenge is both:

- legitimate enough under Concord / Trial of Dominion custom to produce post-Trial obedience
- rigged by Harbinger/Elric manipulation

Kelyra's defeat is framed as losing a hidden and rigged system after effectively winning the surface fight. Kelyra does not realize how heavily the odds have been weighted against her; from the hidden side of the event, it is essentially impossible for her to win.

Exact Trial rules, fight choreography, Karra recruitment details, chamber route, Serathis handoff staging, and manor evacuation lie remain candidate or deferred.

The current chapter's Ink-running-out and transfer-to-Davian explanation is candidate material. The chapter may remain structurally strong even if that specific mechanism changes or is removed.

## Rationale

This event should preserve Kelyra's competence while making the betrayal and capture causally clear. She can dominate the visible contest against Karra and still lose because Elric/Harbingers control the deeper conditions.

The legitimate-plus-rigged Trial structure also explains why Karra can later command enough Concord obedience to evacuate the manor without reducing the event to a simple external abduction.
