# Zirene Technical Neutrality and Device Boundaries

Date: 2026-06-26

Status: accepted; stored in approved `char-zirene` record

## Decision

Zirene is a standard-depth dedicated character and technical hinge, not just an unnamed zänwright/device-maker.

She is eccentric, dangerous, funny, independent, and advanced. Her driving motive is discovery and craft rather than Clade loyalty or money itself. Money matters as access to materials, tools, and opportunities for new designs.

In Book 1, Zirene is truly neutral. She will make tools for Serathis that could be used to kill Niall or Maya, just as she makes tools for Niall that he can use to kill other magi. She is interested in creating great designs and seeing them tested under extreme circumstances.

Zirene loves creation and by proxy has at least a slight appreciation for the Creator who made the things she experiments with. She definitely would not know or approve Elric's plan.

## Device Boundaries

Accepted:

- Zirene made or maintains Niall's silver latticework.
- Zirene definitely makes Davian's bands, though exact function and design remain for later review.
- Davian's bands may use an engrave-on-flesh-with-metal technique similar to newest-draft healing abilities that put strike-lines directly onto flesh.
- Zirene directly made the best Archeon gauntlets used by Presented magi.
- Zirene made Harvick's returning sword.
- Zirene made Serathis's dual returning daggers.
- Zirene will make Paradox from ruined Antithesis according to Maya's design.
- Antithesis itself was not made by Zirene. It is a cheap Archeon copy of older Zirene designs.

Deferred:

- Exact low-level mechanics for Niall's silver, Davian's bands, Archeon gauntlets, Harvick's returning sword/bracers, Serathis's returning daggers/bracers, and Paradox.
- Exact workshop security, weapon inventory, and backstory.
- Exact Naeroth/Elarion advanced-zä lineage and old-book provenance.

## Knowledge Boundary

Zirene can infer the power structure behind the events: Niall as the "one man above" and Elric or the Harbinger leader as the "one man below." She does not know that the lower figure is Elric.

She can recognize Ink layered over Archeon patterns and identify Davian as Serathis-descended.

She does not know or approve Elric's full plan.

## Rationale

This preserves Zirene as a vivid technical character who explains devices through craft, motive, and comedy. It also keeps the object/technology mechanics from exploding inside a character review while making the critical ownership boundaries clear for future outline work.
