# Decision 0012: Archeon High Clade Rise and Training System

Date: 2026-06-24

## Status

Accepted; stored in `bible/organizations/org-archeon.md`.

## Decision

Archeon is currently a High Clade because it holds one of the six citadels that form the core of a city-wedge in the Six Cities. It was previously a Low Clade and rose by killing the prior occupying High Clade.

The older `Transcendence` / spirit-summoner label is obsolete.

Archeon publicly brands itself as having especially powerful Shrouds that enhance martial ability and strength beyond the normal Shroud baseline. The true root of its power is a constructed overlap of short-range kinesis based in original pre-refinement/pre-original-sin magic, Ink Pacts for speed, Shroud use, martial arts, and Zirene-derived force-buffering/transfer technology for less accomplished practitioners.

Serathis is Archeon's current Head Magus and leader, and mother/grandmother/etc. of virtually all current members.

Archeon has brutal child-training programs, dark breeding programs, and breeding contracts. Archeon children are taken from their Archeon mothers at birth and trained rather than raised.

Kelyra was truly allowed to escape as part of Archeon's release-and-catch practice. Serathis herself was part of an allowed escape and met Elric that way. Release-and-catch is usually used with members who have not manifested a Shroud and are considered more disposable.

Elric's influence in Archeon is moderate. He set Serathis up, provided Archeon's first Ink Pact before her Shroud was formed, continues to mark Archeon members including Kelyra, and occasionally meets with Serathis, but he does not directly run day-to-day Archeon operations.

## Rationale

This preserves the important plot distinction between Archeon's public status and its hidden construction. Archeon is politically a High Clade, but its power is built from overlapping techniques, magic, Ink, and technology that Serathis does not want publicly understood.

## Consequences

- `org-archeon` owns political status, leadership, breeding/training systems, and high-level power-stack facts.
- Exact kinesis, Ink, Shroud, and Zirene-technology mechanics remain for later magic/object/technology reviews.
- Kelyra and Davian should link to Archeon as the source of their inherited or learned Archeon-derived abilities.
- Drakhal and High Clade politics reviews must account for Archeon's status anxiety, public branding, and hidden technology dependence.
