# Caius Drakhal Heir Role and Narcissistic Provocation

Date: 2026-06-26

## Status

Accepted.

## Decision

Caius Drakhal is Evana Drakhal's son, Vorren's younger brother, Drakhal heir after Vorren's banishment, and a Presented Drakhal force-magus.

Caius is a standard-depth recurring antagonist and Drakhal pressure point, not a Book 1 final-boss-level figure.

Caius is polished, arrogant, dangerous, and insecure. He has a narcissistic edge that leads him to act out in dangerous or deadly ways, including visiting Maya when she does not answer his letter quickly enough and killing civilians while trying to capture the Shade.

The Maya/Caius marriage plus Niall-banishment offer is a bad-faith Drakhal trap from Evana's, Elric's, and Serathis's perspective. Caius likely has not been fully looped into the decision-making and thinks he has a real shot.
