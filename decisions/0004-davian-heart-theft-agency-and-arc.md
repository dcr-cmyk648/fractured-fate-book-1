# Decision 0004: Davian Heart Theft Agency and Arc

Date: 2026-06-23

Status: accepted; stored in approved `char-davian` record

## Decision

Davian chose to steal the Heart both to protect his own family and to satisfy his own desires: to matter, to hurt the High Clades, and to prove agency by striking back.

Davian clearly murdered Arrek.

Davian's broad arc moves from negation toward values. He begins by wanting to tear everything down, hurt the High Clades, prove agency, and stop feeling helpless through destruction. His intended movement is toward valuing Maya and protecting valued things rather than only destroying hated things.

Davian's worry that his attraction to Maya may be unnatural or magically compelled is approved as part of his record. This worry sits on top of his inaccurate bias that High Clades are bred to be attractive.

The Shade persona should mostly live in `object-davian-mask`; Davian's character record should treat Shade as an alias/persona rather than a major independent layer.

## Rationale

The author clarified that Davian's culpability and agency are important. His family protection motive should not erase his own destructive desires, and his arc should be framed as a movement from acting against hated things toward protecting valued things.

## Implications

- Davian should not be written as purely coerced or purely helpless.
- Davian's accepted record can state that he murdered Arrek.
- Later romance work should preserve both attraction and Davian's fear that the attraction is compromised by magic or High Clade breeding myths.
- Shade symbolism should be reviewed mainly through `object-davian-mask`.
