# Decision 0015: Harbingers Name, Elric Takeover, and Coordination

Date: 2026-06-24

Status: accepted; stored in approved `org-harbingers-syndicate` record

## Decision

The Harbingers and the Syndicate should be stored as one organization for now. `Syndicate` is the earlier or former name, while `Harbingers` is the current Ink-powered identity. Some people may still call the organization the Syndicate if they knew it long ago.

The Syndicate-to-Harbingers name change is a proxy measure for Elric's takeover of the young leader's psyche.

The leader was once much more minor than he is now, but already up-and-coming because of tactical genius and an ability to make bargains. Elric selected him because that trajectory made it easy and convenient for Elric to continue the rise while effectively taking over the entire underworld.

The five hooded figures from the early meeting can remain canon. Elric has many allies whose identities he may want to conceal, including Archeon, Vorren, and Drakhal-aligned figures.

The threat in the Heart-theft setup should now be aimed at Kelyra's position as Concord leader. Kelyra received an Ink Pact and killed the prior Concord leader in a Trial of Dominion to take over, so the implication is that someone else could be granted the same power to challenge her.

The Harbingers' public-facing cover story is revolutionary/populist, presenting them as power-to-the-people opposition to the High Clades. People deeply involved with the Harbingers would know this is strained, but it is useful public relations and gives justice-focused members a moral fig leaf.

Elric directly coordinates with Drakhal, Vorren, and Archeon through the Harbingers. That the Harbinger leader is actually Elric in another body is not generally known, though Serathis may possibly suspect it.

The masked figure beside the Harbinger leader during Kelyra's Heart delivery is Vorren.

## Rationale

This preserves the visible underworld organization while making its transformation part of Elric's hidden psychological and organizational takeover. It also aligns the Heart-theft threat with the later Kelyra/Jalen role swap and keeps detailed Ink, Concord, and late-plot mechanics for their owner files.

## Implications

- `org-harbingers-syndicate` owns the organization identity, name transition, public cover story, and high-level Elric coordination.
- `org-concord` should own Trial of Dominion mechanics, Kelyra's leadership position, and the later challenge to that position.
- `magic-ink-boons` should own Ink Pact / Boon mechanics and possession mechanics.
- `char-vorren`, `org-drakhal`, `org-archeon`, and `thread-high-clade-politics` should own detailed coordination and knowledge boundaries.
- Older manuscript references to Jalen's seat are suspect and should be revised later to reflect Kelyra's leadership position.
