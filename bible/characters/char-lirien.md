# Lirien

## Identity and Role

Lirien is Kelyra and Jalen's living child, Davian's younger sister, and Kael's sibling.

The accepted spelling is `Lirien`. `Lirin` is stale manuscript/source spelling and should be corrected during later manuscript or chapter-map cleanup.

Lirien lives in or is based in the Concord manor family space during current manuscript material.

In Book 1, Lirien's primary role is family-stakes and domestic texture beside Kael. She does not currently have a major independent subplot.

## Personality and Function

Lirien is more composed, serious, precise, and controlled than Kael, while still trusting Davian and worrying about him.

Her trust in Davian matters because it presses against the reality of Davian's violence and manipulation. She assumes competence and rightness from Davian in a quieter way than Kael, which helps make Davian's family-protection motive emotionally specific.

Lirien should remain mostly a family-stakes pressure point for Book 1. Any independent future-book role remains candidate material unless later outline work needs it.

## Relationships

Davian:

Lirien trusts Davian, assumes competence from him, and is relieved when he returns. This supports Davian's protective motive while increasing the emotional cost of his self-justifying violence.

Kael:

Kael and Lirien work best as a sibling pair for Book 1. Kael externalizes eager hero-worship; Lirien gives a quieter, more serious version of trust and worry.

Kelyra and Jalen:

Lirien is one of the children Kelyra and Jalen claim to be protecting, and one of the children their secrecy, Concord operations, Harbinger bargains, and Archeon danger put at risk.

Karra:

Accepted Concord-manor/Karra direction implies Lirien is among the family and Concord household members Karra likely moves out after Kelyra is captured. The exact evacuation scene and lie remain deferred.

## Current Manuscript State

Lirien appears at Concord manor when Davian and Jalen return after the Sorevin/Syndicate setup. She approaches at a measured pace, assumes Davian won because he is Davian, and is sent inside by Kelyra with Kael before adult business begins.

During Davian's later return home, Kael and Lirien are in the living quarters near the fireplace. Kael swings a wooden sword while Lirien blocks his strikes with calm precision.

In that later return scene, Lirien follows Kael to Davian more carefully, shows shy relief, admits she was worried, trusts that Davian did the right thing, and is glad he is back.

After Davian leaves again, Kael and Lirien are sent to bed amid tears and questions while Kelyra and Jalen sit with the consequences.
