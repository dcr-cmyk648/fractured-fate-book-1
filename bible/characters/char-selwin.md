# Selwin Rezin

## Identity

Selwin Rezin is Niall Rezin and Alara's young son, Maya Rezin's younger brother, and an unPresented High Clade child.

He is cheerful, open, eager, and deeply admiring of Maya and Niall. He is also anxious about whether he is strong enough and whether Niall still wants to be with him.

## Family Role

Selwin is a living pressure point in the Rezin family grief and perfectionism pattern. He wants closeness and proof of worth, not just magical instruction.

His relationship with Niall is shaped by Niall's grief, withdrawal, mistaken belief that Maya stepping up meant Selwin needed less from him, and subconscious blame related to Alara's postpartum psychosis.

Niall does not consciously know he blames Selwin. The accepted pattern is that Selwin makes Niall uncomfortable, and Niall may make excuses to avoid or not train him.

Maya trains Selwin and tries not to reproduce Niall's harsh childhood Shroud training, even though she fears the pressure may have been necessary.

## Personality and Function

Selwin is open and affectionate in a way that complicates the adults' abstractions around power, family, and enemy categories.

For Davian, Selwin is an innocent High Clade child who reminds him of Kael and exposes Davian's underlying desire to protect rather than only destroy. Davian initially wants to see Selwin as a future High Clade monster, but Selwin's openness and desire to protect people cut through that abstraction.

## Magic

Selwin is at a dangerous manifestation stage and currently cannot form a Shroud at all.

It may never be possible for Selwin to form a normal Shroud because of his heritage or materially different abilities, but this question does not become very relevant in Book 1.

Selwin's magical outburst manifests an unfamiliar sigil and a destructive decay/erosion effect called Dissolution. Exact Dissolution mechanics remain for later magic review.

In dialogue, characters do not need to call magic types by name often. Individual abilities are rare enough that formal labels are not always necessary in ordinary speech.

## Current Manuscript State

Current manuscript material establishes:

- Selwin admires Maya and Niall.
- Selwin worries about whether his magic will be strong enough.
- Selwin cannot currently form a Shroud.
- Selwin's Dissolution outburst threatens the courtyard and nearly kills him.
- Davian saves Selwin during the outburst.
- Niall recognizes that Selwin thought he did something wrong or lost Niall's attention.
- After the Heart theft, Davian apologizes to Selwin, gives his real name, and says he wants to help make things right.
