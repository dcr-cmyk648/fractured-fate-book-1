# Erynn

## Identity and Role

Erynn is Maya's personal attendant / ladies' maid-like companion and a Rezin inner-circle household figure.

She is not Rezin blood family.

Erynn was hired at a fairly young age to be a companion and attendant for Maya. This likely happened as soon as Maya could form a Shroud: Maya was then less likely to be dangerous to others by accident, and anyone exposed to Maya was less likely to be able to kill her.

Her exact formal household title remains unresolved. Current manuscript language can frame her as a maid, attendant, or ladies' maid-like companion until a more precise title is needed.

## Maya

Erynn is one of Maya's closest personal supports.

She helps Maya with Presentation, daily routines, private recovery, social navigation, ordinary needs, and emotional grounding.

Erynn's core function is practical reality-checking. She voices what Maya is doing in a direct, grounded way that can increase Maya's insight.

This practicality is a direct counterpoint to Maya's internal voices, which are influenced by Niall's grief, neglect, micromanagement, and over-compensatory perfectionism.

Erynn helps Maya remain human and connected through:

- rest
- food
- warmth
- ordinary bodily needs
- private teasing
- personal loyalty
- lower-stakes social perception
- reminders that Maya is still a person, not only a Rezin heir, weapon, or political symbol

## Class Perspective

Erynn brings lower-status ordinary-person perspective into Maya's world.

She can name class and social realities Maya does not naturally see, such as the actual conditions beyond the citadel, the limits of what power can fix, and how High Clade logic looks from below.

She is useful because she can ask questions or make observations that High Clade characters might miss precisely because they are trained into the system.

## Personality and Method

Erynn is warm, practical, teasing, observant, and bolder on Maya's behalf than her social station should safely allow.

Her humor should not reduce her to comic relief. Her teasing often protects intimacy: it lets Maya hear truths that might feel too vulnerable or confrontational if stated directly.

Erynn is a social reader. She notices when Maya is pretending not to be upset, when a room is shifting, when Davian's attention matters, and when Maya is rationalizing something that is plainly emotional.

## Niall

Erynn is much lower in the hierarchy than Niall, but she will defend Maya when Maya is being pushed too hard.

Her interruption after the assassination attempt matters because she risks speaking across a massive power gap.

She also serves as a counterweight to the way Niall's grief and inconsistent parenting shape Maya: he can swing between neglect and micromanagement, while Erynn repeatedly brings Maya back to the immediate human reality in front of her.

## Aldira

Erynn and Aldira serve complementary Maya-support functions.

Erynn is personal and practical.

Aldira is strategic and dry.

Both can correct Maya, but Erynn's correction is rooted in intimacy, daily life, class perspective, and ordinary reality-checking.

## Davian

Erynn is useful as an observer of Maya's attraction to Davian, Maya's attempts to rationalize that attraction, and the social awkwardness around Davian entering more intimate Rezin spaces.

She can tease Maya about Davian while still protecting Maya from spiraling into abstraction, status performance, or self-denial.

## Current Manuscript State

Current manuscript material establishes Erynn as:

- attending Maya during Presentation preparation
- teasing and reassuring Maya before the High Clade Council
- grounding Maya after the wall defense / first major battle
- standing at Maya's shoulder during Presentation
- bringing class perspective during the procession through poorer city areas
- physically comforting Maya after the assassination attempt
- speaking up when Niall pushes Maya too hard
- helping Maya with morning routines and private transitions
- receiving Maya's confession about Alara's journal
- noticing Maya's moods, secrets, and social dynamics
- occasionally showing her own ordinary interests, including a possible guard flirtation
