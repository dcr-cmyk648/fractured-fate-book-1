# Aldira

## Identity and Role

Aldira is the accepted current character. All old `Alden` references now refer to Aldira.

She is a Rezin inner-circle household figure, not Rezin blood family.

Her role is intelligence, records, analysis, and spymaster-like operational support for House Rezin. Characters may colloquially call her `spymaster`, but the exact formal office name remains unresolved.

Aldira helps make Rezin power visible as more than Niall's personal force. The household has records, informants, procedures, analysis, institutional memory, and quiet operators.

## Competence

Aldira tracks criminal organizations, informants, Low Clade patterns, contractor records, High Clade politics, lineage material, and old birth-contract records.

She is practically expert in Rezin records and can analyze auratic codices and zán/codex structures well enough to identify anomalies, overlays, and tampering. She does not own all underlying magic theory.

She is skeptical, precise, dry, and records-oriented. Her default mode is to notice what is missing, what is politically dangerous, and what might go wrong.

## Maya

Aldira is one of Maya's key adult corrective figures.

She actively tries to trust and support Maya while Maya is still growing into her role. This matters because Aldira understands the danger around Maya's authority without treating Maya as incapable.

Aldira's support is not primarily sentimental. She uses questions, evidence, dry observation, and careful pressure to help Maya see more clearly.

Her main Maya functions include redirecting Maya around:

- overcontrol
- self-blame
- catastrophizing
- Niall's fear and rigidity
- Davian's betrayal and usefulness
- bright-thread / fate-sight overwhelm
- the difference between responsibility and omniscience

She should not replace Niall's own conversations with Maya, but she can prepare Maya to have them.

## Niall

Aldira is trusted enough to be near Niall's secrets and volatile moments, but she remains careful around his authority.

She can interpret Niall for Maya and challenge Niall's assumptions in limited ways, but she does not own the full truth of Niall, Elric, Alara, and Harvick.

She knows enough to say when Niall is afraid, grieving, rigid, or lashing out. She also knows some truths should come from Niall himself.

## Harvick

Aldira and Harvick have a dry, familiar rhythm.

She understands that Harvick's pain around Elric is real and warns Maya not to push him there. She knows Harvick is still human beneath the calm, discipline, and jokes.

## Davian

Aldira is skeptical of Davian but not uselessly hostile.

She can structure exchanges, ask operational questions, scrutinize Ink and tattoo evidence, and keep Maya and Davian from turning every exchange into an emotional collision.

Her later inference that Kelyra's escape from Archeon was likely deliberate is accepted at the high level: Kelyra learned foundational techniques that would support Archeon-blooded magic later, if the right linchpin instruction appeared.

## Caius Drakhal

Aldira is useful for interpreting the Caius/Shade/Maya incident as political risk rather than only personal misconduct. She can frame Caius's civilian harm, self-defense claim, Drakhal impunity, and courtship pressure in practical terms for Maya and Niall.

## Elric

Aldira knows Elric was charismatic, influential, intellectually important, and historically capable of making the High Clades collaborate.

She knows about his public or semi-public reform interests, including his attempt to oppose birth contracts as a form of exploitation. She does not know the full truth of what happened among Elric, Niall, Alara, and Harvick.

## Narrative Use

Aldira is especially useful for:

- Rezin intelligence and information-network scenes
- records, archives, and evidence analysis
- codex and Registry-adjacent exposition through competence
- High Clade politics explained through practical consequence
- Maya's growth into authority
- dry tonal contrast in tense scenes
- keeping Davian/Maya/Niall/Harvick conversations functional enough to move plot

## Current Manuscript State

Current manuscript material establishes Aldira, sometimes still under the old Alden name, as:

- reporting Drakhal field-agent evidence before Presentation
- managing or interpreting Registry passive-mode and contractor verification
- explaining the Sorevin contractor-record breach and Low Clade head-magus assassination pattern
- identifying auratic codices and warning Maya about vivid memory immersion
- analyzing Alara's corrupted codex key and Elric-linked bridge overlay
- explaining birth-contract records, Elric's reforms, lineage research, and Naeroth record gaps
- tracking Masked Clade-style criminal organizations, including the Concord / `K. Virellis` leadership change
- watching over Maya after the Heart theft and redirecting her self-blame
- helping reconstruct Davian's Ink, Shroud, Archeon, and Masked Clade implications
- structuring the Davian information/training exchange after his capture
