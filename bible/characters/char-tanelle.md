# Tanelle

## Identity and Role

Tanelle is a young zänwright contractor assigned to the Rezin citadel ward and Treasury restoration project.

She is a recurring supporting character, not a generic contractor-group detail. She anchors Davian's worker-space scenes, provides technical exposition through character, and helps make his temporary belonging in the Rezin citadel emotionally credible.

## Personality and Social Function

Tanelle is enthusiastic, talkative, socially forward, openly admiring of High Clade power, and starstruck by Maya Rezin.

She is genuinely kind enough to pull Davian, under the Arrek cover, into the worker group with Kolvar and Jessa. Her warmth is not strategic. She makes Davian feel included almost by accident.

She can be exhausting, awkward, and funny, but she should not be flattened into comic relief. Her friendliness and curiosity help make the contractor group feel socially real.

## Zänwright Competence

Tanelle is unusually skilled at practical zänwright work and pattern recognition.

She re-etches and replaces sequences quickly, confidently, and with strong memory for work orders. Her chatter helps Davian learn enough practical zänwright work to function under his cover.

She loves hands-on etching. She does not want to be supervisor because supervision would keep her from doing the work herself.

Tanelle was likely hired "accidentally." The Rezin contract specifically wanted reliable but not highly talented zänwrights for Treasury work, because Niall did not want workers inspecting the containment sphere and realizing something was unusual. Tanelle may not have had glowing references from prior jobs because supervisors viewed her as annoying, so Sorevin vetters would not have known how talented she was.

## Treasury Insight

Tanelle notices the Treasury sphere's lattice behaves more like containment than outward weightlessness generation.

She sees that the surface lattice is unusually homogeneous, includes sections that may have been replaced or sealed, and appears to hold energy in rather than push it out. She can infer that something inside the sphere or an inner layer may be involved.

Tanelle does not know the Heart or the full Treasury secret. She does not guess the true system. She is only the worker who suspects that something is unusual beyond the baseline oddity of a giant floating sphere.

## Davian / Arrek

Tanelle treats Davian as a coworker and friend under his Arrek identity. She repeatedly disrupts his isolation, forces him to maintain his cover story, and contributes to the ordinary warmth that complicates his hatred of everyone inside Rezin protection.

Her technical competence makes her useful to Davian, while her kindness makes her inconvenient to his ideological certainty.

## Maya

Tanelle is fascinated by Maya as a High Clade magus and Rezin heir. Her blunt curiosity can expose Maya to ordinary worker questions rather than formal court deference.

She can function as a low-stakes social observer for Maya/Davian tension if later scenes need that perspective.
