# Magus Status / Presentation

## Identity

Presentation is the formal public recognition that a High Clade heir or magus is strong, controlled, useful, and politically viable.

It is both ritual and power politics. A High Clade uses Presentation to prove strength, continuity, legitimacy, and future danger to the other High Clades.

Presentation is not merely social introduction. For Maya, it publicly demonstrates that the Rezin line remains strong while also exposing her as a major threat.

## Presentation Gates

Reliable Shroud control is a central safety and status gate for Presentation.

The candidate must also have hereditary magic that is potent and flashy enough that the Presentation ceremony's `show your power` component is not embarrassing for the Clade.

A magus who cannot form and maintain a Shroud is physically vulnerable. If exposed, an unPresented magus who cannot make a Shroud yet can be killed by ordinary means, including a normal assassin with a knife.

## Presented and UnPresented Status

High Clades generally try to keep unPresented magi hidden.

UnPresented status can indicate youth, strategic concealment, hidden instability, danger, weakness, or failure to develop. If an unPresented magus is exposed, they become a target.

If an unPresented magus is not developing toward Presentation, they become an embarrassment: proof that the Clade produced a magus who is not powerful or controlled enough to be publicly recognized.

A High Clade that does not produce enough safely Presented magi shows weakness and may invite attack from another Clade, High or Low, if it controls something worth taking.

More Presented magi is generally better only if they are high-quality enough to hold their own.

## Power Display

A power display is always part of Presentation.

The display exists because Presentation must publicly prove the candidate's value and danger. It is not enough to claim an heir or magus is useful; the ceremony requires the candidate to show power.

Maya's revised fire/ice display is over the top. Most Presented magi do not have her level of power and control, and her display pushes her physically to the limit.

Maya's display cements her as an extreme threat to other Clades on Niall's level. Niall would have told Maya not to be over the top because he did not want her stepping into that level of responsibility and danger.

Exact Maya Presentation choreography, speech, exhaustion, and political fallout belong to `event-maya-presentation`.

## Fashion

Sleeveless High Clade fashion is universal in Presentation-era High Clade culture and symbolizes reliance on one's own power.

When Niall was Presented, he would have worn the sleeveless High Clade style.

Niall's current military jacket is an exception tied to his hidden conduit mechanics and personal/military presentation. Exact Niall jacket comments and conduit mechanics remain deferred.
