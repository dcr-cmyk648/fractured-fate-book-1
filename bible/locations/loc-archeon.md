# Archeon

## Identity

Archeon is the physical territory controlled by the Archeon High Clade: one of the six High Clade citadels and its attached city-wedge / legally distinct city in the Six Cities.

This file owns the Archeon citadel, its surrounding city-wedge/territory, major internal location concepts, and broad raid geography. Archeon as a Clade, Serathis's leadership, breeding program, and power stack belong to `org-archeon`.

## Citadel and City-Wedge

The Archeon citadel is one of the six High Clade citadels. Archeon seized it from a prior occupying High Clade when it rose from Low Clade status to High Clade status. The prior High Clade's exact identity remains deferred.

Like all High Clade citadels, the Archeon citadel has a central tower. The central Spire/amphitheater sits at the center of the Six Cities, surrounded by the six High Clade citadel towers at the points of a hexagon-like geometry.

Access in and out of the Archeon citadel is heavily restricted, as with most High Clade citadels. Archeon keeps much of its internal life hidden, including un-Presented magi, sensitive descendants, training systems, and breeding-control infrastructure.

## Subterranean Complex

Kelyra's Archeon captivity sequence occurs inside the Archeon citadel in lower or subterranean areas rather than a public formal level.

Many citadels have long-forgotten or recently reclaimed subterranean areas, similar to the Rezin citadel's forgotten underground areas and the Harbinger tunnel system beneath the city. These areas are sprawling and poorly characterized.

The Archeon lower complex includes rough stone chambers, corridors, wall rings, chains, guards, healers, messenger routes, controlled corridor access, and routes into training/testing spaces.

## Arena and Training Spaces

Archeon's internal arena is a durable location inside the citadel complex. It is a dim stone training/testing pit with a sand floor, iron rings, iron grates, torches rather than zänlight, and a raised stone lip where Serathis can observe.

The arena is used for testing, punishment, and controlled violence. Serathis can send fighters into the pit, evaluate results from above, and use the space to pressure Kelyra toward Archeon expectations.

The citadel/compound also includes training halls and communal child-training infrastructure. Archeon children are taken at birth, trained institutionally, and returned to the compound around age five.

`Breeding pits` is an accepted concept for the Archeon citadel/compound, with exact layout and final terminology deferred.

## Central Tower and Throne Room

Serathis's final conflict will take place in a throne room in the Archeon central tower.

The throne room has stained glass windows along the sides. These are remnants from the prior High Clade occupants' Creator-worship.

The throne room contains a trap that collapses the floor, dropping Niall into the subterranean areas below the Archeon citadel, where Vorren waits for him.

Accepted high-level endgame direction: Davian fights the remaining Presented Archeon magus to free Kelyra; Kelyra manifests a Shroud to break out and help him; Davian and Kelyra together fight and kill Serathis; Kelyra stays to handle the remaining Archeon forces while Davian goes to help Maya and Niall against Vorren. Vorren is killed by Maya, Niall, and Davian together. Exact route and fight choreography remain deferred.

## Final Attack Geography

The final attack approach will lead through the slums and neglected city-wedge-boundary areas before the main characters enter the Archeon citadel.

The current plan is for all main attackers to approach from aboveground. Once inside, they proceed through the Archeon citadel toward the central tower for the battle in and under the throne room.

Exact route choreography, outer wall/gate configuration, and battle matchups remain deferred.
