# Sorevin Manor

## Identity

The Sorevin manor is the physical Low Clade manor from the prologue where Davian fights the Sorevin Head Magus.

Sorevin is a Low Clade house/family that acts as one of the Rezin proxy administrators. It primarily vets contractors and handles other contracts, along with other local tasks around the Sorevin manor.

This file owns the Sorevin manor and directly location-relevant Sorevin material. Sorevin as a contractor network, Low Clade proxy administrator, or possible later organization/culture example may need later non-location treatment.

## Prologue Manor

The Sorevin manor is the Low Clade manor attacked during the prologue. Davian fights and kills the Sorevin Head Magus there.

The Sorevin Head Magus is the Low Clade magus Davian fights in the prologue.

The manor is near enough to the Rezin citadel for Maya's defensive fire during the Rezin attack to cut through part of the manor structure during Davian's fight.

## Contractor Records

The Sorevin job was the specific Concord operation to retrieve contractor records.

The Sorevin records include the approved contractor list for the Rezin citadel ward-overhaul project and Arrek Hadrin's vetting packet.

The later Arrek / Rezin-citadel contractor infiltration is an add-on from the Harbingers, not the original purpose of the Sorevin job.

Karra was saved by Davian during the Sorevin job.

## Attack Consequences

The Sorevin manor was attacked for contractor records.

The Sorevins were likely not completely wiped out, but the loss of their Head Magus / only significant magic user along with most personnel effectively cripples them for plot purposes.

The attack fits the broader pattern of Low Clade head-magus assassinations and destabilization, but exact culprit and political routing remain for later plot/entity reviews.

## Redbridge

Redbridge is not accepted as part of Sorevin territory. It is candidate-only map/location material.

Arrek happened to drink near Redbridge, and that routine let the Concord locate and follow him into alleys as part of the replacement plan.

Redbridge is probably a district named for a landmark, but exact physical form remains deferred.

Arrek's final fate is accepted in his character record: Davian murders him in an alley after tracking him from a bar near Redbridge, then uses Harbinger Ink to capture/steal/mimic his aura for the Rezin-citadel cover.
