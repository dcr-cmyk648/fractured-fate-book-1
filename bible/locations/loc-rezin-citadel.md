# Rezin Citadel / Rezin Fortress

## Identity

The Rezin citadel is a massive fortress-city complex and the Rezin Clade's power center.

This file is the umbrella location record for the whole citadel. Detailed sublocation records remain separate:

- `loc-registry`: physical Registry chamber
- `loc-treasury`: Treasury / vault chamber
- `loc-spire`: the separate Spire / central amphitheater location, not the Rezin citadel's central tower

## Overall Structure

The citadel includes outer walls, gates, ramparts, towers, bridges, covered walkways, halls, family and command spaces, contractor areas, training courtyards, old statues and hedges, the central tower / architectural spire, the Registry, the Treasury, Heart infrastructure, and ancient hidden tunnels/chambers.

The Rezin citadel has a central tower / spire as an architectural feature. All six High Clade citadels have central towers; seen from the central Spire/amphitheater, those towers stand at the points of a hexagon-like geometry around the Six Cities. The Rezin central tower is distinct from the shared Spire / central amphitheater.

The citadel's present form is layered: ancient base, central spire, and tunnels beneath it, plus later aboveground expansions and rebuilding.

## Fortress-City History

The citadel was originally designed to shelter the older city-wedge population during war. Modern cities have grown far beyond that older scale, so the citadel cannot house the entire present-day city population.

Many courtyards and open spaces that seem excessive in peacetime are inherited wartime staging areas for supply storage, livestock, tents, temporary shelters, military assembly, and emergency use.

## Security and Movement

The citadel is secure but not sealed like a prison.

Contractors and personnel can leave, but routine searches and Registry/security tracking monitor movement. The Registry is central to why the Rezin citadel can allow freer movement than many other High Clade centers.

## Key Placement

The Registry is below ground.

The Treasury is high up.

There are no elevators or similar magical vertical-transport solutions in the accepted direction. Specific maps, routes, stairs, and access sequences remain deferred.

## Ancient Layer

Old tunnels beneath the main spire and Elric's hidden chamber are part of the citadel's ancient layer. Exact map, access points, and chamber relationships remain deferred.
