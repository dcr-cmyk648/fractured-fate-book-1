# Kinesis

## Identity

`Kinesis` is the accepted name for the Archeon hereditary magic. It may also be called `Archeon kinesis` or `Archeon hereditary magic`.

`Subversion` is Kelyra's misnomer for kinesis. It can appear in character-facing material as Kelyra's or Davian's inherited terminology, but it should not be treated as the technical name.

Kinesis is short-range, primarily kinetic redirection of energy. It does not primarily create force from nothing; its goal is diversion, because energy has to go somewhere.

## Technical Model

Kinesis redirects kinetic energy at short range.

Efficient kinesis favors diversion rather than dead-stopping or direct reversal. Dead-stopping or reversing energy produces more backlash because energy still has to go somewhere. A more efficient redirection creates less backlash, wastes less force, and produces more usable effect.

The magic has high cognitive and bodily demands. It becomes safer and more effective when the user has trained physical and motor patterns deeply enough that some parts of the process can operate reflexively.

Inefficient use can cause backlash, injury, exhaustion, or severe physical strain.

## Combination With Other Systems

Kinesis can combine with other systems to become more outward or aggressive:

- Ink Pact movement can supply rapid wrinkle-in-space / Skein-related motion that kinesis can exploit or stabilize.
- Shrouds can protect the user and participate in energy handling, though exact Shroud interaction remains unresolved.
- Martial arts provide body structure, timing, and practiced motor patterns.
- Tools, anchors, gauntlets, knives, walls, and external substrates can give redirected energy somewhere to go.
- Zirene-derived technology can buffer or transfer force for less accomplished users.

## Archeon Stack

Archeon's real combat power is not simply "strong Shrouds." It comes from a stack:

- kinesis
- Ink Pact movement
- Shroud use
- martial arts
- tools and technology

Archeon publicly frames its power as unusually strong Shrouds, but that is propaganda / PR rather than the technical truth.

## Known Users

Kelyra:

Kelyra has strong kinesis and genius martial talent. She calls the magic Subversion, but that is her misnomer. Her version of Archeon martial arts is important because it is less stagnant than the formal Archeon pattern and can support stronger future development.

Davian:

Davian has mild-to-moderate kinesis. His development depends on learning to combine kinesis with martial skill, Shroud use, Ink Pact movement, and practical redirection rather than relying on raw power.

Serathis:

Serathis is a near-perfected Archeon-kinesis user. Her total power comes from mastery of the Shroud / martial arts / Ink Pact / kinesis stack, not from kinesis alone.

Kelyra and Davian:

Kelyra and Davian working together can surpass Serathis. Both have the potential to become better than Serathis because of strong fundamental abilities combined with Kelyra's version of Archeon martial arts.

## Antithesis

Antithesis is an Archeon rip-off of Zirene's designs, specifically her older and more prototypical designs at the time of Book 1. Kelyra stole it.

Exact Antithesis mechanics belong to the later `object-antithesis`, Zirene, Archeon technology, and zan-lattice reviews.

- Exact broader energy-transformation limits remain unresolved beyond the accepted short-range primarily kinetic model.
