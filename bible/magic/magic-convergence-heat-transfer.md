# Convergence Heat Transfer

## Identity

Convergence heat transfer is a rare Naerothi-linked ability associated with Alara and Maya.

Convergence manipulates heat flow rather than creating heat from nothing. Cold effects usually result from heat being pulled out of an area or object, not from a separate cold substance.

Like Clade hereditary magic, Convergence is affected by the reality distortion caused by the original sin. This lets it become much more powerful than it would have been before reality was damaged.

## Maya's Inheritance and Power

Maya receives her Shroud ability from Niall, along with some of the mental abilities that let her engage reality directly and operate at such a high level.

Maya receives her Naerothi Convergence inheritance through Alara.

Maya's unusual power should not be reduced to a single source. Her Book 1 ability profile combines Rezin-derived Shroud and mental capacity, Alara-derived Naerothi Convergence, emerging fate/Skein perception, training, perfectionism, and the broader reality distortion that empowers magic after the original sin.

## Working Model

Maya experiences heat and cold as perceptible overlays and can use Convergence to gather, compress, constrain, redirect, and release heat.

The preferred on-page description should lean toward carving-based language and surfaces:

- schemas ingrained deeply enough to quickly generate useful carvings
- mental circuits required to engage with reality directly
- surfaces or conduits created to hold, move, or transfer energy in useful battle patterns

Thread, ribbon, strand, or channel imagery may remain visually useful, especially when Maya perceives heat flow, but that imagery should not imply that Convergence is identical to Fateweaving.

## Limits

Convergence has practical limits.

Distance, complexity, containment surface, and high-energy concentration increase cognitive and technical load.

Extreme heat concentrations are unstable. They are better treated as brief hold-and-discharge effects than as indefinite storage.

Convergence can make Maya terrifyingly dangerous, especially because reality distortion lets magic exceed pre-original-sin natural limits, but it should not become unlimited heat creation or consequence-free energy manipulation.

## Alara

Alara had Convergence heat transfer at a high level.

Exact Alara usage, limits, terminology, combat style, and relationship to her fateweaving remain deferred unless later chapter, endgame, or Alara-specific review requires more precision.

## Continuity Constraints

- Do not treat Convergence as unlimited heat creation.
- Preserve that cold is absence or removal of heat.
- Preserve distance, complexity, containment, and concentration limits.
- Preserve that Convergence is strengthened by the same damaged-reality conditions that empower Clade hereditary magic.
- Preserve Maya's dual inheritance: Shroud and some mental power from Niall, Convergence from Alara/Naerothi lineage.
- Do not collapse Convergence into Fateweaving without later approval.
- Do not treat Maya as a fully trained Fateweaver because Convergence can use strand/channel imagery.
- Keep exact Fateweaving/Convergence/Selwin/Dissolution relationships deferred.

## Canonical Uncertainties

- Exact Fateweaving / Convergence relationship.
- Exact relationship among Alara's abilities, Maya's abilities, Selwin's Dissolution, and Shroud inheritance.
- Exact pre-original-sin natural version of Convergence and how much weaker it would have been.
- Exact maximum distances, temperatures, and energy limits.
- Exact Alara combat usage and terminology.
